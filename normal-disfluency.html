10. ESSAY Discuss some forces that influence the formality of strategic management systems.


Explanation:

The formality of strategic management systems is influenced by several forces, including the size and complexity of the organization, the level of competition in the industry, the regulatory environment, and the organization's culture and leadership style. Larger and more complex organizations tend to have more formal strategic management systems, with clearly defined processes and structures. In highly competitive industries, formal strategic management systems may be necessary to ensure a company's survival. The regulatory environment may also require formal strategic management systems to ensure compliance with legal and ethical standards. Finally, the organization's culture and leadership style can influence the formality of strategic management systems, with more hierarchical and centralized organizations typically having more formal systems than those with more decentralized and collaborative cultures
