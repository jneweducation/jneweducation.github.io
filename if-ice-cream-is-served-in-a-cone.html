Answer: 120 ways

Given:

n = 6

r = 3

Solution:

[tex]P (n,r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \\ P (6,3) = \frac{6!}{(6-3)!} \\P (6,3) = \frac{6!}{(3)!} \\ P (6,3) = \frac{6x5x4x3x2x1}{3x2x1} \\ P (6,3) = \frac{720}{6} \\\left[\begin{array}{ccc} P (6,3) = 120\end{array}\right][/tex]

18. An ice-cream shop offers 3 types of cones and 5 different flavours of ice-cream. How many possible ice-cream cone combinations are there?


Solution:

Probability:

So we have 3 types of cones and 5 different flavors of ice-cream.

3*5 = 15 possible ice-cream cone combinations.

Cones = {1,2,3}
Flavor of ice-cream = {1, 2, 3, 4, 5}

So,the possible ice-cream cone combinations are {(1,1), (1,2), (1,3), (1,4), (1,5), (2,1), (2, 2), (2, 3), (2,4), (2, 5), (3,1), (3,2), (3,3), (3,4), (3,5)}.

Thus, we have 15 possible ice-cream cone combinations.
