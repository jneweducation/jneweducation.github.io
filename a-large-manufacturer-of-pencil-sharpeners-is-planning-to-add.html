19. Why do we sharpen drawing pencil regularly?\


Answer:

Explanation:

We sharpen drawing pencils regularly to maintain a fine, pointed tip that allows for precise and controlled lines to be drawn. Pencils become dull as their graphite or lead core wears down from use, making lines less precise and lighter. Sharpening the pencil exposes fresh graphite, which creates a sharper point, allowing for more control and precision in the drawing.
