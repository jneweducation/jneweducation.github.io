2. sino nag nagpatupad ng north at south luzon expressway


Ang North at South Luzon expressway ay orihinal na kinontrol ng Originally Philippine National Construction Corporation (PNCC). Ngunit noong taong 2005, inilipat ang pamamahala nito sa NLEX Corporation, isang subsidiary ng Metro Pacific Investments Corporation na dating pagmamay-ari ng Lopez Group of Companies.

Ito ay nagpasimula sa administrasyon ni Pangulo Corazon Aquino sa ilalim ng Department of Public Highways at nagpapatuloy hanggang ngayon dahil sa mga dinagdag na mga expressway ngayon.


3. sino ang nagtatag ng north at south luzon expressway


Ang North Luzon Expressway ay una nang pinamahalaan ng Philippine National Construction Corporation (PNCC) saka ipinasa sa pag-ooperate ng NLEX Corporation, isang subsidiary ng Metro Pacific Investments Corporation.

Ang South Luzon Expressway naman ay ikalawa sa proyektong tinatawag na South Luzon Tollway Corporation, mula sa pinagsamang puwersa ng Philippine National Construction Corporation at sa San Miguel Corporation-backed Citra group of Indonesia sa pamamagitan ng Manila Toll Expressway Systems, Inc. (o MATES).

22. construction of expressway functions​


Answer:

Expressway, also called throughway, thruway, parkway, freeway, superhighway, or motorway, major arterial divided highway that features two or more traffic lanes in each direction, with opposing traffic separated by a median strip; elimination of grade crossings; controlled entries and exits; and advanced designs eliminating steep grades, sharp curves, and other hazards and inconveniences to driving. Frequently expressways have been constructed over completely new routes, passing near but not through large centres of population, on more or less direct lines between desired termini. Their advantages include high speed, greater safety, comfort and convenience for drivers and passengers, and lower vehicle operating costs. Many of these new express highways, especially in the United States, are toll roads, but that is an incidental, not an essential, feature.
