7 blue

4 yellow

9 red

6 green

[tex]=\frac{9}{total.marbles}[/tex]

To find the total marbles, add all the marbles

    9 red

+   6 green

  26 total marbles

[tex]= \frac{9}{26}[/tex]

How to make it percent?

Numerator divided by denominator then multiply it by 100, and adding a percent (%) sign.

Numerator divided by denominator

9 ÷ 26

≈ 0.35

then multiply it by 100

0.35 × 100

=35

then add (%)

= 35%
