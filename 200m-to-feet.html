Answer:

To find the velocity, we need to know the distance traveled and the time it took to travel that distance.

If 200m is the distance traveled in 15.5 seconds, we can use the formula:

velocity = distance / time

velocity = 200m / 15.5s

velocity ≈ 12.903 m/s

Therefore, the velocity is approximately 12.903 m/s.
