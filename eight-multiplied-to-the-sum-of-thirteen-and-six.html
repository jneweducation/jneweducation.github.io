9. Translate the following verbal phrases to expressions or equations.- The sum of a number and eight is thirteen- thrice a number decreased by six- If twelve is added to the difference of a number and eighteen, the sum is seventy-six- The product of twelve less than thrice a number


QUESTION:

Translate the following verbal phrases to expressions or equations.

The sum of a number and eight is thirteen.Thrice a number decreased by six.If twelve is added to the difference of a number and eighteen, the sum is seventy-six.The product of twelve less than thrice a number.

ANSWER:

To translate verbal phrases to expressions or equations, all we need to do is to look for some key words and phrases that are used to describe common mathematical operations. Then, assign variable/s to represent the unknown number. Note that the variable can be any of the letters in the alphabet unless it is already provided the the problem.


The sum of a number and eight is thirteen.

The key words are sum (+) and is (=).Let’s use x as the unknown number.The answer will be x + 8 = 13.The statement is a mathematical equation.

Thrice a number decreased by six.

The key words are thrice (three times, 3×) and decreased (-). Let’s use y as the unknown number.The answer will be 3y - 6.The statement is a mathematical expression.

If twelve is added to the difference of a number and eighteen, the sum is seventy-six.

The key words are added and sum (+), difference (-), is (=).Let’s use z as the unknown number.The answer will be (z - 18) + 12 = 76. Note that you can write the equation even without the parenthesis since it will still result to the same answer.The statement is a mathematical equation.

The product of twelve less than thrice a number.

The key words are thrice (three times, 3×) and product (×).Let’s use a as the unknown number.There is something wrong in the given statement. It gives us the questions “What number will we multiply with twelve to get the product?” or if we get twelve less than thrice a number which is 3a - 12, “how about the term, product?”.
