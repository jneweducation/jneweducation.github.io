4. what benefits can teachers get about the study of investigating students academic performance in online learning of college students through qualitative research approach?


Answer:

Teachers can benefit in several ways from investigating students' academic performance in online learning of college students through a qualitative research approach. Some of these benefits include:

1. Improved teaching strategies: Qualitative research can help teachers understand the factors that contribute to students' success or challenges in online learning. They can use this information to develop more effective teaching strategies that cater to different learning styles and needs, leading to improved academic performance among their students.

2. Enhanced student engagement: By understanding the factors that motivate students to engage in online learning, teachers can adapt their teaching methods to encourage greater participation and engagement from their students. This can lead to increased academic performance as students become more invested in the learning process.

3. Identification of student needs: Qualitative research can help teachers identify the specific needs of their students, such as language barriers or learning disabilities, which may be impacting their academic performance. Teachers can then implement appropriate accommodations or support services to ensure that all students have an equal opportunity to succeed.

4. Development of personalized learning plans: By analyzing student performance data through a qualitative research approach, teachers can develop personalized learning plans that cater to the unique needs of each student. This can lead to improved academic performance as students receive targeted support and instruction.

5. Professional development: Conducting qualitative research can help teachers develop their research skills and deepen their understanding of the factors that impact student academic performance in online learning. This can contribute to their professional development and improve their ability to support their students' academic success.

11. what is the importance of the study in investigating academic performance in online learning of the college students, through qualitative research?​


Answer:

Qualitative research investigating academic performance in online learning of college students is important as it provides valuable insights into student experiences, informs the design of effective online learning practices, enhances student support services, and contributes to the academic literature on the topic.

Explanation:

The investigation of academic performance in online learning of college students through qualitative research is important for several reasons:

1. Understanding student experiences: Qualitative research allows researchers to explore and understand students' experiences with online learning in-depth. By analyzing students' perceptions, attitudes, and behaviors, researchers can identify factors that affect academic performance in online learning, such as motivation, engagement, and self-regulated learning.

2. Improving online learning practices: The findings from qualitative research can inform the design and implementation of online learning practices that support student success. For example, the research may reveal effective instructional strategies, such as providing clear instructions, fostering interaction and collaboration, and using multimedia materials.

3. Enhancing student support: The study can also identify factors that can hinder students' academic performance, such as technological barriers, lack of social support, and academic stress. The results can inform the development of support services and interventions that address these barriers and promote student success in online learning.

4. Contributing to the literature: Qualitative research can contribute to the academic literature on online learning by providing rich, detailed descriptions of student experiences and perspectives. This can help to build theory and inform future research on the topic.

Overall, investigating academic performance in online learning of college students through qualitative research can provide valuable insights that can help to enhance the effectiveness of online learning and support student success.
