1.A cutting tool is a pointed tool mounted in a machine tool and used for cutting materials. Lathes and milling machines use different types of cutting tools. Diamonds are sometimes used in cutting tools because its hardness allows it to cut other hard materials.

2.Chef knife-What is a chef knife used for? The curved blade of a chef knife allows it rock back and forwards on a chopping board, which makes it the perfect tool for chopping and dicing lots of vegetables at once.

Paring knife-What is a paring knife used for? The small but mighty paring knife is used to cut, chop and slice fruits and vegetables, but they can also be used for a multitude of other kitchen tasks.

Bread knife-The primary job of a bread knife is to slice bread. A good bread knife should saw through a tough crust without mangling or compressing the tender crumb within.

Carving knife-Carving knives are used for slicing thin cuts of large cooked meats like poultry, ham, and roasts. They are distinctive with their thinner form, and their size allows them to carve more precise and thinner slices. Carving knives are typically large, with sizes that are typically between 20cm and 38cm

Cleaver or butcher knife-A cleaver can take on heavy vegetables like different varieties of squash and root vegetables with more force than a chef's knife or santoku knife. Aside from breaking down tendons and bones, cleavers can also be used for pounding, mincing, dicing, and slicing of a variety of other foods.

Boning knife-Boning knives have long, thin, flexible blades with a sharp tip to make piercing meat easier and safer. The blade is designed to cut through ligaments and connective tissue to remove raw meat from the bone. Boning knives have to be extremely sharp, and the tougher the blade, the longer it will maintain its sharpness.

Filleting knife-Fillet knives are specifically designed for cutting fish and removing bones. A wide array of fish knives with various cutting edges exists - but the most common ones are fillet knives, large serrated knives and those designed for cutting tuna

Salmon knife-What is a salmon knife used for? A salmon knife is used to slice, fillet and remove the skin from larger fish, like salmon. They're slender enough to fit between the skin and flesh without damaging the delicate fish, allowing the chef to create clean, tidy fillets.

29. Examples of cutting tools and their function


Alligator Shear:

Also known as a crocodile or lever shear with a hinged jaw and powered by a flywheel or hydraulic cylinder.

They are sued for cutting long metal stock or scrap, generally where accuracy is not so important.

It is often used with large metal shredders to clean or prepare the scrap for shredding.


Abrasive Saw:

Also known as cut-off saw or metal-chop saw.

It is specifically used to cut hard materials such as metals.

Portable abrasive saws have made site jobs quite easier.


Bandsaw:

It uses blades consisting of continuous band of metal with teeth along one edge to cut various work-pieces.

Bandsaw helps in uniform cutting as it has evenly distributed teeth load.

They are used in cutting meat, metals and even hard timbers.


Diamond Saw:

A variety of diamond saw blades like circular diamond blades, drills and surface grinding wheels are used in construction work.

They are used in cutting stone, cobalt, asphalt, bricks, glass in the construction industry.

Diamond saw tools are sued in cutting semiconductor materials in IT industry and cutting gemstones like diamonds in the gem industry.


Hacksaw:

A fine-toothed saw for cutting metal.

Also used in cutting plastic and wood. For eg. Plumbers and electricians often use them to cut pipes and plastics.


High Speed Steel:

It is a subset of tool steels, and is commonly used in tool bits and cutting tools.

It is often used in power saw blades and drill bits.

They allow cutting faster than high carbon steel.

The main use of HSS continues to be in the manufacture of various cutting tools: drills, taps, milling cutters, tool bits, gear cutters, saw blades, router bits etc.


Jigsaw:

A tool used for cutting arbitrary curves such as stenciled designs or other custom shapes into a piece of wood, metal or other material.

Modern jigsaws are power tools made up of electric motor and a reciprocating saw blade.


Milling Cutters:

They are cutting tools used in milling machines or machining centers to perform milling operations.

They remove material by their movement within the machine or directly from the cutter’s shape


Bimetal Bandsaw:

They are metal cutting blades used on the bandsaw to cut carbon steels, tool steel, structural steel, stainless steel, pipes and tubes.

They can even cut ferrous and non-ferrous metals such as brass, aluminium bronze or steel.


Tungsten Carbide Tipped (TCT):

TCT saw blades are used for industrial use in cutting metals, aluminium, wood or plastic.

The cutting edge consist of a separate piece of material either brazed, welded or clamped on to a separate body.
