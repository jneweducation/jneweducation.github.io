11. If fib(22)=17,711 and Fib(24)=46,368 what is fib(23)?​


Answer:

28657

Step-by-step explanation:

The notation Fib(n) has something to do with the Fibonacci Sequence. You may be new to this concept so I will define it first.

The Fibonacci Sequence is a specific number sequence that has its first two terms as one. The next terms can be derived by adding the previous two terms of the sequence.

The n in the equation is the position of the term. In this case, we are given the 22nd and 24th term of the Fibonacci Sequence and we need to find the 23rd.

Since you can get the 24th term by adding the 22nd and 23rd terms, we need to find the difference in this case.

Fib(24) - Fib(22) = 46368 - 17711

Fib(23) = 28657

Therefore, the 23rd term of the Fibonacci Sequence is 28657.
