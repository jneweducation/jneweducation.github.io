26. the circulatory sytem


WHAT IS CIRCULATORY SYSTEM?

The circulatory system is made up of blood vessels that carry blood away from and towards the heart. Arteries carry blood away from the heart and veins carry blood back to the heart. The circulatory system carries oxygen, nutrients, and hormones to cells, and removes waste products, like carbon dioxide.

WHAT ARE 4 MAIN PARTS OF CIRCULATORY SYSTEM?

The circulatory system is composed of the hearts, arteries, capillaries, and veins. This remarkable system transports oxygenated blood from the lungs and heart throughout the body via the arteries.
