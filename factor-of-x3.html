5. What is that factor of x3-27


Answer:

[tex](x-3)(x^2+3x+9)[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Note that [tex]x^3-27[/tex] is a difference between the two cubes. Recall that:

[tex]a^3 - b^3 = (a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)[/tex]

2. Since the cube root of [tex]x^3[/tex] is x and the cube root of 27 is 3; therefore, a = x and b = 3:

[tex]a^3 - b^3 = (a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)\\x^3-27=x^3-3^3=(x-3)(x^2+3x+3^2)\\(x-3)(x^2+3x+3^2)=(x-3)(x^2+3x+9)[/tex]

3. The factor is:

[tex](x-3)(x^2+3x+9)[/tex]
