The first is red the second is blueB. Both balls are yellow.Compare the process of getting the probabilities in each of the situation above?​


A. Both are blue:

The probability of drawing a blue ball on the first draw is 12/35. Since the ball is put back, the probability of drawing a blue ball on the second draw is also 12/35. Therefore, the probability of drawing two blue balls in a row is (12/35) x (12/35) = 144/1225 or about 0.1178.

B. The first is red, the second is yellow:

The probability of drawing a red ball on the first draw is 14/35. The probability of drawing a yellow ball on the second draw is 9/35. Since the first ball is put back, the probability of drawing a red ball on the first draw is still 14/35. Therefore, the probability of drawing a red ball followed by a yellow ball is (14/35) x (9/35) = 126/1225 or about 0.1029.

A. The first is red, the second is blue:

The probability of drawing a red ball on the first draw is 14/35. Since the first ball is not replaced, there are now only 34 balls left in the box, including 11 blue balls. Therefore, the probability of drawing a blue ball on the second draw after drawing a red ball on the first draw is 11/34. Therefore, the probability of drawing a red ball followed by a blue ball is (14/35) x (11/34) = 77/595 or about 0.1294.

B. Both balls are yellow:

The probability of drawing a yellow ball on the first draw is 9/35. Since the first ball is not replaced, there are now only 34 balls left in the box, including 8 yellow balls. Therefore, the probability of drawing a yellow ball on the second draw after drawing a yellow ball on the first draw is 8/34. Therefore, the probability of drawing two yellow balls in a row is (9/35) x (8/34) = 72/1225 or about 0.0588.

In the first set of probabilities, the balls are replaced after each draw, which means the probability of drawing a particular color ball remains the same for each draw. In the second set of probabilities, the first ball is not replaced, which means the probability of drawing a particular color ball changes for the second draw depending on what was drawn first.
