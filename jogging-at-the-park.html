9. miss anne jogs around a square park that is 120 meters on a side. one morning she jogged around the park 9 times. how many kilometer dud she jogs?​


Answer:

4.32 kilometers

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked? The total distance (in kilometer) Miss Anne jogs. What are given? 120 meters - one side of the square park. 9 times - number of times she jogged around the parkHow to solve the problem?

Step 1. Find the perimeter.

Since the problem asks for the distance around the park, we are supposed to find the perimeter.

Formula to be used:

Perimeter= side x 4

We will use the formula in finding the perimeter of the square (it is mentioned that the park is square)

Solution:

P = S x 4

P = 120m x 4

P = 480 meters

Step 2. Find the total distance she jogged.

We simply multiply the distance around the park (perimeter) by the times she jogged around.

Formula to be used:

Perimeter x times she jogs around

Solution:

P x T = 480m x 9 =4320 meters

Step 3. Convert into kilometers

The problem asks for distance in kilometers so we will convert this.

Solution:

1 kilometer is equal to 1,000 meters.

4,320/1000m =4.32 km

Conclusion:

Therefore, she jogged 4.32 kilometers.
