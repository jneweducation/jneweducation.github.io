3. 12. 180 is 50% of what number?B, 380A. 360D. 460C. 420​


Answer:

B

Step-by-step explanation:

To understand this, you need to see the equation then find the missing value.

50% x ? = 180

convert the percent to decimal

50% = 0.50 = 0.5

then solve for ?

0.5 x ? = 180

0.5 x ? / 0.5 = 180 / 0.5

? = 360

*the reason it gets bigger is because dividing by 0.5 is the same as [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex]. when you divide by a fraction, it flips and multiplies, so 180 x 2/1
