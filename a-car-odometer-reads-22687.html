8. The odometer of my car indicates 789 546. All the digits are different. After how many more kilometers will this happen again?


Okay, so we start with 789,546.

We know that from 789,547-789,549, we have repeating numbers since 7,8, and 9 are in the thousands places.

Now from 789,550 - 789,559, they all have 2 fives.

Then we arrive at 789,560, which has all the digits different.

We now just subtract 789,560 - 789,546 = 14 km.

Usually with problems like this, we tend to overthink them. So here's a friendly reminder to sometimes just start laying out what the most obvious thing is, and see if you can find a solution from there.

Hope this helps.
