5. BACKGROUND/REVIEW OF LITERATURE Abarro (2016) stated that an individual's desired job choice should be selected ahead of time. The PNHS Research Innovation Project (2020) aims to enhance learners basic skills, produce more competent citizens, an prepare graduates for learning and employment. Work Immersion programs can concretize the ideas, lessons, and opinions learned in school. Authentic assessments are the most effective way to measure skills acquired by learners through products and performances, while traditional assessment should be put aside as it does not measure the skill required for each lesson.explanation​


Answer:

The cited sources highlight the importance of preparing individuals for the workforce and the role of educational programs in achieving this goal. Abarro (2016) emphasizes the need for individuals to select their desired job choice ahead of time, which could help them focus their efforts towards developing the necessary skills and competencies required for their chosen profession.

The PNHS Research Innovation Project (2020) aims to enhance learners' basic skills, produce more competent citizens, and prepare graduates for learning and employment. One of the ways they intend to achieve this is through work immersion programs. This type of program can help learners concretize the ideas, lessons, and opinions they have learned in school by providing hands-on experience in a real work environment.

Furthermore, the sources suggest that authentic assessments are more effective than traditional assessments in measuring the skills acquired by learners. Authentic assessments involve evaluating learners through their products and performances, which provide a more accurate representation of their abilities than traditional assessments that rely on tests and exams. By prioritizing authentic assessments, educators can better assess the skills and competencies of their learners and ensure that they are well-prepared for the workforce
