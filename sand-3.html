Groups of kids who want to make sandcastle.they can only use 16 3/4kg of sand for all groups.

1. Group 1 got 2 1/4 kg how much sand left?

= 14 1/2

2. Group 2 got 5 2/3 how much sand is left and how much does both group 1 and 2 have?

14 1/2 - 5 2/3

Change mixed numbers into improper fractions

29/2 - 17/3

87/6 - 34/6

Subtract the numerators, copy the denominator

53/6

Change improper fraction into a mixed fraction

= 8 5/6

8 5/6 kg of sand is left.

2 1/4 + 5 2/3

2 3/12 + 5 8/12

= 7 11/12

11 67/60
