The price of 32 LED television is P15.500 less than twice the price of the old model. it cost P29.08.00 to buy a new 32 LED television, what is the price of the old model2. The perimeter of the rectangle is 96 when the length of a rectangle is twice the width. What areme dimensions of the rectangle​


Answer:

1. The price of old model is 13,578

2.The Length is 64

   The Width is 32

Explanation:

Let      P= Price

       2X=Old Price

      NP=New Price

     

NP – 2X = P

29,078.00 – 2X = 15,500

               -2X=15,500 – 29,078.00

              -2X= -13,578

                -2X/-2 = -13,578/-2

                  X = 6,789

29,078.00 – 2X = 15,500

29,078.00 – 2(6,789) = 15,500

29,078.00 – 13,578 = 15,500

                   15,500 = 15,500

The price of old model is 13,578

_______________________________________________

Remember the formula for perimeter 2L+2W=P

Let X = Width

    2X = Length

2L+2W=P

2(2X) + 2(X) = 96

4X + 2X = 96

       6X = 96

      6X/6=96/6

       X = 16

2(2X) + 2(X) = 96

2(2*16) + 2(16) = 96

2(32) + 32 = 96

64 + 32 = 96

96= 96

The Length is 64

The Width is 32
