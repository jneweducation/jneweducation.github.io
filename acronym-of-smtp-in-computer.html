20. Write the meaning of the following computer related acronyms. 1 CMOS​


Write the meaning of the CMOS, a computer-related acronym.

Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) is an acronym for complementary metal-oxide semiconductors of the computer. It's a real component of the computer's motherboard. The Basic Input/Output System (BIOS) settings are stored on a particular sort of memory chip that is inserted on the computer motherboard.

It also allows you to adjust the time and date, as well as command the computer to do a variety of basic tasks, such as the Boot Device Priority function. A CMOS battery is used to power it.

A CR2032 cell battery is typically used to power CMOS. A CMOS battery is a lithium-ion battery that is the size of a coin.

Rather than RAM or other memory, the BIOS setup of the computer is kept on the CMOS chip. It features a 256-byte storage capacity.
