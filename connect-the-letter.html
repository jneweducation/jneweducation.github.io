16. Authorization letter to apply for internet connection


Answer:

Authorization Letter

An Authorization letter is a type of written letter that allows one person to claim, pay, request, apply for etc on behalf of one person who is not available to do such actions in the event that he/she is needed.

Some authorization letters may require to be notarized, some do not. It depends upon what type of transaction that person is dealing with.

Now going back, the below is my sample authorization letter:

__

(Write Date Here)

(Write Cable Provider Company here)

(Write address of Cable provider company)

To whom it may concern,

Good Day.

I would like to authorize (write name of authorized person) to apply for an internet on my behalf. I hereby attached the copy of my valid Ids Namely PRC ID with signature as proof of authorization.

I would not be able to be present at the time of application due to some errands that I need to deal.

If there arises any problem or if you need to validate this authorization letter, you may directly reach me at my email address, (write your e-mail address here), or my mobile number (write you mobile number here).

I hope you understand.

Respectfully yours,

(Write your name here)
