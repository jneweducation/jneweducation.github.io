5. Calculate the wavelength of red light with a frequency of 460 x 10¹2Hz. ​


Answer:

The formula for calculating wavelength is:

wavelength = speed of light / frequency

The speed of light is approximately 3 x 10^8 m/s.

Plugging in the values:

wavelength = 3 x 10^8 m/s / 460 x 10^12 Hz

wavelength = 6.52 x 10^-7 m or 652 nm

Therefore, the wavelength of red light with a frequency of 460 x 10¹2Hz is approximately 652 nanometers.
