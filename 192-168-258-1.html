9. The product of two positive consecutive even integer is 168. Assuming the smaller integer to be x, frame an equation for the statement. A. x (x + 2) = 168 B. x2 = 168 C. 2x = 168 D. x (2x) = 168​


Answer:

A. x(x+2) = 168

Step-by-step explanation:

The product of two positive consecutive even integer is 168.

Smaller even integer: xSecond even integer, add 2: (x + 2)Product: 168Operation: Multiplication

Equation:

(x)(x+2) = 168

To solve for x, the first even integer:

x² + 2x - 168 = 0

Factor:

(x + 14)(x - 12) = 168

Equate each factor to 0:

x + 14 = 0

x = -14

x - 12 = 0

x = 12

Smaller even integer x: 12

Second even integer: 12 + 2 = 14

The consecutive even  integers are 12 and 14.

Check:

(12)(14) = 168

168 = 168  (True)

➤We should use the formula to get the volume of the rectangular prism.

Formula = (V = lwh)THE SOLUTION:

➤Using the formula we have to solve to get the volume:

V = (L) (W) (H)V = (4cm)(6cm)(8cm)V = (24cm²)(8cm)V = 192 cm³

➤THE VOLUME = 192 centimeters²

[tex]\boxed{ANSWER:}[/tex]A. 192 cm³

➤Letter A is the correct answer in this problem and among the other choices.

Answer:

Volume of a Rectangular Prism

[tex]______________________________________[/tex]

[tex]\orange{{\tt{Problem:}}}[/tex]

1. Matthew made a rectangular prism, whose length os 4 cm, height is 8 cm and width is 6 cm. Find the volume of a rectangular prism.

A. 192 cm³

B. 240 cm³

C. 182 cm³

D. 258 cm³

[tex]\orange{{\tt{Formula:}}}[/tex]

The formula in finding the volume of a rectangular prism is;

• Volume = Length × Width × Height

• V = LWH

[tex]\orange{{\tt{Given:}}}[/tex]

Length – 4 cm

Width – 6 cm

Height – 8 cm

[tex]\orange{{\tt{Solution:}}}[/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: {{\tt{• \: V = LWH:}}}[/tex]

[tex]\: \: \: \: {{\tt{• \: V = (4 cm)(6 cm)(8 cm)}}}[/tex]

[tex]\: \: \: \: {{\tt{• \: V = (24 cm²)(8 cm)}}}[/tex]

[tex]\: \: \: \: {{\tt{• \: V = 192 cm³}}}[/tex]

[tex]\color{orange}{\boxed{\tt{ Volume = 192 cm³}}}[/tex]

Therefore, the correct answer in this problem is letter A. 192 cm³.
