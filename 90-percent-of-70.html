20. Solve P÷B×100% show with solution1.)what percent of 70 is 24?2.)what percent of 70 is 19?3.) what percent of 180 is 60?4.)what percent of 270 is 108?5.)what percent of 90 is 63?pls help po ​


Answer:

The formula for percentage is:

percentage = (part / whole) x 100%

We can use this formula to solve the problems:

1. To find what percent of 70 is 24:

percentage = (24 / 70) x 100%

= 34.29%

Therefore, 24 is approximately 34.29% of 70.

2. To find what percent of 70 is 19:

percentage = (19 / 70) x 100%

= 27.14%

Therefore, 19 is approximately 27.14% of 70.

3. To find what percent of 180 is 60:

percentage = (60 / 180) x 100%

= 33.33%

Therefore, 60 is approximately 33.33% of 180.

4. To find what percent of 270 is 108:

percentage = (108 / 270) x 100%

= 40%

Therefore, 108 is 40% of 270.

5. To find what percent of 90 is 63:

percentage = (63 / 90) x 100%

= 70%

Therefore, 63 is 70% of 90.
