4. what will be the 17th term for the sequence 5,11, 17, 23, 29... A. 101 B. 107 C. 113 D. 119 pasagot ng maayos


Answer:

A. 101

Step-by-step explanation:

This sequence is an arithmetic sequence since there is a common difference between each term. In this case, adding  6  to the previous term in the sequence gives the next term. In other words, [tex]a_{n} = a_{1} + d (n - 1)[/tex], where [tex]a_{1} = 5[/tex] and [tex]d = 6[/tex].

Let us first solve for the rule of the sequence.

This is the formula of an arithmetic sequence.

[tex]a_{n} = a_{1} + d (n - 1)[/tex]

Substitute in the values of [tex]a_{1} = 5[/tex] and [tex]d = 6[/tex].

[tex]a_{n} = 5 + (6) (n - 1)[/tex]

Simplify each term.

Apply the distributive property.

[tex]a_{n} = 5 + 6n + 6 (- 1)[/tex]

Multiply  6  by - 1 .

[tex]a_{n} = 5 + 6n - 6[/tex]

Subtract 6 from 5.

[tex]a_{n} = 6n - 1[/tex]

The rule of the sequence is [tex]a_{n} = 6n - 1[/tex].

Let us now solve for the 17th term of the sequence.

Write the rule.

[tex]a_{n} = 6n - 1[/tex]

Substitute n by 17.

[tex]a_{17} = 6(17) - 1[/tex]

Multiply 17 by 6.

[tex]a_{17} = 102 - 1[/tex]

Subtract 102 by 1.

[tex]a_{17} = 101[/tex]

101 (Letter A) is the 17th term of the sequence.

If you want to know the values of the other terms, just substitute n in the rule by the number of their place in the sequence.

18. what is 62% of 113?​


Answer:

54.87%

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution for 62 is what percent of 113:

62:113*100 =  

(62*100):113 =  

6200:113 = 54.87

Now we have: 62 is what percent of 113 = 54.87  

Question: 62 is what percent of 113?  

Percentage solution with steps:

Step 1: We make the assumption that 113 is 100% since it is our output value.  

Step 2: We next represent the value we seek with x.

Step 3: From step 1, it follows that 100%=113.

Step 4: In the same vein, x% = 62.

Step 5: This gives us a pair of simple equations:

100% = 113(1)

x% = 62(2)

Step 6: By simply dividing equation 1 by equation 2 and taking note of the fact that both the LHS

(left - hand side) of both equations have the same unit (%); we have

100%/ x% = 113/62

Step 7: Taking the inverse (or reciprocal) of both sides yields

x%/100% = 62/113

-> x = 54.87%

Therefore, 62 is 54.87% of 113
