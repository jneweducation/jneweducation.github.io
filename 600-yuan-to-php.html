23. what percent of 75,000 is php 600?​


Answer:

0.8

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution for 600 is what percent of 75000:

600:75000*100 =

(600*100):75000 =

60000:75000 = 0.8

Now we have: 600 is what percent of 75000 = 0.8

Question: 600 is what percent of 75000?

Percentage solution with steps:

Step 1: We make the assumption that 75000 is 100% since it is our output value.

Step 2: We next represent the value we seek with $x$.

Step 3: From step 1, it follows that $100\%=75000$.

Step 4: In the same vein, $x\%=600$.

Step 5: This gives us a pair of simple equations:

$100\%=75000(1)$.

$x\%=600(2)$.

Step 6: By simply dividing equation 1 by equation 2 and taking note of the fact that both the LHS

(left hand side) of both equations have the same unit (%); we have

$\frac{100\%}{x\%}=\frac{75000}{600}$

Step 7: Taking the inverse (or reciprocal) of both sides yields

$\frac{x\%}{100\%}=\frac{600}{75000}$

$\Rightarrow x=0.8\%$

Therefore, $600$ is $0.8\%$ of $75000$.
