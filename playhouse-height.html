The playhouse theater is basically a theater in the city of Westminster, London, England. It was built y F.H. Howler and Hill. It can house up to 1,200 people and was rebuilt in 1907. It opened on March 11 1882. It hosted comic operas, barlesques, and farces. 1997 was  the return of its use.

A theater, theatre or playhouse, is a structure where theatrical works or plays are performed, or other performances such as musical concerts may be produced. While a theater is not required for performance, a theater serves to define the performance and audience spaces.
