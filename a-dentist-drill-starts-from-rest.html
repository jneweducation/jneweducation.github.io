11. A committee of four people is to be formed from six doctors and eight dentists. find the probability that the committee will consist of three doctors and one dentist. (round your answer in four decimal places.)


Probability is very crucial in the field of Mathematics and in our everyday lives. It is the study of chance or the likelihood of an event to occur. It is also one of the common grounds essential in studying Statistics. Practically, we use probability when choosing the combination of today's outfit, stating a new venture (business) or accepting a job offer.

The general formula in solving a probability problem is:
[tex]P = \frac{possible no. of outcomes}{total no. of outcomes} [/tex]

You need to create a committee consisting of four (4) people from six (6) doctors and eight (8) dentists, wherein three (3) members are doctors and the other one is dentist.

To compute for the possible number of outcomes you have to realize that the candidate that's already chosen is no longer included in the next selection. That goes for the total number of outcomes as well. Since we need to form a four-member committee with three doctors and one dentist, the possible number of outcomes should go like this:

Possible no. of outcomes = (total no. of doctors) * (total no. of doctors - 1) * (total no. of doctors - 2) * (total number of dentists)
Possible no. of outcomes = (6) * (5) * (4) * (8)
Possible no. of outcomes = 960

On the other hand, the total number of outcomes should be:

Total no. of outcomes = (total no. of nominees) * (total no. of nominees - 1 * (total no. of nominees - 2) * (total no. of nominees - 3)
Total no. of outcomes = (14) * (13) * (12) * (11)
Total no. of outcomes = 24,024

Using the general formula, we should arrive at:
[tex]P = \frac{possible no. of outcomes}{total no. of outcomes} [/tex]
[tex]P = \frac{960}{24024}[/tex]
[tex]P = 0.04[/tex]

The probability of electing a committee of three doctors and one dentist from a group of six doctors and eight dentists is approximately 0.04 or 4%! That would be the answer to your question!

Having a deep understanding in probability and mathematics gives us an edge into understanding our world better. Keep practicing and appreciate the essence of Mathematics in our lives. Good luck!

18. Hypothetically, if you will go to the core, where should you start drilling and why?


You will start drilling in the crust, its either in the continental crust or oceanic crust but I preferred oceanic crust since it is much more closer to the core compared with continental crust. The crust is the first layer of the earth which serves as the skin and it is made up of rocks. After drilling in the crust you encounter the layer of mantle the asthenosphere and mesosphere. After the mantle you will enter the liquid outer core made up mostly of liquid iron and nickel and then you will reach the inner core which is a very dense solid layer.

Here are the four layers of the Earth

CrustMantleOuter coreInner core

Crust

The crust is the thinnest and the outermost layer of the Earth that extends from the surface to about 32 kilometers below. This are layer where we live and volcanoes, earthquake, and mountain ranges are formed.  

Type of crust

Oceanic crustContinental crust

Oceanic crust  

Are denser and thinner compared to continental crust.

Continental crust

Thicker and are less dense compared to oceanic crust.

Mantle

Beneath the crust is the mantle, which extends to about 2,900 kilometers from the Earth's surface. The mantle is the thickest layer of the earth and is more dense compared to the crust. It is way hotter than the crust and capable of melting it. The upper mantle are plastic, its made up of magma that because of too much heat it flows which causes changes on the surface of the Earth.

Outer Core

The outer core is 2900 kilometers below the surface of the Earth. It is mostly made up of iron and nickel but in liquid phase and its temperature reaches to about 2000°C. Even if the outer core has a high pressure, but due to its much higher temperature, nickel and iron are melted. The outer core is flowing outside the inner core which generate a magnetic field that protects the earth from the dangerous radioactive material coming from the heat of the sun.

Inner Core

The inner core is the deepest layer made up of solid iron and nickel and has a radius of approximately 1,300 kilometers. Its temperature reaches to about 5000°C. The inner have the highest density compared to the other. The heat in the interior of the Earth is caused by the radioactive decay of uranium in the inner core which releases tremendous amount of heat.
