13. (-4,6);slope undefined ​


When a slope is undefined, it means that the line is vertical.

The point (-4,6) has an x-coordinate of -4 and a y-coordinate of 6. Therefore, if the line passing through this point has an undefined slope, it must be a vertical line passing through the point (-4,6).

A vertical line has an undefined slope because the formula for slope involves dividing by the difference in x-coordinates, which is zero for a vertical line. Therefore, the slope is undefined for any vertical line.
