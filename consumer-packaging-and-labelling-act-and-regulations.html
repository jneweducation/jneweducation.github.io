5. 1. Are the product packages and label important and useful to convince consumers to buy health products? Why? 2. Why should you read and understand the information in the label of products before eating drinking, or using it?


Answer:

1. Yes, product packages and labels are important and useful to convince consumers to buy health products. The packaging and labeling of a product can provide important information about the product's ingredients, nutritional value, and potential health benefits. Consumers are more likely to purchase products that are packaged attractively, with clear and informative labeling that highlights the product's health benefits.

2. It is important to read and understand the information in the label of products before eating, drinking, or using it because this information can provide important details about the product's ingredients, nutritional value, and potential health risks. For example, a label may indicate if the product contains allergens or if it is high in sugar or sodium. Reading the label can also help consumers make informed decisions about their health and dietary needs. By understanding the information in the label, consumers can avoid products that may be harmful to their health and choose products that align with their dietary preferences and restrictions.

26. what is packaging and labelling?​

Packaging is the science, art and technology of enclosing or protecting products for distribution, storage, sale, and use. Packaging also refers to the process of designing, evaluating, and producing packages.

Labels are any piece of material attached to a product to identify it, or to a container to identify its contents. Information that is directly written on a product can also be considered a label
