1. Thirty liters of a weak solution is combined with 50 liters of a strong solution of ethyl alcohol to form a mixture that is 13% alcohol. If the strong solution is twice as concentrated as the weak one, find the percent of the strong solution.


Answer:

16%

Explanation:

let x = %weak solution

y = %strong solution

30x+50y = (80)(0.13)  ----> eq. 1

y = 2x ----> eq.2

Subs. eq.2 to eq. 1:

30(y/2) + 50y = (80)(0.13)

y = 0.16 or 16%
