10. 1. What is the role of food packaging in the food industry?2. Why is it important to select proper packaging for each type of food item?3. What is the difference between Compostable and Biodegradable food packaging?4. Name some food packaging materials commonly use in the food industry.​


Answer:

1.Food packaging is packaging for food. A package provides protection, tampering resistance, and special physical, chemical, or biological needs. It may bear a nutrition facts label and other information about food being offered for sale.

2.If food packaging integrity is not well maintained and tested, food safety can compromise a lot. Packaging protects the food from external elements, contamination, heat, physical damage, and much more. Thus, proper packaging keeps your food products as safe as possible.

3.The primary difference between compostable and biodegradable is that compostable products require a specific setting in order to break down, whereas biodegradable products break down naturally. Typically composting is a faster process, but only under the right conditions.
