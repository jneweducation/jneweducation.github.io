16. pa help please. A. Define the following.1. Polygon2. Convex Polygon3. Non Convex Polygon4. Regular Polygon​


Answer:

1. Polygon- a plane figure with atleast three straight side and angles, and typically five or more

2. Convex Polygon- The convex polygon definition explains that one is a polygon whose angles are all less than 180 degrees each. That also means that the vertices, the point where two sides meet, all point outward from the center of the shape. The easiest example of a convex shape is a basic equilateral triangle. Its three vertices all point away from its center and the angles are all less than 180 degrees.

3. Non Convex Polygon- A simple polygon that is not convex is called concave,or non-convex, or reentrant. A concave polygon will always have at least one reflex interior angle—that is, an angle with a measure that is between 180 degrees and 360 degrees exclusive

4. Regular Polygon- A regular polygon is an sided polygon in which the sides are all the same length and are symmetrical placed about a common center

Explanation: A polygon is a closed plane figure made up of straight line segments that are connected end-to-end to form a closed shape. The sides of a polygon do not intersect and the angles between adjacent sides are fixed. Therefore, a polygon is made up of straight lines.
