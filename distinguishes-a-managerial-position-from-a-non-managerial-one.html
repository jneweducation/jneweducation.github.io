One of the earliest and most enduring descriptions of managerial roles comes from?

Henry Mintzberg,who (as we have already noted) shadowed managers observing what they did during the day. Mintzberg developed a list of roles that he grouped into three categories: interpersonal roles, informational roles, and decisional roles.
