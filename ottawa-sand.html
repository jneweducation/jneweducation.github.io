4. 1. A woman is 3 times as old as his daughter. After 9 years, the mother will be 2 times as old as the daughter. How old is mother now? 2. A car left Ottawa at 1 p.m. going at 35 mph. A second car left the city at 4 p.m. going in the same direction at 50 mph. At what time will the second car catch up with the first?


Answer:

1. x = 9

2. t = 10 hrs

Step-by-step explanation:

Question 1 solution:

Woman:

Present age: 3x ; Future age: 3x + 9


Daughter:

Present age: x ; Future age: x + 9


Equating the age of the woman to her daughter the equation is:

3x + 9 = 2 (x+9)

3x + 9 = 2x + 18

3x - 2x = 18 - 9

x = 9


Question 2 solution:

Using the uniform motion formula d = rt

d = r_{1} t_{1}

d = (35)(t) - eq. 1


d = r_{2} t_{2}

d = (50)(t - 3) - eq. 2


Equating eq. 1 and eq. 2:

35t = 50 (t - 3)

35t = 50t - 150

150 = 50t - 35t

150 = 15t

t = 10 hrs

Ottawa as National Capital of Canada

Ottawa was chosen to be the national capital of Canada though it was not the largest and the major city of the country. It was based on the historical background of Canada. In 1857, Queen Victoria chose Ottawa as its national capital despite the close competition between the different cities. Ottawa was centrally located between the two major cities, which were Montreal and Toronto (formerly called as York).
