10. Cassy was building a rectangular pen for his pigs. he has 62 feet of fencing. the length of the pen is 9 feet longer then the width. use any strategy to find the dimensions of the big pen?


Answer:

Assume that all 62 feet of fencing will be used.

Let l represent the length and w the width.

Since the length is 9 feet longer than the width, the length can be represented as:

l=w+9

Take note that the Perimeter of the Pen is required and the pen is a rectangle:

P=2l+2w

Substitute l=w+9 and P=62:

62=2(w+9)+2w

62=2w+18+2w

62=4w+18

62-18=4w

44=4w

44/4=4w/4

11=w

Therefore, the width is 11 feet.

The length is then given by l=w+9=11+9=20 feet.

The dimensions of the pen is 20 by 11 feet.
