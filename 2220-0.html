[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The force acting on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F = ma}[/tex]

where:

F = force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the net force

F = ma

F = (2,220 kg)(13.5 m/s²)

[tex]\boxed{F = \text{29,700 N}}[/tex]

Therefore, the net force is 29,700 N.

let n = no.of yrs after 2004  

//Possible values 1-16 (where 1 is 2005 and 16 is 2020//

Given: 0.15n^2 + 3.35n +2220

let n=3;

=0.15(3)^2 +3.35(3) + 2220

= 1.35 +  10.05 +2220

= 11.40+ 2220

= 2231.40

// 3 is 2007//

Therefore, 2007 is the exact yr where the averaged weekly income of an employee equals to Php.2,231.40
