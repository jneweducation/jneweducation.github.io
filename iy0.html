Answer:

44 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

a father is twice as old iya 20 years ago, the age of the father was 12 times the age of the son. the present age of the father is.

let iya be x years

father = 2x

..

20 years ago

iya = x-20

father = 2x-20

..

2x-20=12*(x-20)

2x-20 = 12x -240

add 20 to both sides

2x = 12x-240+20

2x=12x-220

add -12x to both sides

2x-12x= 12x-12x-220

-12x+2x= -240+20

-10x= -220

divide by -10

x= -220/-10

x=22 iya's age

Father's age = 2*22 = 44 years
