13. 47°12'20" convert to decimal degrees


This is an example of converting or changing degrees, minutes, seconds, into decimal degrees.

Take note that ° is the symbol of degrees, ' is the symbol of minutes, and
'' is the symbol of seconds.

Take note also that;
1° = 60'
1' = 60''

We will now start converting the given to decimal degrees.
Given: 47°12'20''
        = 47°12'20''(1'/60)
        = 47°12'+0.33'
        = 47°12.33'(1°/60')
        = 47°+0.21°
        = 47.21°

Therefore, 47°12'20'' is equal to 47.21°

16. what is the LCM of 12,20 and 24?​


Answer:

= 120

Step-by-step explanation:

1. find the prime factors of 12.

12 = 6 × 2

ㅤ6 = 3 × 2

therefore the prime factors of 12 are 2 and 3.

2. find the prime factors of 20.

20 = 10 × 2

ㅤ10 = 2 × 5

therefore the prime factors of 20 are 2 and 5.

3. find the prime factors of 24.

24 = 12 × 2

ㅤ12 = 6 × 2

ㅤ6 = 3 × 2

therefore the prime factors of 24 are 2 and 3.

the prime factors 3 and 5 occur one time, while 2 occurs more than once.

the least common multiple is the product of all factors in the greatest number of their occurrence.

LCM = 2³ × 3 × 5

the least common multiple of 12, 20 and 24 is 120.
