8. What is the amount n a bank account after 9years if 26000 as invested originally at 7.55 annual interest compounded quarterly​


Answer:

50,960

Step-by-step explanation:

PV = 26000

i = 7.55% ÷ 4 = 1.8875% or 0.018875

*NOTE: We need to divide the interest rate by 4 because it was compounded quarterly.

n = 9 x 4 periods per year = 36periods

FV = PV(1 + i)^n

= 26000(1 + 0.018875)³⁶

= 26000(1.018875)³⁶

= 26000(1.96)

= 50,960
