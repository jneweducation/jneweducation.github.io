13. a stone is dropped into a deep well and is heard to hit the water 2.37 s after being dropped. how deep is the well?​


Answer:

We can use the equation of motion for an object under free fall to solve this problem. The equation is:

h = (1/2) * g * t^2

Where:

h = height or depth of the well

g = acceleration due to gravity, which is approximately 9.8 m/s^2 near the Earth's surface

t = time it takes for the stone to hit the water

Substituting the given values:

h = (1/2) * 9.8 m/s^2 * (2.37 s)^2

h = 27.92 meters (rounded to two decimal places)

Therefore, the depth of the well is approximately 27.92 meters.
