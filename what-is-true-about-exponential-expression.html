Changing Exponential to Radical Expression and Vice - Versa

To change an exponential to radical expression, identify the denominator. Place the denominator as the index for the radical expression. The numerator will be the power by which the radicand will be raised. To change radical expression to exponential, place the index as the denominator of the fractional exponent. Make sure to take note of the negative exponents.

15. What is Exponential Expression​


Answer:

Exponential expressions are just a way to write powers in short form. The exponent indicates the number of times the base is used as a factor. So in the case of 32 it can be written as 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2=25, where 2 is the “base” and 5 is the “exponent”. We read this expression as “two to the fifth power”.
