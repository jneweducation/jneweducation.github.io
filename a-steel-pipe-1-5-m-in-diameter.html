2. Water flows in a pipe of 23-cm diameter with an average velocity of 0.73 m/s, the diameter of the pipe is reduced, and the average velocity of the water increases to 1.66 m/s. What is the diameter of the smaller pipe? What is the flow rate? ​


Answer:

d₂ = 15 cm and Q = 30,000 cm³/s

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

d₁ = 23 cm = 0.23 m

v₁ = 0.73 m/s

v₂ = 1.66 m/s

Solution:

Using the equation of continuity:

A₁v₁ = A₂v₂

(π/4)(0.23)²(0.73) = (π/4)(d₂²)(1.66)

d₂ = 0.15 m

d₂ = 15 cm <== answer

Q = A₁v₁ = (π/4)(0.23)²(0.73)

Q = 0.03 m³/s

Q = 30,000 cm³/s <== answer

12. A cylindrical pipe with water flowing downward at 0.02 m3/s having top diameter of 0.08 m, bottom diameter of 0.04 m, and height of 1.5 m. find the pressure between the pipe


Answer:

The pressure between the pipe is AP = 15.27 Psi

Explanation:

Q = 0.02 m³/s

d₁ = 0.08 m

d₂ = 0.04 m

h₁ = 1.5 m

h₂ = 0

A₁ = [tex]\frac{πd₁²}{4}[/tex]

   = [tex]\frac{π}{4}[/tex] (0.08)² = 0.005026 m²

P₂ = [tex]\frac{πd₂²}{4}[/tex]

    = [tex]\frac{π}{4}[/tex] (0.04)² = 0.001256 m²

Flow Rate. Q = A₁V₁ = A₂V₂

                  V₁ = [tex]\frac{Q}{A₁}[/tex] , V₂ = [tex]\frac{Q}{A₂}[/tex]

                  V₁ = [tex]\frac{0.02}{0.005026}[/tex] , V₂ = [tex]\frac{0.02}{0.001256}[/tex]

                  V₁ = 4 m/s  , V₂ = 16 m/s

between (1) and (2)

P₁ + [tex]\frac{1000 - (4)²}{2}[/tex] + 1.000 ˣ 9.81 ˣ 1.5 = P₂ + [tex]\frac{1000 ˣ (16)²}{2}[/tex] + 1.000 ˣ 9.81 ˣ (0)

P₁ + 8.000 + 14715 = P₂ + 128.000

P₁ - P₂ = 105285 Pa  ( 1 Pa = 0.000145038 Psi

ΔP = 105285 ˣ (0.000145038) Psi

ΔP= 15.27 Psi

29. a pipe has an outer diameter of 4 1/2 CM. If the pipe is 2/3 CM thick, what is the inner diameter of the pipe?


Hello! For this problem, the pipe is considered as two concentric circles. I illustrated how it would look like in the attached picture. To get the diameter of the smaller circle (inner diameter d), we simply need to subtract twice the length of the distance of the two circles, or twice it's thickness, from the outer diameter.


d = 4 1/2 - 2(2/3)

d = 9/2 - 4/3

d = 19/6 cm or 3 1/6 cm
