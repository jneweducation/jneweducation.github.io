Technical definition:Reflection is the change in direction of a wavefront at an interface between two different media so that the wavefront returns into the medium from which it originated. Common examples include the reflection of light, sound and water waves.

Operational definition:Reflection is the process of engaging the self in attentive, critical, exploratory and iterative inter- actions with one's thoughts and actions, and their underlying conceptual frame, with a view to changing them and with a view on the change itself.

Interest

Technical definition:The technical interest is the interest rate with which the present value is calculated for an insurance's future obligations, such as the disbursement of the sum payable at death.

Operational definition:Operational Interest means the right to lease, manage or control the operations of a Person, directly or indirectly.

Timeline

Technical definition:A timeline is the presentation of a chronological sequence of events along a drawn line that enables a viewer to understand temporal relationships quickly. The term is also sometimes extended to mean a chronology that is tabular, year-by-year paragraphs or purely conceptual.

Operational definition:A timeline is the presentation of a chronological sequence of events along a drawn line that enables a viewer to understand temporal relationships quickly.Timelines are useful to document any type of development, providing an easy-to-understand history and helping viewers to understand past and ongoing trends.

Solution

Technical definition:A solution is an implementation of people, processes, information and technologies in a distinct system to support a set of business or technical capabilities that solve one or more business problems.

Operational definition:Of or relating to an operation or operations.

In working order and ready for use.

Operational definition:Either end of a carrier line having facilities for the handling of freight and passengers.A freight or passenger station that is central to a considerable area or serves as a junction at any point with other lines.A town or city at the end of a carrier line.
