27. mr. sanchez has 30 students ih hiss class.If 60% are girls how many girls are there ih hiss class​


QUESTION:

Mr. Sanchez has 30 students in his class. If 60% are girls, how many girls are there in his class​?

ANSWER:

use the concept of Percentage, Base, Rate

P = B × R

where

P = Percentage of Total (Base) = ?

B = Base or the Total = 30

R = Rate = 60% = 60/100 = 0.60

solve for P,

P = B × R

P = (30) × (0.60)

P = 18

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{\sf There are 18 girls in his class.}}[/tex]
