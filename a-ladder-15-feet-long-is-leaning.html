18. A ladder leaning against the wall of a house makes an angle of 55° with the ground. The foot of the ladder is 8 feet from the foundation of the house. How long is the ladder?​


Answer:

Let's call the length of the ladder "x". We can set up the following equation:

tan(55°) = x/8

To solve for x, we can multiply both sides by 8:

8 tan(55°) = x

Using a calculator, we can find that tan(55°) ≈ 1.43. So:

8 * 1.43 ≈ 11.44

Therefore, the length of the ladder is approximately 11.44 feet.
