1. The distance from Baguio to Metro Manila is 250 kilometers. How long will a bus travelling at 40km/hr to reach Baguio to Metro Manila?​


Distance, Speed, and Time

Formulas:

Distance = Speed × Time

Speed = Distance / Time

Time = Distance / Speed

________________________________

For the problem, we will use Time = Distance / Speed. Substitute the given into the formula.

Time = Distance / SpeedTime = 250 / 40Time = 6.25

Answer

Therefore, it will take 6.25 hours for the bus to reach Metro Manila from Baguio.

24. Joel drove his car at the rate of 47.25 kilometers per hour from Manila to Baguio. On the trip back to manila, he drove his car at the rate of 62.5 kilometers per hour. About how much faster did he drive his car on the trip back to manila?


Joel drove his car at 2.0

The distance between Manila to Baguio is constant nomatter where your starting point is if your route is just the same. And since distance is equal to speedxtime,

distance = speed1*time1 = speed2*time2

Let 1 be the trip going to Baguio, and 2 be the trip going back to Manila. The question is asking how much faster is his trip back to Manila. Therefore, we must find the formula for time2 as a function of time1.

(47.25)(time 1) = (62.5)(time 2)

time 2 = (47.25/62.5)*time 1

time 2 = 0.756*time 1

It means that his trip back to Manila is 75.6% faster than his time for the trip going to Baguio.
