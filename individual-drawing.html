EARTH PLANET

My Planet is called Earth. It is located on the third planet in the solar system. The weather is warm. It would have various kinds of organisms. The individual who live there are called biotic organisms.

The explanation of the answer above is:

The Solar System is an interactive system of celestial bodies consisting of the sun as its center and also the celestial bodies that surround it such as the planets. Earth is a planet with a rocky terrestrial flat surface and the only planet that has life on it. Earth is the third in the solar system after Mercury and Venus.

Earth occupies the position of the 7th coldest planet and the third hottest planet. The heat energy emitted by the sun to the Earth will pass through the atmosphere before reaching the Earth's surface. The energy will expand or move up and be replaced by cold air that is not too hot and cold. Thus, Earth is a planet that has a warm temperature or weather.

The atmosphere that covers the Earth will trigger the formation of weather and climate on the Earth's surface through the redistribution of heat energy which is known as circulation in the atmosphere.

Circulation patterns in the atmosphere will produce water vapor due to evaporation. When the atmosphere lifts warm and moist air, water vapor will experience condensation and will be lowered to the Earth's surface in the form of rain.

The water from the atmospheric circulation will flow from the highest place to the lowest place on the Earth's surface so that various living things inhabit the earth. Not only is the availability of water on Earth that causes the Earth to have life, but there is also nitrogen and oxygen (gases that living things need to breathe) in Earth's atmosphere.

Life on Earth is filled with biotic and abiotic components. Individuals who inhabit the earth are referred to as biotic organisms consisting of animals, plants, humans, microorganisms, and others. These individuals have a close relationship with abiotic components for their survival.

They need oxygen from the air to breathe, water to help them get food, and the right air temperature for their bodies. All of these things are provided by Planet Earth, so Earth is said to be the only planet in the solar system that has life.
