5. fabm2 accounting looking for total assets:cash 240account payable 640tax payable 120dividends payable 60non current liabilities 95owners capital 640​


To calculate the total assets, you need to add up the values of all the assets. The given information includes cash, account payable, tax payable, dividends payable, non-current liabilities, and owner's capital.

Adding up the values:

Cash: $240

Account Payable: $640

Tax Payable: $120

Dividends Payable: $60

Non-Current Liabilities: $95

Owner's Capital: $640

To calculate the total assets, you add all these values together:

Total Assets = Cash + Account Payable + Tax Payable + Dividends Payable + Non-Current Liabilities + Owner's Capital

Total Assets = $240 + $640 + $120 + $60 + $95 + $640 = $1,795

Therefore, the total assets amount to $1,795.
