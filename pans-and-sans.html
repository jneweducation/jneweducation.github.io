4. A rectangular shape shallow metal pan used to bake bread, cookies and pastries. a. b. Muffin pan Tube pan c. Roll Pan d. Cookie pan​


A rectangular shape shallow metal pan is used to bake bread, cookies, and pastries.

The Jelly Roll Pan is a shallow rectangular baking pan for jelly rolls.

A jelly roll pan is a flat sheet pan with a 1-inch rim all the way around the sides. It's made for making a thin sponge or sheet cakes that can be filled with crème, jelly, or other fillings and rolled into cylinders. Jelly roll pans are available in a number of materials, although the most popular are aluminum or aluminized steel.
