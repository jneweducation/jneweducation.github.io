11. Learning Task 4: Study the graphic. In your notebook, discuss in one paragraph the content of the enrolment update presented below.​


Answer:

The statistics show the enrollment data as of July 2020 of SDO IMUS City. There is a comparison between the number of enrollees from the Public and Private schools, and as well as the grade levels. The Elementary level has 49, 465 enrollees for the public school and 4,503 for the private school. The Junior High School level has 29, 169 enrollees for the private school and 4, 296 enrollees for the public school. Lastly, the Senior High School level has 3, 834 enrollees for the private school and 3, 061 for the public school. In totality, public schools have 81, 695 enrollees and private schools have 12, 633 enrollees.

Step-By-Step Explanation:

Writing a descriptive paragraph is very important to explain the situation provided by a given data. In this example, we are asked to discuss in one paragraph the content of the enrolment based on the data presented.    

Some Tips in Writing a Paragraph

1. Start with the main topic for your paragraph.

2. Provide descriptions or support to your topic sentence. This can be a description based on the data provided.

3.  Use transition words to show the connection of each sentence.

4. A single consists mostly of three or more sentences.

4. Make a conclusion about the data.

In this situation, we have provided a descriptive paragraph. Remember that there are different types of paragraphs.

Different Types of Paragraph

1. Narration

2. Description

3. Exposition

4. Persuasion
