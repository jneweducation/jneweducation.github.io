At carnival Eme bought 15 tickets. If he used 6 tickets trying to win the color game, what is the ratio of tickets he has to tickets he's used?

[tex]\huge\color{red} {\boxed{\tt{Answer}}}[/tex]

[tex]\large{\boxed{\tt{{3:2}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\huge\color{red} {\boxed{\tt{Solution:}}}[/tex]

• Total number of tickets [tex]\large{\bold{\sf{{= 15}}}}[/tex]

• Number of tickets Used [tex]\large{\bold{\sf{{= 6}}}}[/tex]

• Remaining tickets [tex]\large{\bold{\sf{{= 15 - 6 = 9}}}}[/tex]

Ratio of tickets he has to, tickets he's used:

[tex]\large{\bold{\sf{{= \frac{9}{6} = \frac{3}{2} }}}}[/tex]
