Answer:

B. r¹² • r³ = r¹⁵

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the variables are the same, simply copy it and add the exponents.

A. r³ • r⁸ = r⁵ (should be r¹¹) ❌

B. r¹² • r³ = r¹⁵ ✔️

C. r⁵ • r⁷ = r² (should be r¹²) ❌

D. r³ • r¹⁴ = r¹¹ (should be r¹⁷) ❌

A = π(3cm)^2 = 9π cm^2

A = π(2cm)^2 = 4π cm^2

A = π(5cm)^2 = 25π cm^2

25. What binomials give the product of r^2 – r – 56 ? A. (r + 7) (r – 8) B. (r – 7) (r + 8) C. (r – 7) (r – 8) D. (r + 7) (r + 8) ​


Answer:

A. (r+7) (r−8)

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's factor r^2−r−56

r^62−r−56

The middle number is -1 and the last number is -56.

Factoring means we want something like

(r+_)(r+_)

Which numbers go in the blanks?

We need two numbers that...

Add together to get -1

Multiply together to get -56

Can you think of the two numbers?

Try 7 and -8:

7+-8 = -1

7*-8 = -56

Fill in the blanks in

(r+_)(r+_)

with 7 and -8 to get...

(r+7)(r-8)

Answer:

(r+7) (r−8)
