Active voice

➤ In a sentence written in the active voice, the subject of sentence performs the action.

examples:

The naughty child broke the vase. The dog ate the old bone. The worker cleans the roof. Passive voice

➤ In a sentence written in the passive voice the subject receives the action.

examples:

The vase was broken by the naughty childThe old bone was eaten by the dog. The roof is cleaned by the worker.
