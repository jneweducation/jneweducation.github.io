First, let's find the prime factors of 255 and 650:

255 = 3 x 3 x 5 x 3

650 = 2 x 5 x 5 x 13

The common factors of 255 and 650 are 5 and 1. The greatest common factor is 5.

So, we can simplify 255/650 by dividing both the numerator and denominator by 5:

255/650 = (255 ÷ 5) / (650 ÷ 5) = 51/130

Therefore, 255/650 simplified to 51/130.
