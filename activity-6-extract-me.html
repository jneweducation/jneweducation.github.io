10. Activity 8: Extract Then Describe Me!Solve each of the following quadratic equations by extracting square roots. Answer the questions that follow.1. 3t² = 122. x²-7 =03. 3r²=184. x² =1505. x² = 9/166. (s - 4) - 81 = 0​


Answer:

Extracting the Square Roots

Ito ay isang method ng pag-solve sa Quadratic Equation na kung saan ay gumagamit tayo ng SQUARE ROOT PROPERTY.

Na kung saan, sinasabi dito na:.

[tex]\green{\boxed{If x^2 = k, and \sqrt{x^2} = \sqrt{k}, then x = ±k}}[/tex]

Solution Time!1. [tex]\green{\boxed{3t^2 = 12}}[/tex]

Divide both sides by 3.

[tex]\green{\boxed{\frac{3t^2}{3} = \frac{12}{3}}}[/tex][tex]\green{\boxed{t^2 = 4}}[/tex]

Extract.

[tex]\green{\boxed{\sqrt{t^2} = \sqrt{4}}}[/tex]

Simplify. The answer is:

[tex]\purple{\boxed{t = ±2}}[/tex]

or

[tex]\green{\boxed{t = 2}}[/tex], [tex]\green{\boxed{t = -2}}[/tex]

2. [tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 - 7 = 0}}[/tex]

Transpose -7 to the right side.

[tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 = 7}}[/tex]

Extract.

[tex]\red{\boxed{\sqrt{x^2} = \sqrt{7}}}[/tex]

The answer is:

[tex]\purple{\boxed{x = ±\sqrt{7}}}[/tex]

or

[tex]\purple{\boxed{x = \sqrt{7}}}[/tex] [tex]\purple{\boxed{x = -\sqrt{7}}}[/tex]

7 does not have square roots.

3. [tex]\red{\boxed{3r^2 = 18}}[/tex]

Divide both sides by 3.

[tex]\red{\boxed{\frac{3r^2}{3}= \frac{18}{3}}}[/tex][tex]\red{\boxed{r^2 = 6}}[/tex]

Extract.

[tex]\red{\boxed{\sqrt{r^2} = \sqrt{6}}}[/tex][tex]\blue{\boxed{r = ±\sqrt{6}}}[/tex]

6 is not a perfect square.

Answer:

[tex]\red{\boxed{r = \sqrt{6}}}[/tex][tex]\red{\boxed{r = -\sqrt{6}}}[/tex]

4. [tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 = 150}}[/tex]

There's no need to do anything. Proceed by extracting it.

[tex]\red{\boxed{\sqrt{x^2} = \sqrt{150}}}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\boxed{\sqrt{x^2} = \sqrt{25 • 6}}}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\boxed{x = ± 5 \sqrt{6}}}[/tex]

The answer is:

[tex]\red{\boxed{x = ± 5 \sqrt{6}}}[/tex]

or

[tex]\blue{\boxed{x = 5 \sqrt{6}}}[/tex], [tex]\blue{\boxed{x = - 5 \sqrt{6}}}[/tex]

5. [tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 = \frac{9}{16}}}[/tex]

Extract.

[tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 = \sqrt{\frac{9}{16}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\boxed{x^2 = {\frac{\sqrt{9}}{\sqrt{16}}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\boxed{x = ± \frac{3}{4}}}[/tex]

Answer:

[tex]\red{\boxed{x = \frac{3}{4}}}[/tex] [tex]\blue{\boxed{x = - \frac{3}{4}}}[/tex]

6. [tex]\green{\boxed{(s - 4)^2 - 81 = 0}}[/tex]

Transpose -81 to the right side.

[tex]\green{\boxed{(s - 4)^2 = 81}}[/tex]

Extract.

[tex]\green{\boxed{\sqrt{(s - 4)^2 = \sqrt{81}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\green{\boxed{s - 4 = ± 9}}[/tex]

Transpose - 4 to the right side.

[tex]\green{\boxed{s = ± 9 + 4}}[/tex]

There are two answers:

[tex]\green{\boxed{s = 9 + 4}}[/tex][tex]\purple{\boxed{s = 13}}[/tex]

[tex]\green{\boxed{s = -9 + 4}}[/tex][tex]\purple{\boxed{s = -5}}[/tex]
