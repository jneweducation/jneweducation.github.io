What is the volume of rectangular solid that is 4 m long, 3 m wide and 6 m high?

Given:

Length = 4 m

Width = 3 m

Height = 6 m

Formula:

Volume = Length × Width × Height

Solution:

Volume = 4 m × 3 m × 6 m

Volume = 72 m³
