Word Problems Involving Fractions and Decimals:

2 1/7 kilos of bangus will cost ₱217.50.

Solutions:

Given: 5/7 kilos of bangus sold for ₱72.50

           2 1/7 kilos of bangus that will be bought

1. First, compute for the price of bangus per kilo

₱72.50 ÷ 5/7 kilos = ₱72.50 × 7/5

Note: the operation was changed to multiplication and the reciprocal of the divisor was derived

2. Proceed with the multiplication.

₱72.50 × 7/5 = ₱507.50/5 =  ₱101.5

3. Given that the price of bangus per kilo is ₱101.5, multiply it with 2 1/7 kilos to determine the amount that should be paid.

₱101.5 × 2 1/7 kilos = ₱101.5 × 2.142857143

Note: 2 1/7 was expressed as a decimal

4. Proceed with the multiplication.

₱101.5 × 2.142857143 = ₱217.50

Therefore, I will pay ₱217.50 for 2 1.7 kilos of bangus that I have bought.
