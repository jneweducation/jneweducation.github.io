4. a girl what's to have a toy cost 200php. She is in Thailand. How many dolls that she can buy if She have 800 baht?​


PROBLEM:

A girl wants to have a toy cost 200 php. She is in Thailand. How many dolls that she can buy if she have 800 baht?

SOLUTION:

Let's first convert 800 baht to Philippine Peso (piso). If one baht is equivalent to 1.53 pesos, then 800 baht is equal to 1, 224 pesos.

[tex]\rm x=1.53 × 800 \:baht [/tex]

[tex]\rm x=Php.\: 1224.00[/tex]

If the girl's desired toy costs Php. 200 each and she has Php. 1224.00, then divide the money she has by the cost of the toy to get the number of dolls (y) she can buy.

[tex]\rm y=\frac{Php.\: 1224.00}{Php. \:200.00} [/tex]

[tex]\rm y=6.12[/tex]

ANSWER:The girl can buy 6 dolls.

30. ina resort entrance fee per person is 80 baht in a group of 10 people it fixed at 70% if the resort give additional additional 50% discount for every person that exists then how much is entrance fee paid by twenty one person with solution plss​


Answer:

Given:

Entrance fee per person = 80 baht

Group size = 10 people

Discount for a group of 10 people = 70%

Additional discount per person = 50%

Number of people = 21

To find:

Entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts

Solution:

The group of 10 people gets a discount of 70%, which means they will pay only 30% of the total entrance fee.

Total entrance fee for 10 people = 10 x 80 = 800 baht

Entrance fee for 10 people after 70% discount = 800 x 0.3 = 240 baht

So, the entrance fee for 1 person in the group of 10 = 240 / 10 = 24 baht

Now, every person gets an additional 50% discount. Therefore, the new entrance fee per person for the group of 10 people will be:

24 - (24 x 0.5) = 12 baht

The remaining 11 people will each pay 80 baht, and they will also receive a 50% discount:

80 - (80 x 0.5) = 40 baht

Therefore, the total entrance fee paid by 21 people is:

(10 x 12) + (11 x 40) = 120 + 440 = 560 baht

Therefore, the entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts is 560 baht.
