Answer:

lots of amusement park rides demonstrate the overall concept of physics, for example a roller coaster is a machine that uses gravity and inertia to send a train of cars along a winding track. 24. a 75kg person is suspended 15m above the air in an amusement park ride. How much potential energy does he have? ​


Answer:

Potential Energy is 11.03 KJ

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

m = 75 kg

h = 15 m

g = 9.8 m/s²

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Potential Energy

Step 3: Solve for Potential Energy

Solution:

The formula for Potential Energy is given by:

PE = mgh

where: PE - Potential Energy

m - mass

g - gravitational acceleration constant

h - height of object from the reference level

Substituting the value of m,g and h in the formula,

PE = (75 kg)(9.8 m/s²)(15 m)

PE = 11,025 kgm²/s² or 11,025 Nm or 11.025 KJ

or 11.03KJ
