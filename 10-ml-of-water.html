24. Calculate the percentage concentration of ethyl alcohol in a solution of 10 mL ethylalcohol in 40 mL water​


Given:

volume of solute = 10 mL

volume of solvent = 40 mL

solute: ethyl alcohol

solvent: water

Required:

percentage concentration

Solution:

[tex]\text{percentage concentration} = \dfrac{\text{volume of solute}}{\text{volume of solute + volume of solvent}} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex]\text{percentage concentration} = \dfrac{\text{10 mL}}{\text{10 mL + 40 mL}} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{percentage concentration} = 20\%}[/tex]

Answer:

percentage concentration = 20%
