19. Sphere is A=πr² Rectangle is A=lw/hw Square is SxS/S² Triangle is ½ bxh


Answer:

I think you meant to ask for the formulas for finding the area of a sphere, rectangle, square, and triangle. Here they are:

Area of a sphere: A = 4πr²

Area of a rectangle: A = lw (where l is length and w is width)

Area of a square: A = s² (where s is the length of one side)

Area of a triangle: A = 1/2 bh (where b is the base and h is the height)
