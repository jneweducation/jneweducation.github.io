15. the donation given to the flood victim amounted Php 37000. 00. if this amount was rounded to the nearest 1000, what could have been the lowest actual amount?​


Numbers that will become 37000 if rounded off to the nearest thousands:

36500, 36501, 36502, 36503, 36504, 36505, 36506,..., 36595, 36596, 36597, 36598, 36599

The lowest actual amount is 36500.

Since 5 is at the right of the rounding digit, it is rounded up so 36500 becomes 37000.

36499 is not the lowest actual amount because the number at the right side of the rounding digit is 4. If it is 4 and below, it is rounded down while if it is 5 to 9, it is rounded up. Therefore 36499 becomes 36000.

18. A. All1. It is currently 7:00 PM. What time in AM or PM) will it ne in 1000 hours?​


The time in 1000 hours will be 11:00 AM.

Step-by-step explanation:

Time Word Problem

The problem is asking for the time in 1000 hours if the current time is 7:00 PM. There are two steps to find the time in 1000 hours.

Divide the 1000 hours by 24 hours since the time 7:00 PM repeats every 24 hours. Add the remaining hours to 7:00 PM.

Solution:

Step 1: Divide

41

24 √ 1000

- 96

40

- 24

16

There 41 days and 16 hours in 1000 hours. Add 16 to 7:00 PM.

Step 2: Add

7:00 PM

+ 16:00

23:00

23:00 is the same as 11:00.

If you are confused about AM or PM, notice that the starting time is in PM, after 12 hours it will be 7:00 AM. So for the remaining 4 hours up to 11 is still in AM.

Final Answer:

The time in 1000 hours will be 11:00 AM.
