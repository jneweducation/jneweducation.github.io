Lesson 4: Analyzing Business Transaction Analyze the statements below and determine the affected account/s in each transaction and in which financial statement the account can be seen (balance sheet, income statement) 4 points for each number. 1. Purchased office equipment for cash. 2. Performed services to customers on account. (Assuming that the business is following the Accrual basis of accounting) 3. The owner invests cash to the business. 4. Received cash for providing services to customers. 5. Purchase supplies on account. 6. Paid telephone bill for the month. 7. Rendered services to clients to clients receiving a promissory note. 8. The owner withdrew cash for personal use. 9. Paid employee's salaries. 10. The owner invested additional cash to the business. Follow th I RAMAK​


Step-by-step explanation:

1. Purchased office equipment for cash.

Affected account/s: Office Equipment (asset account) and Cash (asset account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet

2. Performed services to customers on account. (Assuming that the business is following the Accrual basis of accounting)

Affected account/s: Accounts Receivable (asset account) and Service Revenue (income account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet and Income statement

3. The owner invests cash to the business.

Affected account/s: Cash (asset account) and Owner's Equity (capital account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet

4. Received cash for providing services to customers.

Affected account/s: Cash (asset account) and Service Revenue (income account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet and Income statement

5. Purchase supplies on account.

Affected account/s: Supplies (asset account) and Accounts Payable (liability account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet

6. Paid telephone bill for the month.

Affected account/s: Telephone Expense (expense account) and Cash (asset account)

Financial statement/s: Income statement

7. Rendered services to clients receiving a promissory note.

Affected account/s: Notes Receivable (asset account) and Service Revenue (income account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet and Income statement

8. The owner withdrew cash for personal use.

Affected account/s: Owner's Equity (capital account) and Cash (asset account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet

9. Paid employee's salaries.

Affected account/s: Salaries Expense (expense account) and Cash (asset account)

Financial statement/s: Income statement

10. The owner invested additional cash to the business.

Affected account/s: Cash (asset account) and Owner's Equity (capital account)

Financial statement/s: Balance sheet
