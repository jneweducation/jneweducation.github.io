15. What is reliable means? And what is Unreliable means?​


RELIABLE AND UNRELIABLE

What is reliable means? And what is Unreliable means?RELIABLE

When we say reliable, it is something that is usually rationally based, likely to be true and legitimate.

It can be relied on, because it can be trusted and provides what is necessary and needed.

UNRELIABLE

Unreliable on the other hand is something that is far-fetched, not likely to be true, subjective and does not provide any reliable and necessary thing.

This is usually something that is fake, fabricated or improvised.
