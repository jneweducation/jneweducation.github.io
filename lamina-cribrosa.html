15. 2. Leaves that are directly attached to the plant stem is.A. LaminaB. Mildrib C. Petiole D. Sessile​


ANSWER:

D. SESSILE

EXPLANATION:

Each leaf typically has a leaf blade called the lamina, which is also the widest part of the leaf. Some leaves are attached to the plant stem by a petiole. Leaves that do not have a petiole and are directly attached to the plant stem are called SESSILE leaves.

17. which of the following is the external structure that supports a leaf in capturing the light energy? a.) midrib b.) mesophyll c.) lamina d.) chloroplast pasagot po ng maayos plsssss


Answer:

D chloroplast

Explanation:

In plants, the process of photosynthesis takes place in the mesophyll of the leaves, inside the chloroplasts. Chloroplasts contain disc-shaped structures called thylakoids, which contain the pigment chlorophyll. Chlorophyll absorbs certain portions of the visible spectrum and captures energy from sunlight.
