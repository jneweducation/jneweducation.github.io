Given:

net income - P30,000.00

Nina - ½ of net income

Sarah - ⅓ of net income

Tessa - remaining net income

a. Tessa's share

1 - (½ + ⅓) = 1 - (3 + 2)/6 = 1 - ⅚ = (6 - 5)/6 = ⅙

⅙ of net income is Tessa's share.

b. Each net income.

Nina's share of net income = ½(P30,000.00) = P15,000.00

Sarah's share of net income = ⅓(P30,000.00) = P10,000.00

Tessa's share of net income = ⅙(P30,000.00) = P5,000.00

23. Mrs. Bullock is teaching a 5th grade class. She is standing 6 meters in front of Tessa. Anna is sitting to Tessa's right. If Anna and Mrs. Bullock are 10 meters apart, how far apart are Tessa and Anna?​


Answer:

8 meters

Step-by-step explanation:

Imagine a triangle with the three points: Mrs. Bullock, Tessa and Anna. They form a right triangle, so you can apply the pythagorean theorem.

a^2 + b^2 = c^2 where a and b are the legs and c is the hypotenuse.

6^2 + b^2 = 10^2

36 + b^2 = 100

b^2 = 64

b = 8
