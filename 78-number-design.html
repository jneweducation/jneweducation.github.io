Formula= Number x 100 = 78 x 100 = 100

Step 1: If 78% of a number is 78, then what is 100% of that number? Setup the equation.

78% 100%

78Y = 7800

Substitute it to equation 1

x + y = 78

14 + y + y = 78

y = 32

Substitute y = 32

x = 14+y

x = 14 + 32

x = 46

7. the sum of 3 consecutive even numbers is 78. find the numbers​


the sum of 3 consecutive even numbers is 78. find the numbers24 + 26 +28 = 78

Answer:

24, 26, 28

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the smallest of the 3 consecutive even numbers.

[tex]\sf x + (x+2) + (x+4) = 78[/tex]

[tex]\sf 3x + 6 = 78[/tex]

[tex]\sf 3x = 78-6[/tex]

[tex]\sf 3x = 72[/tex]

[tex]\sf x = \frac{72}{3}[/tex]

[tex]\sf x = 24[/tex]

[tex]\sf x + 2 = 24 + 2 = 26 \: (second)[/tex]

[tex]\sf x + 4 = 24 + 4 = 28 \: (third)[/tex]

Hence the numbers are 24, 26, and 28.
