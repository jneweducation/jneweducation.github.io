If the receiving clerk was careless in checking the quality and counting the quantity of food or supplies, it could lead to a variety of issues. The most severe consequence would be that the food establishment would receive incorrect orders, which could lead to customer dissatisfaction, wasted product, and lost revenue. Additionally, the food establishment would be at risk of receiving spoiled, expired, or contaminated food, which could lead to foodborne illness and legal action.
