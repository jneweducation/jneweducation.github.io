7. what is the median 41 40 45 56 56 54?​


Answer:

49.5

Step-by-step explanation:

The median is the middle number of the set of numbers. To find the median compile all numbers in ascending order and find the middle number. If there are 2 middle numbers, find the average of those two numbers. Since our ascending-ordered set is {40, 41, 45, 54, 56, 56}, and our middle numbers are 45 and 54, find the average of 45 and 54. Since their average is 49.5, the median is then 49.5.
