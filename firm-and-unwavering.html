15. why larger firms lower operating costs than small firms​


With a larger firm, there is more capital to invest in greater dimensions like a double decker bus, that can carry more people, so the fixed costs will be spread over a larger output, causing the average cost to be lower. Hence, production at a higher quantity can result in lower average costs.
