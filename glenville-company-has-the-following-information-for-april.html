10. 1. A credit card account has a P7680 balance on April 1. A purchase of P3160 was made on April 5, and a payment of P2000 was made on April 18. Find the average daily balance if the new billing date is May 1.​


Step-by-step explanation:

To find the average daily balance, we need to calculate the balance on each day and then add them up and divide by the number of days in the billing cycle. We can do this as follows:

1. From April 1 to April 4 (4 days), the balance is P7680.

2. On April 5, a purchase of P3160 is made, so the balance becomes P7680 + P3160 = P10,840.

3. From April 6 to April 17 (12 days), the balance is P10,840.

4. On April 18, a payment of P2000 is made, so the balance becomes P10,840 - P2000 = P8840.

5. From April 19 to April 30 (12 days), the balance is P8840.

To calculate the average daily balance, we add up the balances for each day and divide by the number of days in the billing cycle (30):

[tex]$(4 \times 7680) + (1 \times 10,840) + (12 \times 10,840) + (1 \times 8840) + (12 \times 8840) = 339,840$[/tex]

[tex]$339,840 \div 30 = 11,328$[/tex]

Therefore, the average daily balance for this billing cycle is P11,328.

25. On April 1, 2018 on the Company bought an equipment costing ₱100,000 with the useful of 5 years and salvage value of ₱10,000. Based on the information provided, how much is the depreciation expense to be reported in 2018?


Answer:

P 13, 500

Step-by-step explanation:

In solving for the Depreciation expense, there are several methods that ca be used. Based on the given problem, it is best to use the Straight Line Depreciation method.

The formula is:

(Book value-Salvage value) / Useful Life

Substituting the given to our formula,

(P 100, 000 - P 10, 000) / 5

P 18,000

We have P 18, 000 depreciation expense per year. However, for 2018, we only have to record depreciation for 9 months which is April 2018 - December 2018.

Thus, we multiply P 18, 000 to 9/12 (9 months out of the 12 months in a year) ans it will give us P 13, 500.

Therefore, the depreciation expense for 2018 is P 13, 500.
