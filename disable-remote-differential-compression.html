Answer:Energy transfer is the movement of energy from one location to another. For example, when electricity moves from a wall plug, through a charger, to a battery.

Energy transformation is when energy changes from one form to another – like in a hydroelectric dam that transforms the kinetic energy of water into electrical energy.

THE DISCOURAGE

To encourage someone means to give them support, confidence, or hope. An antonym is a word with the opposite meaning of another word. So, the antonym of encouraged would be a word with the opposite meaning of support, confidence, or hope. In this case, the word that best fits this description is discouraged. To discourage someone means to make them feel less confident, hopeful, or determined. It is the opposite of encouraged in that it undermines or weakens someone's resolve rather than strengthening it.

To discourage someone means to make them feel less confident, hopeful, or determined. This can be done through actions, words, or body language. For example, if a teacher tells a student that they are not good at math and will never be able to learn it, the teacher is discouraging the student. The student may then feel less confident and less motivated to learn math. In a real-world situation, someone who is illiterate may face discouragement from others who do not believe they can learn to read and write. This could lead the illiterate person to feel less confident and less motivated to learn, potentially making it harder for them to become literate.
