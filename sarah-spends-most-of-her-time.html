10. Rosa had 3 times as much money as Sarah. After spending $5, she had twice as much money as Sarah. How much money did Rosa have at first?​


Answer:

Rosa had $ 15

Solution:

Let Sarah's money be x and Rosa's money be y.

Rosa's Initial Money Equation

y = 3x

Rosa's Money After Spending Equation

y - 5 = 2x

Substitute the Rosa's Initial Money to Rosa's Money After Spending Equation

3x - 5 = 2x

3x  - 2x = 5

x = 5 → Sarah's initial money

Initial money of Rosa:

y = 3(5)

y =  15
