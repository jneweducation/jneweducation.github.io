1. Do we join groupsorgroup gatherings?​


We join groups.

Groups lie at the core of the human experience and underpin the structure  and accomplishments of human society. The variety of groups we may  belong to and identify with is enormous – it may include groups based on  ethnicity, ability, gender, nationality, religion, political ideology, profession,  employment, family, hobbies, etc.

In groups, people can coordinate their actions and cooperate to achieve

goals that individuals acting alone could never achieve (e.g., building

pyramids, landing on the moon, and curing diseases).

Groups vs. Group Gatherings:  

As nouns the difference between group and gathering is that:

group is a number of things or persons being in some relation to one another  gathering is a meeting or get-together; a party or social function.

As verbs the difference between group and gathering is that:

group is to put together to form a group while  gathering is present continuous of gather; collecting or bringing together.

Gatherings have groups of people who already know each other but we can still mingle and form connections with people in gatherings who already groups of their own. If we're lucky, we may also be included in their groups.

Definition of group

Recently, the use of words in speech and descriptions has become more widespread and many use words that are rarely used. So that sometimes we don't know the meaning of these words. Like the use of the word group. The group here can be defined as a community consisting of a collection of individuals. The definition of a community is individuals or people who have similar characteristics such as similar geography, culture, race, religion, or equal socioeconomic status. A community can be defined by location, race, ethnicity, occupation, interest in a problem, or other things that have something in common

An association is a legal entity that is a collection of people, established to realize certain common goals and objectives in the social, religious, and humanitarian fields, and does not distribute profits to its members.
