1. there are 100 bars of blue and green soap in a box.The blue bar weigh 50 g per bar and the green bar 40 g per bar.The total mass of tge soap in tge box is 4.66 kg.How many bars of green soap are in the box?â€‹


Answer:

34 green soaps

Step-by-step explanation:

Equations needed :

40x + 50y = 4660 (Equation 1)x + y = 100 (Equation 2)

Given :

green bars weigh 40gblue bars weigh 50g4.66kg = 4660g

Representations :

x = number of green barsy = number of blue bars

Solution :

Solve for y in Equation 2.

x + y = 100

x + y - x = 100 -x

y = 100 - x

substitute the value of y in Equation 1.

40x + 50y = 4660

40x + 50(100 - x) = 4660

40x + 5000 - 50x = 4660

-10x + 5000 - 5000 = 4660 - 5000

-10x = -340

-10x/-10 = -340/-10

x = 34

Checking :

Solve for the number of blue soaps in Equation 2

x + y = 100

34 + y = 100

34 + y - 34 = 100 - 34

y = 66

Substitute both values of x and y in Equation 1.

40x + 50y = 4660

40(34) + 50(66) = 4660

1360 + 3300 = 4660

4660 = 4660 âœ“

Therefore there are 34 green soaps in the box.
