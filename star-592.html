4. The surface area of a rectangular box is 592 cm^2. The edges of two faces are 10 cm and 8 cm. What is the other dimension?


Rectangular Box/Prism Surface Area = 2 [(l)(h) + (w)(h) + (l)(w)]

Where:

l = lenght = 10 cm
w = weight = 8 cm
h = height = ?

Equation:

592  cm² = 2 [10h cm + 8h cm + (10 cm) (8 cm) ]

592 cm² = 2 [18h cm + 80 cm²]

592 cm² = 36h cm + 160 cm²

36h cm = 592 cm² - 160 cm²

36h cm/ 36 cm  =  432 cm²/ 36 cm

h = 12 cm

ANSWER:  The other dimension is 12 cm.
