1. What force is required to punch a 25-mm-diameter hole in a plate that is30 mm thick? The shear strength is 450 MN/m2.​


Answer:

1060.29 KN

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

τ = 450 MN/m^2 = 450 x 10^6 N/m^2

t = 30 mm = 0.03 m

d = 25 mm = 0.025 m

SOLUTION:

Compute the area

A = (π)dt

A = (π)(0.025)(0.03)

A = 2.356 x 10^-3

τ = F/A

F = Aτ

F = (2.356 x 10^-3)(450 x 10^6 N/m^2)

F = 1,060,287.52 N

or

F = 1060.29 KN

11. How much material is required to make a circular skirt if the waist hole has a diameter of 10cm and the diameter of the circular skirt is 30cm? (Show solution)


Answer:

The fabric material required for the circular skirt 628 cm², excluding the allowances for hemline and edges.

Step-by-step explanation:

Solve for the area of the circular skirt with diameter 30 cm or radius 15 cm

Area = πr²

Area = (3.14)(15 cm)²

Area = (3.14)(225 cm²)

Area of the circular skirt = 706.5 cm²

Solve for the waist hole area with diameter of 10 cm or radius of 5 cm.

Area = (3.14)(5 cm²)

Area = (3.14)(25 cm²)

Area of waist hole = 78.5 cm²

Solve for the materials required:

706.5 cm² - 78.5 cm² = 628 cm²
