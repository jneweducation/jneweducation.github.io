What is Signage?

In general, signage is also called a graphic sign that aims to convey information. Which is useful for conveying and or providing information to others. The shape is very diverse and can adjust the size of the media, the design of the room, or the taste of the owner of the signage.

Purpose of Signage

The purpose of signage is to communicate and convey information so that the viewer/visitor to the place can understand and make decisions on the information provided. That's why today signage is more often found in public places such as hospitals, offices, and markets.

Whether we realize it or not, we are helped a lot by this signage. We have no trouble finding directions, confusion about finding goods, where the parking lot is when the workshop is open, and many others.

What are the Types

There are several types of signs that we encounter most often, including:

Building Signage

This is the most often encountered on the road when we are traveling around the city, usually placed on a building that shows the name of the place.

Directions

If this sign will be encountered when we are already in the building, usually attached to the wall, or ceiling.

Product Name

This one is usually found inside stores or supermarkets, showing detailed product information.
