The length of the stick is 57.75 cm.

Let us use L as the length of the stick. Since we know that 1 popsicle stick has a length of 7 cm and the length of the bigger stick measures 5.5 popsicle sticks, we can solve L using ratio and proportion.

The solution goes like this

[tex]\frac{1popsicle}{7cm}= \frac{5.5 popsicle}{\frac{2}{3}L } \\L =\frac{5.5(7)}{\frac{2}{3}(1)}\\ L = 57.75 cm[/tex]
