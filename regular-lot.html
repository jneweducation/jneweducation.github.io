[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

1.If the sum of the measures of the interior angles of a convex polygon is 3600 how many sides does the polygon have?

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad \boxed{ \begin{array}{} \rm \: sum = 180(n - 2) \\ \\ \rm 3600 = 180(n - 2) \\ \\ \rm\frac{3600}{180} = \frac{180(n - 2)}{180} \\ \\ \rm \cancel{\frac{3600}{180} } = \frac{ \cancel{180}(n - 2)}{ \cancel{180}} \\ \\ \rm20 = n - 2 \\ \\ \rm20 + 2 = n \\ \\ \boxed{\rm22 = n} \end{array}}[/tex]

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

The sides of the polygon are 22

[tex] \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

3. Find the number of sides of a regular polygon with an exterior angle of 45°.

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad\boxed{ \begin{array}{}\rm Exterior = \frac{360}{n} \\ \\ \rm 45 = \frac{360}{n} \\ \\ \rm n = \frac{360}{45} \\ \\ \boxed{ \rm n = 8} \end{array}}[/tex]

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{ANSWER:}}[/tex]
