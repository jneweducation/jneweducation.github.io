3. Consider that the mass of venus is 81.5% that of earth, and its radius is 94.9% that of the earth.what is the constant acceleration due to gravity near the surface of planet venus in terms of that of earth, g?


  g is proportional to the mass over radius square.


thus, g(venus)= 9.8m/s^2 (m/r^2)
Substitution:
g(venus)=  9.8m/s^2 [81.5%/(94.9%)^2]
              = 9.8m/s^2 x 0.904951
              = 8.87 m/s^2


The constant acceleration due to gravity near the surface of planet in term of that of earth, the venus is 8.87 m/s^2.
