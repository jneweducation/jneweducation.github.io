13. A ___________ relationship is created when the uid for an entity includes the relationship with another entity.


Answer:

An identifying relationship is created when the UID for an entity includes

the relationship with another entity.

The UID for the ACCOUNT entity is the  ACCOUNT Number as well as the relationship between BANK and  ACCOUNT.The identifying relationship is depicted with a vertical bar on the relationship line. Note that a relationship included in a UID must be mandatory and oneand-only-one in the direction that participates in the UID.An entity can be uniquely identified through multiple relationships.An EMPLOYEE and a PROJECT are  needed to make WORK ASSIGNMENT unique, so both relationships are  included in the UID for WORK ASSIGNMENT. Note that WORK ASSIGNMENT is an intersection entity that is the  resolution between an M:M relationship. Unique identifiers can be classified as natural and artificial. Natural unique identifiers exist in the real world system. Artificial UIDs do not occur in the natural world but are created for  identification purposes in a system.
