Answer:

The wide of the surrounding border is 1.5 meters.

In the given problem, we need to use the formula of finding the area and perimeter of a rectangle shape. The formula for area if LW, and perimeter is 2(L+W), where L is length and W is width.

First, find out the area of the water park without stone since the covered area of stone is only 144 sq. meters. So,

Area of a water park minus area of part covered by stone, the solution would be:

A= LW - 144 sq. m.

A= (20)(12) - 144

A= 280 - 144

A= 96 sq. meter

So, 96 sq. meter is the area of a border, and to identify the width of the border, calculate the perimeter of the pool in a water park.

P= 2(L+W)

P= 2(20+14)

P= 64 meters

Since the total area of water park without stone is 96 sq. meter, and the lenght of the pool (perimeter is 64 meters), and to identify the width of the border, just use the perimeter of the pool as lenght of border to calculate its lenght.

Area= LW

w= 1.5 meters

Therefore, the width of the border is 1.5 meters the surrounds the swimming pool without stone.
