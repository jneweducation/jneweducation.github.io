7. What is the difference between a blender and a food processor?


Answer:

A blender is designed to puree, mix, and blend liquids and soft ingredients such as fruits and vegetables. It has a narrow and tall pitcher with a rotating blade at the bottom. A food processor, on the other hand, is designed for chopping, shredding, slicing, and mixing solid and semi-solid ingredients such as nuts, cheese, and dough. It has a wider bowl and several interchangeable blades and disks for different types of processing.
