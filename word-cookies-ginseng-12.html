1. how ginseng reproduce​


Answer:

Ginseng Buds and Flowers

Ginseng buds and flowers

Ginseng Reproduction

Ginseng does not spread clonally, and therefore populations expand only through production, dispersal and germination of seeds. Ginseng flowers are not showy, but rather small and whitish-green, bunched together in an umbel. Ginseng flowers mature centripetally; flowers on the outside of the umbel blooming before the flowers located in the center of the umbel. Ginseng has a mixed mating system, meaning that seeds may be produced autogamously or through out-crossing events. Autogamous reproduction means that the plant reproduces via self-fertilization, and outcrossing refers to an ovum being fertilized by an external pollen source. There are two known pollinators of ginseng: syrphid flies and halictid bees. Both of these insects are generalist pollinators, meaning that they visit multiple species of plants when gathering pollen and nectar.

21. I LOVE BAKING: 750 grams of flour makes 25 cookies. How many grams of flour would beneeded to make the following?a. 9 cookies -b. 12 cookies -c. 10 cookies -d. 2 cookies -e. 18 cookies -​


a. 270 grams

b. 360 grams

c. 300 grams

d. 60 grams

e. 540 grams

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

The problem is asking for the grams of flour needed to make the following numbers of cookies. There are two steps to find the answer.

Find how many grams of flour is used in one cookie by dividing the total grams of flour by the number of cookies.Find the grams of flour needed for each number of cookies by multiplying the grams used in one cookie by the number of cookies to make.

Given:

total grams of flour - 750 grams

number of cookies - 25

9 cookies

12 cookies

10 cookies

2 cookies

18 cookies

Solution:

Step 1: Divide

750 grams ÷ 25 cookies = 30 grams

30 grams of flour is used to make one cookie.

Step 2: Multiply

a. 9 cookies × 30 grams = 270 grams

270 grams of flour are needed for 9 cookies.

b. 12 cookies × 30 grams = 360 grams

360 grams of flour are needed for 12 cookies.

c. 10 cookies × 30 grams = 300 grams

300 grams of flour are needed for 10 cookies.

d. 2 cookies × 30 grams = 60 grams

60 grams of flour are needed for 2 cookies.

e. 18 cookies × 30 grams = 540 grams

540 grams of flour are needed for 18 cookies.
