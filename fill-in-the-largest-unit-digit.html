Divisibility of a Number

The given problem is an application of basic mathematics specially in multiple and divisible of a numbers.

Divisibility is define as a number's quality of being evenly divided by another number, without any remainder.

Given problem

Fill in the largest unit digit to make them divisible by the number on the left.4(125)

Divisible of 4

A number is divisible by 4 if the last two digits of the number is multiple of 4.

125_, the last two digits is 5_. The possible largest unit number can be filled is 6. The number would be 56, 56 is a multiple of 4.

Check:

1256 ÷ 4 = 314 ✓ It is a divisible by 4, since there is no remainder when it was divided by 4 having an exact quotient of 314.

Divisible of 8

A number is divisible by 8 if the last three digits of a given number are zeros or it is divisible by 8.

8(3673), the last three digit is 73_. The posible greatest unit can be filled is 6 since 736 is divisible by 8.

Divisible of 11

The number is divisible of 11 if the difference between the sum of the digits from the odd places and the sum of the digits at the even places is 0 or multiples of 11.

11(1496), the even digits are 4, 6 and the sum is 10. The odd digits are 1, 9, and the missing one, their sum must be 10. So it was 0.

11(1562), the even digits are 5, 2 and the sum is 7. The digits are 1, 6, and the missing one, their sum must be 7. So it was 0.

Divisible of 12

A number is divisible of 12 if it is both divisible by 3 and 4. Note, a number is divisible by 3 if the sum of all digits is multiple of 3 and a number is divisible by 4 if the last two digits of the number is multiple of 4.

12(1562), the last two digits is 2 and the missing one. The posible unit are 0, 4 and 8 in order to make it divisible by 4. Get the sum of all digits to satisfy divisibility of 3.

1 + 5 + 6 + 2 + 0 = 14

1 + 5 + 6 + 2 + 4 = 18

1 + 5 + 6 + 2 + 8 = 22

So it was 4.
