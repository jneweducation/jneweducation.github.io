28. A doll was sold for php 2,460 with an 18% discount. What was the original price of the doll​


The original price of the doll is Php 3000.

Step-by-step explanation:

Finding the Original Price

To find the original price of a discoun ted or sale item, you need to know the sale price and the discount percentage.

Let us analyze the problem.

Given:

sale price - Php 2460

discount percentage - 18%

Formula in Finding the Original Price:

To calculate the original price, follow this formula:

OP = Sale Price ÷ (1 - Discount)

Solution:

In any calculations, you need to convert the percent to decimal first.

18% = 0.18

OP = Sale Price ÷ (1 - Discount)

= Php 2460 ÷ (1 - 0.18)

= Php 2460 ÷ 0.82

= Php 3000
