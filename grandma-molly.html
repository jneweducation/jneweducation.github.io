1. Your little sister Molly is one third the age of your mom. In 12years,Molly will be half the age of your mom.how old is Molly and your mom now?solution


Present ages:
Mom: x
Molly: 1 (x)
          3

In 12 years (+12):
Mom:  x + 12
Molly: 1 (x) + 12
          3

Equation: In 12 years, Molly will be half age of Mom:

[tex] \frac{x + 12}{2} = \frac{1}{3} x +12[/tex]

[tex]2( \frac{1}{3}) (x)+ 2(12) = x + 12[/tex]

[tex] \frac{2}{3} x+24 = x+12[/tex]

[tex] \frac{3}{3} x- \frac{2}{3} x = 24 - 12[/tex]

[tex] \frac{1}{3} x = 12[/tex]

[tex]( \frac{3}{1} )( \frac{1}{3} )x = 12( \frac{3}{1} )[/tex]

x= 36

Present ages:
Mom: x = 36 years old
Molly: 1/3 (36) = 12 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Ratio of Chris' prize money to Molly's prize money  ⇒  2:3Molly's prize:  $240Chris' prize money:  x

Find the prize money Chris receives:

2 : 3 = x : 240  ⇒  Direct proportion

3x = 2(240)   ⇒   Product of the means is equal to product of extremes

3x = 480

3x/3 = 480/3

x = 160

Chris receives $160.

30. Why does snowball tell mollie she cannot wear ribbons


Mollie, a conceited carriage horse, expresses anxiety that she will no longer be able to indulge in small luxuries such as eating sugar and wearing ribbons in the new utopia. Snowball informs her firmly that ribbons represent slavery and that they would have to be banned in the animals' heaven.

This signifies that when she turns to her ribbons, she is turning to the evil side, also known as the humans. This is because humans enslaved animals, and ribbons were made by humans.

Mollie comprehends only cold, hard facts. However, Mollie believes that selling the ribbons would make the lives of all farm animals more comfortable.

In the original Orwell novel, Mollie symbolizes the upper and middle-class Russians who emigrated to the West. In the novel, she fled to another farm after being banned from consuming sugar cubes and wearing ribbons on her head. However, the adaptation glosses over her real departure.
