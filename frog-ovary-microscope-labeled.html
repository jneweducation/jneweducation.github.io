22. what is the type of microscope (stereomicroscope, electron microscope, compound light microscope)? A high school student wishes to view his frog dissection a littlemore closely​


Answer:

Using the Compound Light Microscope:

1. Always carry the microscope by holding the arm of the microscope with one hand and

supporting the base with the other hand.

2. Place the microscope on a flat, sturdy surface. The arm should be positioned toward you.

3. Look through the eyepiece(s). Adjust the diaphragm so that the light comes through the

opening in the stage.
