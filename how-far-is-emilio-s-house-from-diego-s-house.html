30. How far is the gasoline station from Jose’s house if it is km south of Emilio’s house? Explain your answer.


Answer:

[tex]d=\sqrt{2}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

The distance from the gasoline station to Jose's house can be determined by using the Pythagorean theorem assuming that the distance from the Jose's house and Emilio's house is only 1 km.

From here, we set up the Pythagorean equation as:

[tex]d=\sqrt{1^2+1^2}[/tex]

From there, we get the distance as:

[tex]d=\sqrt{2}[/tex]
