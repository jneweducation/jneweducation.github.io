8. Glenn bought a car for 600000 the yearly depreciation of his car is 10% of its value at the start of the year. what is its value after 4 years


The answer is: 360,000

Given:

Original Car price: 600,000
Yearly depreciation: 10% of Original Car Price

Question: What is its value after four years?

Solution:

Depreciation= [600,000 x 0.10] x 4
Depreciation: 240,000

Car Price after 4 years= Original Car Price - Depreciation
Car Price after 4 years= 600,000 - 240,000
Car Price after 4 years= 360,000
