10. 10. The first term of a geometric sequence is 192 and the fifth term is 0.75. What is the common ratioa.4b.0.5c.0.25d.0.125​


Answer:

1/4

Step-by-step explanation:

In mathematics, a geometric progression or known as a geometric sequence is define as a sequence of numbers which has a common ratio, it means each term after the first is a product of the previous term and a constant value (r), r is non-zero number called the common ratio.

Formula in geometric sequenceFor finding nth term [tex]an = a1( {r}^{n - 1} )[/tex]For finding the sum [tex]sn = \frac{a1( {r}^{n} - 1)}{r - 1} [/tex]WhereSn = Sum of the sequencean = nth term of the sequencea1 = first termn = number of termsr = common ratio

ProblemFinding the Common Ratio

Solution

a1= 192

a5= 0.75

Use the first formula

[tex]an = a1( {r}^{n - 1} )[/tex]

[tex]0.75 = 192( {r}^{5 - 1} )[/tex]

[tex]0.75 = 192( {r}^{4} )[/tex]

[tex] \frac{0.75}{192} = {r}^{4} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{1}{256} = {r}^{4} [/tex]

[tex] \sqrt[4]{ \frac{1}{256} } = \sqrt[4]{ {r}^{4} } [/tex]

[tex] \frac{1}{4} = r[/tex]

r= 1/4

Therefore, the common ratio is 1/4.
