3. what is the efficiency of an engine that exhausts 8700 of heat and in the process was able to perform 3400 of work​


Answer:

Given:

Th = 250°C + 273 = 523 K

Tc = 300 K

Required:

η

Solution:

η = [1 - (Tc / Th)] × 100

η = [1 - (300 K / 523 K)] × 100

η = (1 - 0.5736) × 100

η = 0.4264 × 100

η = 42.64%

Answer:

η = 42.64%

19. Veagara tore earnde 8700 a week how many i her average earning in a day.


Answer:

Problem Solving

Veagara Tore earned 8700 a week, how many is her average earning in a day?

To solve this problem, we need to consider the following:

One week have 7 days, if weekends is not considered as working days, then Veagara Tore should have to work in 5 days per week.To get her average earning in a day, we need to divide her weekly earnings by the number of days in a week.We will be having two solutions, the one is for seven days, and one is for five days. You should choose which one is suitable for your answer.

Solution:

(for 5 days)

8700 ÷ 5 = n8700 ÷ 5 = 1,740She earned 1,740 per day in one week (consisting of 5 days)

(for 7 days)

8700 ÷ 7 = n8700 ÷ 7 = 1,242.857 or 1,242.86 (rounded off into nearest hundredths or two decimal places)She earned 1,242.86 per day in one week (which consists of 7 days)
