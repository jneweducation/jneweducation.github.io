5. What is the percentage of hydrogen in propanoic acid? Select one: a. 11.01 b. 6.81 c. 18.56 d. 8.16


Hello!

What is the percentage of hydrogen in propanoic acid?

Select one: a. 11.01 b. 6.81 c. 18.56 d. 8.16

Solving:

[First step]  Identify the compound:  

The propanoic acid compound has the following molecular formula

[tex]\boxed{CH_3CH_2COOH}[/tex]

[Second step]  Identify molar mass of propanoic acid [tex]MM_{CH_3CH_2COOH}[/tex] :

molecular weight of the element in the compound = number of elements (n) *  molecular weight

C = 3*(12.0107u) = 36.0321 u

H = 6*(1.00794u) = 6.04764 u

O = 2*(15.999u) = 31.998 u

----------------------------------------------

[tex]MM_{CH_3CH_2COOH} = 36.0321+6.04764+31.998[/tex]

[tex]MM_{CH_3CH_2COOH} = 74.07774[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{MM_{CH_3CH_2COOH} \approx 74.08\:g/mol}[/tex]

[Third step] Now, we apply the data to the percentage composition formula of an element, let us see:

[tex]Percent\:composition\:of\:an\:element = \dfrac{n*molar\:mass\:of\:element}{mass\:of\:compound}*100\%[/tex]

[tex]Percent\:composition\:of\:an\:element = \dfrac{6*1.00794}{74.08}*100\%[/tex]

[tex]Percent\:composition\:of\:an\:element = \dfrac{6.04764}{74.08}*100\%[/tex]

[tex]Percent\:composition\:of\:an\:element \approx 0.0816*100\%[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\boxed{Percent\:composition\:of\:an\:element \approx 8.16\:\%}}\end{array}}\qquad\checkmark[/tex]

Answer:
