28. The elisa is easier to perform and less expensive than the western blot


Yes, ELISA is less expensive and easier to perform than a Western blot.

The Western blot and ELISA tests can both detect HIV, or human immunodeficiency virus. It is an infection that weakens the immune system by destroying key cells that fight disease and infection.

ELISA stands for enzyme-linked immunoassay, it has numerous applications, including quality control in industry and diagnostic tools in pathology and medicine. It is a common laboratory test for detecting antibodies in the blood. It is not complicated and faster procedure than Western blotting, which is less specific.

Western blotting on the other hand is an analytical technique used to separate and identify proteins in a mixture. It is a technique for detecting a specific protein in a mixture.
