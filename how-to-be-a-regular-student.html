15. The regular admission rate for musical is ₱850.For students,senior citezen and persons with disability,the discounted rate is ₱400.The number of tickets sold at the regular rate is less than twice the number sold at the discounted rate.If the total ticket sales is ₱590,000 how many tickets were sold at the discounted rate?


Number of tickets sold at a discounted rate = 112,380.92

Further explanation: Given:

Regular admission rate = P850

Discounted rate = P400

Total ticket sale = P590,000

Required:

Number of tickets sold at the discounted rate.

Equation:

Let # of regular tickets sold = x

Let # of discounted tickets sold = y

Equation 1: 2y – x = 0

Equation 2: 850x + 400y = 590,000

Solution:

1. Use Equation 1 to find the value of “x”.

2y – x = 0  Equation 1.

2y = x         Transpose “x” into the other side.

2. Substitute the value of “x” on the Equation 2 to find the “y”.  

850x + 400y = 590,000  Equation 2.

850(2y) + 400y = 590,000  Substitute the value of “x”.

1,700y + 400y = 590,000  Multiply 850 with 2y.

2,100y = 590,000            Add 1,700y and 400y.

2,100y/ 2,100 = 590,000/ 2,100 Divide both sides by 2,100 to eliminate the coefficient.  

y = 280.9523

3. Substitute the value of “y” on Equation 2 to find the “x”.

850x + 400y = 590,000          Equation 2.

850x + 400(280.9523) = 590,000 Substitute the “y” into the equation.

850x + 112,380.92 = 590,000         Multiply 400 and 280.9523.

850x = 590,000 – 112,380.92         Transpose 112,380.92 unto the other side.

850x = 477,619.08                   Subtract 112,380.92 from 590,000.

850x/ 850 = 477,619.08/ 850         Divide both sides by 850 to eliminate the coefficient.  

x = 561.9048

4. To find the number of tickets sold at a discounted rate, multiply “y” to P400.

Number of tickets sold at a discounted rate = (400)(y)

Number of tickets sold at a discounted rate = (400)(280.9523)

Number of tickets sold at a discounted rate = 112,380.92

Checking:

850x + 400y = 590,000

850(561.9048) + 400(280.9523) = 590,000

477,619.08 + 112,380.92 = 590,000

590,000 = 590,000
