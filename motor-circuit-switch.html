1. what type of switch that is purposely used for motor control circuit​


✍Answer:

Motor Control Switches directly operate the motor with AC3 or AC4 duty rating. They are typically used for “Forward-Reverse”, “Star-Delta” and “Two Speed Forward-Reverse”. Motor Control Switches are also designed to operate, with a contactor having built-in tripping feature, in the event of power failure and overload.
