17. PS: This is stoichiometry.


Answer:

P2 = 175 atm

Explanation:

Convert °C to K

T1 = 160 °C + 273.15 = 433.15 K

T2 = 350 °C + 273.15 = 623.15 K

Use Combined Gas Law to solve for the final pressure

P2V2 / T2 = P1V1 / T1

P2 = P1 • (V1 / V2) • (T2 / T1)

P2 = 170 atm • (30 m L / 42 m L) • (623.15 K / 433.15 K)

P2 = 175 atm

24. what is the measure of PS ​


Answer:

The metric equivalent of a single horsepower, referred to as 1 PS, equals 4,500 kilogram-metres per minute, which rounds to 32,550 foot-pounds per minute, or 0.9863 of a horsepower.

The official engineering standard for metric horsepower is the amount of power needed to lift a 75kg of mass one metre vertically in one second, which – once the conversions from imperial to metric are applied – equates to a 1.4 per cent higher figure than the old imperial units

28. PS: This is stoichiometry.


Answer:

MM gas = 162.2 g/mol

Explanation:

Use Graham's Law of Effusion to solve for the molar mass of the unknown gas

√(MM gas / MM H2) = rate H2 / rate gas

MM gas / MM H2 = (rate H2 / rate gas)²

MM gas = MM H2 • (rate H2 / rate gas)²

MM gas = 2.0156 g/mol • (8.97)²

MM gas = 162.2 g/mol
