20. W^2 + 5w - 84 = 0 ? ​


Answer by factoring:

w²+5w-84=0

=(w-7)(w+12)

= w=7 w=-12

Another answer for it:

Using factoring by grouping:

w²+5w-84=0

=(w²-7w)+(12w-84)

=w(w-7)+12(w-7)

=(w-7)(w+12)

= w=7, w=-12
