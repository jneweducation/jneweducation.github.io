19. what is the difference between a libero and defense specialist?​


Answer:

The libero is allowed to serve for one player, and only one player, on the court, while a defense specialist is a player that substitutes in for any player, They are a reliable role player who provides consistent good passes during serve receive, consistent digging, good defensive coverage, and possibly good serving. Defensive specialists are also the players who scramble for every ball, making the impossible saves.
