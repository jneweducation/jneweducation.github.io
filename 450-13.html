2. What is the value of 450+ 9-2(37+ 13-30)? ​


Answer:

Therefore, the value of 450 + 9 - 2(37 + 13 - 30) is 419.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we evaluate the expression inside the parentheses:

37 + 13 - 30 = 20

So we have:

450 + 9 - 2(20)

Next, we evaluate the expression inside the parentheses:

450 + 9 - 2(20) = 450 + 9 - 40

Next, we simplify the expression inside the parentheses:

450 + 9 - 40 = 450 - 31

Finally, we add and subtract from left to right:

450 - 31 = 419

Therefore, the value of 450 + 9 - 2(37 + 13 - 30) is 419.
