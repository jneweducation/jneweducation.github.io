21. There were 9500 spectators at a football match, 6375 of them are men. of the remaining spectators, there were 4 times as many dhildren as women. How many children were there? ​


Answer:

There were 2,500 children

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

9,500 = total no. of spectator, and;

6,375 = men, then:

No. of women and children = 9,500 - 6,375 = 3,125.

Then, let x = (No. of women), therefore;

(No. of children) = 4x.

Number sentence: 4x+x = 3,125

Solution:

First, compute for (No. of women):

4x+x = 3,125

5x = 3,125

x = 3,125/5

x = 625

Then, compute for (No. of children):

(No. of children) = 4x = 4(625) = 2,500

Finally, checking:

If: (No. of children) + (No. of Women) = 3,125; then:

(No. of children) = 3,125 - (No. of women)

2,500 = 3,125 - 625

2,500 = 2,500
