Answer:

Question: The color of copper II sulfate penta hydrate is blue it is chemical property or physical property?

Answer:

Physical Properties

Anhydrous CuSO4 has a grey-white, powdery appearance whereas the pentahydrate has a bright blue colour. Both hydrated and anhydrous copper sulfates tend to decompose on heating and hence do not have exact boiling points.
