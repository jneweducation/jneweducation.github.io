Answer:

We have:

1+ 4= 5

5+ 7= 12

12+ 10= 22

22+ 13 = 35

We observe that difference between 2 consecutive terms of this series is in Arithmetic progression(4,7,10,13) with common difference as 3.

Hence, next term in the series obtained by taking difference between 2 consecutive numbers of the given series will be 13+3=16.

And, next term of the given series is: 35+16= 51.
