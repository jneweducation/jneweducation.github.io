4. Find n in ∛(n+1)= 2 A. 19/27 B. –(19/27) C. 27 D. -27


Answer:

The value of n is 7.

Thus, the answer is not in the choices given.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find n in the given problem:

First, cube both sides of the equation to extract the cube root of the left side of the equation.

[tex]( \sqrt[3]{(n + 1)} ) {}^{3} = {(2)}^{3} [/tex]

[tex]n + 1 = 8[/tex]

Second, transpose 1 to the right side of the equation so that n will be left on the other side.

[tex]n = 8 - 1[/tex]

Lastly, simplify.

[tex]n = 7[/tex]
