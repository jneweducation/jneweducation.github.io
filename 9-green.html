13. if 6 chips are drawn from a bowl containing 9 green and 8 yellow chips, what is the probability that 4 will be green and 2 yellow?​


Answer:

63/221

Step-by-step explanation:

Number of ways drawing 4 green: 9C4=126

Number of ways drawing 2 yellow: 8C2=28

Number of ways drawing 4 green ang 2 yellow: 126(28)=3528

Total number of ways of drawing 6 chips from 9 green and 8 yellow=12736

Probability=3528/12736

Probability=63/221
