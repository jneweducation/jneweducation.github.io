10. evaluate factors causing social political and cultural change and advocate how you message hyatt is should adapt to such change?PA ANSWER​


The causes of socio-political changes include economic development and technological advancement. Economic developments can be good for society but not all societies benefit from this process equally. Many countries around the world have benefited hugely from the technological advancements that came about through globalization. Some other reasons why there has been change in our society includes immigration, environmental pollution etc. It is impossible to find one single cause behind every kind of societal change. We just need to recognize the fact that some people profit from certain trends while others suffer from its effects. Hence, we need to devise ways by which we can avoid negative consequences of some trends and capitalize on positive ones.
