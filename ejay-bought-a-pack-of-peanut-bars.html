1. Ejay bought a pack of peanut bars from the neighborhood store. He ate 1/3 of the context immediately. He gave 20 pieces to his sister Aileen. Then he ate 1/2 of the remaining bars. If he has 25 peanut bars left after, how many bars did the pack originally contain?


Answer:

105 bars

.

Step-by-step explanation:

He ate 1/3 of the context immediately:

x - 1/3x

.

He gave 20 pieces to his sister Aileen:

(x - 1/3x) - 20

.

Then he ate 1/2 of the remaining bars:

(x - 1/3x) - 20 - 1/2[(x - 1/3x) - 20]

.

If he has 25 peanut bars left after:

(x - 1/3x) - 20 - 1/2[(x - 1/3x) - 20] = 25

x - 1/3x - 20 - 1/2(x - 1/3x - 20) = 25

x - 1/3x - 20 - 1/2x + 1/6x + 10 = 25

.

Multiply all by 6:

(x - 1/3x - 20 - 1/2x + 1/6x + 10 = 25) × 6

6x - 2x - 120 - 3x + x + 60 = 150

2x - 60 = 150

2x = 210

x = 105

.

Checking:

He ate 1/3 of the context immediately:

105 - 1/3(105)

105 - 35

= 70

He gave 20 pieces to his sister Aileen:

70 - 20

= 50

Then he ate 1/2 of the remaining bars:

50 - 1/2(50)

50 - 25

= 25

If he has 25 peanut bars left after:

25 = 25
