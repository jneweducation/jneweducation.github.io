21. enumerate and explain the characteristics of a good dance


Answer:

1. Physical Strength

Without physical fitness and stamina, dancers cannot successfully perform. They must also maintain a high degree of physical flexibility for the many types of skilled movement expected of them. And dancers need to be conscious of diet and nutrition to keep their physical systems intact.

2. Creativity

Dancers require creativity, which helps them to express the music and story lines they dance to using unique body movements.

3. Teamwork

Dancers almost always need to work with others, even if they occasionally perform solo. Being able to communicate and respond to others' cues on the dance floor is essential for flawless execution of often complex dance moves.

4. Persistence

They must have the characteristic of persistence or perseverance to keep rehearsing their moves and routines until they are perfect – with the ability to learn quickly and retain information.
