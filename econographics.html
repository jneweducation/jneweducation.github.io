28. strength and weaknesses of ethnographic?​


Answer:

Explanation:

Weakness: time consuming and requires a well-trained researcher. It takes time to build trust with informants in order to facilitate full and honest discourse. Short-term studies are at a particular disadvantage in this regard.

Strength: can help identify and analyze unexpected issues. When conducting other types of studies, which are not based on in-situ observation or interaction, it can very easy to miss unexpected issues.
