29. what is the answer of x+47 > 36


Answer:

x > -11

Step-by-step explanation:

x + 47 > 36

to eliminate +47 from the left side of the inequality, add -47 to both side of the inequality (Note: -47 because it is the additive inverse of +47)

x + 47 + (-47) > 36 + (-47)

x > -11 since 47 + (-47) = 0, and 36 + (-47) = -11
