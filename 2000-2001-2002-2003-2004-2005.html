Ang rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP para sa taon 2002-2003 ay:

[(4,631,479 - 4,218,883) / 4,218,883] x 100% = 9.78%

Ang rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP para sa taon 2003-2004 ay:

[(5,248,064 - 4,631,479) / 4,631,479] x 100% = 13.32%

Ang rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP para sa taon 2004-2005 ay:

[(5,891,183 - 5,248,064) / 5,248,064] x 100% = 12.22%

Ang rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP para sa taon 2005-2006 ay:

[(6,533,775 - 5,891,183) / 5,891,183] x 100% = 10.92%

Ang rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP para sa taon 2006-2007 ay:

[(7,249,323 - 6,533,775) / 6,533,775] x 100% = 10.96%

Kaya ang mga rate ng paglago ng nominal GNP ay:

•2002-2003: 9.78%

•2003-2004: 13.32%

•2004-2005: 12.22%

14. RA 9163 is also known as the_? a.NSTP act of 2001 b.NSTP Act of 2002 c.NSTP act of 2003 d NSTP act of 2004?​


RA 9163:

Answer:

a. NSTP Act of 2001

Explanation:

Republic Act 9163 is a law regarding the National Service Training Program which is enacted in 2001. It is a program which aims to enhance civic consciousness and defense preparedness in youths through enhancing their patriotism and service ethics while on training. It covers three different programs: (1.) Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) (2.) Literary Training Service (LTS) and (3.) Reserve Officers Training Corps (ROTC). Though enacted in 2001, its implementation took place the following year. The main objective of this program is to improve civic consciousness and national defense preparedness among the Filipino youth specifically those that develop their total well - being as agents toward the pursuit of national development.
