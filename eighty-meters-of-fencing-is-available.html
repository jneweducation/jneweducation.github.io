1. 10. Eighty meters of fencing is available to enclose the rectangular garden of Mang Gustin. Give a function A that can represent the area that can be enclosed in terms of x. *


Quadratic FunctionAnswer: A = 4x - x²

Explanation

The given problem is an application of function, perimeter and area of a plane figure. You will apply your basic knowledge in mathematics and algebra. The given problem, "Eighty meters of fencing is available to enclose the rectangular garden of Mang Gustin. Give a function A that can represent the area that can be enclosed in terms of x."

Solution and Discussion

In mathematics, function is define as a binary relation between of two sets. It is an equation for which any variable (input) that can be plugged into the equation will yield exactly to an ouputone out of the equation.

In this problem, in order to get the function, you must create first the mathematical equation related on the problem. It can be done by applying concepts of perimeter and area of the rectangle.

What is perimeter of a rectangle? Perimeter of a rectangle is the total distance around the edge of a rectangle. Finding the perimeter by adding up four sides of various the rectangle. It was derived into P = 2L + 2W, where L is the length, and W is the width.

How about the area of a rectangle? Area of a rectangle is the space occupied by the shape express in square units. Finding area is the product of length and width (A = lw)

Step-by-step Solution

Step 1: Identify all the given in the problem

8 meters, perimeter of the rectangle

dimension in terms of x

Step 2: Create mathematical equation using the formula of perimeter

P = 2l + 2w, let x is the length

8 = 2x + 2w → simplify the equation

4 = x + w

w = 4 - x

Step 3: Make the function in terms of x, using the formula for finding are of a rectangle.

A = lw

Where w = 4 - x and l = x

A = (x)(4 - x)

Simplify the equation

A = 4x - x²

Thus, 4x - x² is the function of A in terms of x.

6. 10. Eighty meters of fencing is available to enclose the rectangular garden of Mang Gustin. Give a function A that can represent the area that can be enclosed in terms of x. a. A(x) = 40x - x2 b. A(x) = 80x - x2 c. A(x) = 40x2 - d. A(x) = 80x2 - x​


FUNCTIONSProblem:

Eighty meters of fencing is available to enclose the rectangular garden of Mang Gustin. Give a function A that can represent the area that can be enclosed in terms of x.

Solution:

Since there are 8 meters of fencing available to enclose a rectangular garden, therefore, the perimeter of this garden is possibly 80 meters. Remember the perimeter of a rectangle which is P = 2(L + W). On the other hand, its area can be solved using the formula A = L×W. Now let us represent the following variables.

The length of the garden can be represented by x.

The width of the garden can be represented by y.

The perimeter is already known as 80 meters.

So:

P = 2(L + W)

80 = 2(x + y)

Let us divide both sides by 2, and we will obtain:

40 = x + y

Then let us equate the equation to get the value of y, so using the additive inverse property, we need to subtract x on both sides of the equation to isolate y.

40 - x = x + y - x

40 - x = y + x - x

40 - x = y

y = 40 - x

Since we already know the value of y, let us go to the formula of the area and substitute this value:

A = L×W

A = xy

A = x(40 - x)

By distributive property of multiplication over addition, we will be able to obtain the function that the problem requires:

[tex]A = 40x - x^{2}[/tex]

Expressing it as a function:

[tex]A(x) = 40x - x^{2}[/tex]

Therefore, the correct option is option A.

One hundred meters of fencing is available to enclose a rectangular area next to a river. Give a function of A that can represent the area that can be enclosed, in terms of x.

How?

Let P = the perimeter of the rectangle. and it is given to be one hundred meters.

A perimeter is the total length that bounded a given shape, for this problem it is a rectangle, in which, its perimeter is equal to 2x + 2y.

Where;

x = Length of the rectangle

y = Width of the rectangle

Therefore, P = 2x + 2y ; 100 = 2x + 2y

Since the problem requires the dimensions in term of "x", we must change y or the width in terms of "x".

 So, 100 = 2x + 2y, then add 2y on both sides, resulting to:

2y = 100 - 2x, next step is divide the whole term by two.

2y/2 = 100/2 - 2x/2

Therefore, the width is equal to: y = 50 - x

Next step is let A = area of a rectangle

In math, area is the space occupied by the object's surface.

Area of a rectangle can be defined by using the formula;

Area = (x)(y)

or simply the product of its length and width.

Area as function of x:

Since width (y) is equal to 50 - x

Therefore, the function of A in terms of x is:

A(x) = (x)(50-x) or

[tex]A(x) = 50x - x^{2}[/tex]
