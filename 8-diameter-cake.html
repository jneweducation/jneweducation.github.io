The units for Volume are always squared_________cubed 2. Volume is the amount of space an object takes up True False Calculate for the volume of solid. 3. Chona is selling Pringle Chips to raise money for a field trip. The container has a diameter of 9 inches and a height of 32 inches. 4. Answer:

1. The units for Volume are always cubed. (Check mark should be placed on "cubed.")

2. Volume is the amount of space an object takes up. (True)

3. Volume of Pringle Chips container:

Given:

Diameter = 9 inches

Height = 32 inches

To calculate the volume of a cylinder, we use the formula:

Volume = π * radius^2 * height

Since the diameter is given, we need to find the radius first:

Radius = diameter / 2 = 9 inches / 2 = 4.5 inches

Now, substitute the values into the formula:

Volume = 3.14 * (4.5 inches)^2 * 32 inches

Volume ≈ 2030.4 cubic inches

4. Volume of the 3-tier cake:

Given:

Height = 10 inches

Radius of the first tier = 12 inches

Radius of the second tier = 8 inches

Radius of the third tier = 5 inches

To calculate the volume of a cylinder, we use the formula:

Volume = π * radius^2 * height

Now, calculate the volumes of each tier and sum them up:

Volume = (3.14 * (12 inches)^2 * 10 inches) + (3.14 * (8 inches)^2 * 10 inches) + (3.14 * (5 inches)^2 * 10 inches)

Volume ≈ 6028.4 cubic inches

5. Volume of the Styrofoam volcano model (cone):

Given:

Diameter of the base = 48 centimeters

Height = 12 centimeters

To calculate the volume of a cone, we use the formula:

Volume = (1/3) * π * radius^2 * height

Since the diameter is given, we need to find the radius first:

Radius = diameter / 2 = 48 centimeters / 2 = 24 centimeters

Now, substitute the values into the formula:

Volume = (1/3) * 3.14 * (24 centimeters)^2 * 12 centimeters

Volume ≈ 27,033.6 cubic centimeters (rounded to the nearest tenth)

Please note that the calculations above are based on the given information and formulas. Double-check the calculations and make sure to use the correct units for the final answers.
