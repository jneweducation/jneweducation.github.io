Factor Tree

A factor tree refers to a diagram used to identify the prime factors of the numbers. In factor tree, you have to start with a pair of factors whose product is equal to the number that you are factoring and you have to branch out each factor until you end up with prime numbers. Keep in mind that there are various pairs of factors that you could choose and your choice does not matter. You will still end up with the same prime factors.
