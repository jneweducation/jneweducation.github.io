30. A 12 inches by 16 inches rectangular board is used to form an open box. An identical square is to be cut out in each corner to form a box with a total volume of 60 cubic inches.What are the dimensions? A. 10 in. x 3 in. x 2 in. B. 15 in x 2 in. x 2 in. C. 6 in. x 5 in. x 2 in4 D. 6 in. x 4 in. x 3 in.


Answer:

C.) 6 in. x 5 in. x 2 in

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

length of rectangle  = 16in

width of rectangle = 12in

V = 60cu.m.

let x be the dimension of cut square in each corner.

Let L  be the new length of cube

Let W be the new width of cube

Let H be the height of cube.

L = 16 - 2x

w = 12 - 2x

H = x

V = LWH

60 = (16 - 2x) (12 - 2x) x

expanding the equation

[tex]60=(192-32x-24x+4x^{2} )x[/tex]

[tex]x(4x^{2}-56x+192 )=60[/tex]

[tex]4x^{3}-56x^{2}+192x=60[/tex] eq.1

we assume the roots, x = [tex]\sqrt[3]{60}[/tex] ≅ 3.9

let say x = 5

substitue x = 5 to eq.1

[tex]4(5)^{3}-56(5)^{2}+192(5)=60[/tex]

500 - 1400 + 960 = 60

60 = 60, ok

x = 5 is one of the roots.

if x  = 5, we can get L, W, H

H = x

H = 5

L = 16 - 2x

L = 16 - 2(5)

L = 6

W = 12 - 2X

W = 12 - 2(5)

W = 2

Check Volume:

V = 6 x 2 x 5 = 60, ok
