17. HELPP.. pls.. answer it reasonable in a short sentence, THANK YOU1. why we need to wipe the thermometer from fingers toward the bulb after use ?2. why we need to wipe the thermometer from bulb toward the fingers before to use ?​


1.) When wiping a thermometer from fingers towards the bulb after use, the purpose is to remove any moisture, dirt or other contaminants that may have accumulated on the thermometer during use. This is important because the presence of these substances can interfere with the accuracy of the thermometer readings. By wiping the thermometer in this direction, you are removing the contaminants and ensuring that the thermometer is clean and ready for use next time.

2.) Wiping a thermometer from the bulb towards the fingers before use is not recommended as a standard practice. This direction of wiping may transfer any dirt or moisture from the fingers to the thermometer, potentially compromising the accuracy of the readings. It is recommended to first sanitize the thermometer before use and to wipe it towards the bulb after use to ensure that it is clean and accurate.

27. Statement Using fingers to measure rice Showing respect for elders Stand Agree or disagree Reason​


I disagree with the statement.

Reason: While it is true that using fingers to measure rice is a common practice in some cultures and is often associated with traditional ways of cooking and serving food, it is not necessarily an act of respect towards elders. Respect for elders should be shown through actions and behaviors that honor their wisdom, life experiences, and contributions to society. Moreover, measuring rice using fingers may not be a hygienic practice as it can lead to contamination of the food. Therefore, while the act of using fingers to measure rice may be a cultural practice, it should not be equated with respect for elders.

30. 5 ways to protect your fingers being hammered​


Answers and Explanation:

Listed below are ways to protect your finger from being hammered:

1. Using a hammer that suits hor hand size.

- Using a hammer that is comfortable for you and that is the proper size and weight for the job can prevent you from possibly getting an injury as you can control it better.  

2. Ensure that the head of the hammer is firmly attached to the handle.

- Another way of avoiding injuries is by checking the condition of your tool. If there is the possibility of detachment of some of it, repair or change to avoid unwanted accidents.

3.  Keep your work area clear.

- By keeping your work area clear, you can see the handwork better and avoid hurting yourself.

4. Use the hammer properly.

- Strike a hammer blow squarely with the striking face parallel to the surface being struck. Always avoid glancing blows and over and under strikes.

5. Do not use a hammer for any purpose for which it was not designed or intended.

- Tools, like hammers are invented for certain purposes. Making use of it for unintended purposes may cause accidents like husting your fingers.
