Answer:

Vertebrates

A vertebrate is an animal that has a backbone. They are classified as “chordates”, and are further subdivided as mammals, reptiles, amphibians, birds, or fish. On the other hand, animals that lack a vertebrate are referred to as invertebrates.

From the list of animals states in the question, the animals with backbone are the following:

Dolphin Bat Tamaraw Monkey Rabbit Mouse

On the other hand, the animals that lack a backbone are the following:

Centipedes Beetle Mosquito Ant

My basis for grouping these animals is their taxonomic classification. Centipedes, beetles, mosquitos, and ants are all insects, classified under the arthropod phylum. The rest are classified as mammals.
