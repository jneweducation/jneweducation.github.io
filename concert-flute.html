12. In the concert there is a ratio of men to women that is 2:3 if the total number of people in the concert is 350. How many women are in the concert?


Answer:

There are 210 women in the concert.

Step-by-step explanation:

Partitive Proportion

Partitive proportion is a kind of proportion. It is the identification of the parts of a whole based on given ratios.

How to solve partitive proportion?

Partitive proportion can be accomplished by adding up the ratios, dividing the whole with the sum and multiplying the quotient by its parts of the ratio.

Going back to the problem above, we need to know the number of women in the concert. There are two steps to find the answer.

1. Add up the parts of the ratio.

2. Divide the total number of people in the concert by the sum.

3. Multiply the quotient by its parts.

Given:

ratio of men to women - 2:3

total number of people in the concert - 350

Solution:

Step #1

2:3

2 + 3 = 5

Step #2

350 ÷ 5 = 70

Step #3

men = 70 × 2 = 140

women = 70 × 3 = 210

Therefore, 2:3 is 140:210.

Final Answer:

There are 210 women in the concert.

Checking:

To check, cross multiply.

140:210 = 2:3

140 × 3 = 210 × 2
