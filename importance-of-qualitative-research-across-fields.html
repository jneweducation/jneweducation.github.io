7. What are your views to the qualitative research across fields of inquiry? Is research important? Why? Present your answers in various viewpoints in terms of? social-economical-political-​


Answer:

I can provide some general information on the importance of qualitative research from various perspectives:

Social:

Qualitative research can provide insights into social phenomena, such as cultural practices, social norms, and interpersonal relationships. It can help researchers to better understand the complexities of human behavior and experiences, and to uncover underlying meanings and motivations. This can inform policy decisions and social interventions aimed at improving the lives of individuals and communities.

Economical:

Qualitative research can help to identify and address problems and opportunities within organizations and markets. It can provide detailed information on consumer preferences and behaviors, as well as employee attitudes and experiences. This can inform marketing strategies, product development, and human resources policies, leading to improved efficiency and profitability.

Political:

Qualitative research can shed light on the experiences and perspectives of marginalized groups, such as women, ethnic minorities, and LGBTQ+ individuals. This can inform policy decisions aimed at reducing inequality and promoting social justice. It can also help to identify areas of social conflict and tension, which can inform peace-building and conflict resolution efforts.

23. explain how does qualitative research across fields is important especially in the disciplines commonly associated in sociology and anthropology?cosist of 5 sentences​


Qualitative research has a long history in sociology and has been used within it for as long as the field has existed. This type of research has long appealed to social scientists because it allows the researchers to investigate the meanings people attribute to their behavior, actions, and interactions with others. But it's also important to understand what qualitative research is – an approach used largely in the social sciences to explore social interactions, systems and processes. It provides an in-depth understanding of the ways people come to understand, act and manage their day-to-day situations in particular settings. The purpose of quantitative research is to generate knowledge and create understanding about the social world.
