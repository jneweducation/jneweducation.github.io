3. Beginning inventory is P50,000, net cost of purchases is P125,000, and ending inventory is P35,000. Compute for the cost of goods sold.


Answer:

P140,000

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's identify the given first and give substitution on variables for simplicity.

B = Beginning Inventory  

N = Net Cost of Purchase

E = Ending Inventory

S = Cost of Goods Sold

The Values of our variables are this:

B = P50,000

N = P125,000

E = P35,000

S = ?

Our problem is to find "S", the Cost of Goods Sold.

S = (B+N) - E

Subtitute the Values

S = (P50,000 + P125,000) - P35,000

Add first the values inside the clause.

S = P175,000 - P35, 000

Proceed on Subtracting

S = P140,000

So the total value of Cost of Goods that were sold is P140,000.
