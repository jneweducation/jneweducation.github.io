5. Give the correct meaning of the abbreviated words in digital terminologies.1. LOWASP- 2. CND - 3. LAN- 4. CW - 5. P2P -​


Answer:

LOWASP - This abbreviation is not commonly used in digital terminologies. Without additional context, it is difficult to determine its meaning.

CND - CND stands for "Content Delivery Network." It refers to a geographically distributed network of servers that work together to deliver web content, such as images and videos, to users in a fast and efficient manner.

LAN - LAN stands for "Local Area Network." It refers to a computer network that connects devices within a limited geographic area, such as a home, office, or school.

CW - CW can refer to several things depending on the context. In digital terminology, it is most commonly used as an abbreviation for "Continuous Wave," which refers to a signal or transmission that is always on.

P2P - P2P stands for "Peer-to-Peer." It refers to a type of network architecture in which each device on the network can act as both a client and a server, allowing them to share resources, such as files or computing power, with other devices on the network directly, without the need for a central server.

30. 3.) The length (1) of the rectangle is x² + 2x - 3and the width CW) is 5x +4, what isthe perimeter of the rectangle​


PERIMETER OF A RECTANGLE

The length (L) of the rectangle is x² + 2x - 3 and the width (W) is 5x + 4, what is the perimeter of the rectangle?

The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is twice the sum of the length and the width.

P = 2 (L + W)

P = 2 (x² + 2x - 3) + (5x + 4)

P = 2 (x² + 2x - 3 + 5x + 4)

P = 2 (x² + 2x + 5x - 3 + 4)

P = 2 (x² + 7x + 1)

P = 2x² + 14x + 2

FINAL ANSWER:

The perimeter of a rectangle is 2x² + 14x + 2.
