5. Earth's magnetic field is generated by? a.a giant bar magnet at the center of the planet b.movement of molten iron and nickel in the outer core c.plate tectonics d.radiation from the sun​


Answer:

B.movement of molten iron and nickel in the outer core

Explanation:

The Earth's magnetic field is mostly caused by electric currents in the liquid outer core. The Earth's core is hotter than 1043 K, the Curie point temperature above which the orientations of spins within iron become randomized. Such randomization causes the substance to lose its magnetization.

The magnetic field of Earth is caused by currents of electricity that flow in the molten core. These currents are hundreds of miles wide and flow at thousands of miles per hour as the earth rotates. The powerful magnetic field passes out through the core of the earth, passes through the crust and enters space.

7. The sum of Annie's and Anna's age is 30. Anna is 8 years more than Annie. Find their age.​


Answer:

Annie is 14 and Anna is 16.

Step-by-step explanation:

The first sentence of the problem states that the sum of their ages is 30. We can treat this as a number problem by saying the sum of two numbers is 30.

If we let x to be Annie's age, then Anna's age is 30 - x.

Then, the next sentence states that Anna's age is 9 years more than half of Annie's age. By translating into a number problem, this is the same as saying 30 - x is equal to 9 plus half of x.

Then, we solve for x.

We then arrive at x = 14. Since Annie's age is x, this means that Annie is 14 years old.

Next, since Anna's age is represented by 30 - x, we substitute 14 for x and we find out that Anna is 16 years old.

We can check this: the sum of their ages, 14 + 16, is indeed 30. Then, provided by Annie's age 14, Anna's age is nine years plus half of 14. Half of 14 is 7, and when 9 is added to 7, we get 16.

We further conclude that Annie is 14 years old and Anna is 16 years old

11. Describe a dwarf, giant and super giant star​


Answer:

Dwarf star, any star of average or low luminosity, mass, and size. Important subclasses of dwarf stars are white dwarfs and red dwarfs. Dwarf stars include so-called main-sequence stars, among which is the Sun.

Giant star, any star having a relatively large radius for its mass and temperature; because the radiating area is correspondingly large, the brightness of such stars is high. 

Meanwhile, Super Giant Stars are relatively larger and more massive than giant stars. They are greater in terms of brightness and mass.

∼(p ∧ q) ∧ r = Annie does not have a stomach ache and does not miss the exam and receives a passing grade for the subject.p ∧ q →  ∼r = If Annie has a stomach ache and misses the exam, then Annie does not receive a passing grade for the subject.p → q = If Annie has a stomach ache, then Annie misses the exam.q → r = If Annie misses the exam, Annie receives a passing grade for the subject.∼r → ∼q = If Annie does not receive a passing grade for the subject, then Annie did not miss the exam.
