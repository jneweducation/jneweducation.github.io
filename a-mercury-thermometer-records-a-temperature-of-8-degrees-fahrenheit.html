13. A mercury thermometer records a temperature of 8 degrees Fahrenheit at 10 A.M. if the temperature drops by 3 degrees Fahrenheit every hour, what will be the temperature by 2 P.M of the same day?​


The temperature by 2 P.M of the same day : -4°F

Further explanation  

Temperature is used as a guide to the level of heat energy from objects  

Temperature measures thermal energy  

Temperature units include Celsius, Fahrenheit, Reaumur or Kelvin  

We can calculate it in two ways

1. manual way

2. with the arithmetic series formula

1. manual

initial temperature = 8

Temperature drops by 3/hr

From 10 a.m to 2 pm , there is a difference of 4 hours

Then the final temperature

[tex]\tt =8^o-(4~h\times 3^o/h)\\\\=8^o-12\\\\=-4^oF[/tex]

2.  the arithmetic series

The arrangement of the numbers is like this:  

a, a+d, a+2d, etc ,

so the temperature series :

8°F, 5°F, 2°F,...

a = initial term  ⇒ initial temperature = 8° F

d = different (common difference)  = -3°/hr

The formula for the nth term ⇒2 o'clock is the 5th hour(from 10 AM), so n = 5

[tex]\tt xn=8+(5-1).-3\\\\xn=8-12=\boxed{\bold{-4^oF}}[/tex]

15. a mercury thermometer records 8 degrees fahrenheit at 10am if the temperature drops 3 degrees fahrenheit every hour what will be the temperature by 2pm of the same day??​


The temperature by 2 pm of the same day : -4°F

Further explanation  

An arithmetic sequence is an arrangement of numbers that has the same difference for consecutive pairs of numbers or it can be said that "the difference between one term and the next is a constant. (adding the same number to the next number) "  

The arrangement of the numbers is like this:  

a, a+d, a+2d, etc

a = initial term  

d = different (common difference)  

The formula for the nth term:  

xn= a+d(n-1)

a=8°F

n=5(from 10 am to 2 pm, every hour)

d=-3°(temperature drops)

[tex]\tt x_5=8^oF+(5-1).-3^oF\\\\x_5=8+(-12)\\\\X_5=\boxed{\bold{-4^oF}}[/tex]

Answer:The temperature will be -4 °F at 2 PM.Step-by-step explanation:

This problem is an example of Arithmetic Sequence.

Given:

[tex]x1 = 8[/tex]

[tex]d = -3[/tex]

[tex]n = \frac{2 PM - 9 AM}{1hr} = 5[/tex]

[tex]x5 = ?[/tex]

Formula:

[tex]xn = x1 + (n - 1)d[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]x5 = 8 + (5 - 1)( - 3)[/tex]

↓

[tex]x5 = 8 + 4( - 3)[/tex]

↓

[tex]x5 = 8 - 12[/tex]

↓

[tex]x5 = - 4[/tex]

The temperature will be -4 °F at 2 PM.
