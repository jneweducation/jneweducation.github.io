7. Add the following (15), (-12), (18), (-20) and (16)

SOLUTION:

[tex] \tt (15) + ( - 12) + (18) + ( - 20) + (16) \\ \\ \tt = 15 - 12 + 18 - 20 + 16 \\ \\ \tt = 3 + 18 - 20 + 16 \\ \\ \tt = 21 - 20 + 16 \\ \\ \tt = 1 + 16 \\ \\ = \red{ \boxed{ \tt 17}}[/tex]

8. Give the sum of the following integers. (3), (-4), (5), (-6), (7) and (-8).

SOLUTION:

[tex] \tt(3) + ( - 4) +(5) + ( - 6) + (7) + ( - 8) \\ \\ \tt = 3 - 4 + 5 - 6 + 7 - 8 \\ \\ \tt = - 1 + 5 - 6 + 7 - 8 \\ \\ \tt = 4 - 6 + 7 - 8 \\ \\ \tt = - 2 + 7 - 8 \\ \\ \tt = 5 - 8 \\ \\ = \red{ \boxed{\tt - 3}}[/tex]

⚠️Remember:⚠️

☑️ When adding integers with the same signs, just add them and copy the sign.

☑️ When adding integers with different signs, subtract them and copy the sign of the bigger number.
