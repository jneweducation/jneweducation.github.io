13. Which is true to Gender Stereotypes?


Answer: Boys are expected to excel in sports, while girls are expected to do better academically.

Explanation: The gender stereotypes we learned in grade school often remain strong throughout our lives. Many people believe that boys and men are better athletes than women and girls are better thinkers. As adults, these beliefs tend to influence how we view ourselves and those around us. We may find that our parents, friends, relatives, and coworkers view us differently depending on our gender. Some people may feel pressure to conform to society’s expectations; others may feel frustrated by them. Regardless of which attitude you hold, you should strive to use your knowledge of gender stereotypes to help you grow into a person who is comfortable with who she is, and who values her strengths without becoming overly influenced by societal views.
