Answer:

MOLES TO MASS

Calculate the molar mass of CO₂

Molar Mass of C = 12 g/mol

12 • 1 = 12 g

Molar Mass of O = 16 g/mol

16 • 2 = 32 g

Molar Mass of CO₂ = molar mass of C + molar mass of O₂

Molar Mass of CO₂ = 12 g + 32 g

Molar Mass of CO₂ = 44 g/mol

Convert the moles to mass

Molar Mass of CO₂ = 44 g/mol

(2.5 mol CO₂)(44 g CO₂ / 1 mol CO₂ )
