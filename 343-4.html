15. factor of completely n3+343​


Problem

Factor of completely n³+343

Answer(n + 7) (n² - 7n + 49)

Solution:

(n + 7) (n² - 7n + 49)

= n(n²) + 7(n²) + n(-7n) + 7(-7n) + n(49) + 7(49)

= n³ + 7n² - 7n² - 49n + 49n + 343

= n³ + 343 ✔
