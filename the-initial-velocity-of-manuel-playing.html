29. the initial velocity of manuel playing luksong tinik has horizontal and vertical component that are equal in magnitude. What angle does his velocity make with the horizontal?a.30Â°b.45Â°c.60Â°d.90Â°


The answer is b which is 45°. Velocity is the rate of speed of Manuel while playing luksong tinik. Playing this game needs a speed forward (horizontal component) and jumping at some point (vertical component). 

Vertical component of a velocity can be expressed in N*sin Ф, while horizontal component is N*cos Ф. Where:

N = absolute value of the velocity
sin Ф = sine function with angle Ф
cos Ф = cosine function with angle Ф

Given from the problem, the vertical and horizontal component of Manuel's velocity/speed are equal. So:

N*sin Ф = N*cos Ф   >>> divide N both sides of the equation
sin Ф = cos Ф  >>> to find the angle just substitute the given choices

If you tried 45°, it will give you sin 45° = cos 45° = 1
So the answer is 45°.
