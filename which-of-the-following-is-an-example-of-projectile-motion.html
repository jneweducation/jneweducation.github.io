The following is not associated with projectile motion:

1. Initial velocity

2. Acceleration due to gravity

3. Atmospheric pressure

4. Angle of projection

The correct answer is atmospheric pressure. Atmospheric pressure is not associated with projectile motion, as it does not affect the motion of a projectile in any significant way. Projectile motion is the motion of an object that is thrown or launched into the air and then moves under the influence of gravity alone. The key factors that affect projectile motion are the initial velocity, acceleration due to gravity, and the angle of projection.
