18. What is the factor of the polynomial 36v^2+24v


Answer:

6v + 4

Step - by - step solution:

Given: 36v^2+24v

Ask: FACTOR

Solution:

The given 36v^2 and 24 v are both divisible by 6v, so let's divide 36v^2+24v by 6v:

= 36v^2+24v

     6v

= 6v + 4 (The final answer)

To check whether the answer is correct let's multiply 6v by (6v + 4)

= 6v (6v + 4)

= 36v^2+24v
