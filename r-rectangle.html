29. ASSIGNMENT Supply the missing statements or reasons to prove that the diagonals of a rectangle are congruent. R E M Given: Rectangle ROME With diagonals OE and RM Prove: OERM​


Answer:

Here is the completed proof:

Statements Reasons

Rectangle ROME | Given

Diagonals OE and RM intersect at point P | Definition of diagonals

OP = PE | Definition of midpoint

OR = RM | Definition of rectangle

OP + OR = OE | Segment addition postulate

PE + RM = OE | Substitution (from 3 and 4)

PE + OR = OE | Substitution (from 3 and 5)

Triangle OPE is congruent to triangle ORM | SSS congruence (using 3, 4, and 6)

OE = RM | CPCTC (corresponding parts of congruent triangles are congruent)

Diagonals OE and RM are congruent | Definition of congruent segments

OERM is a rectangle | Definition of rectangle diagonals

Therefore, we have proven that the diagonals of a rectangle are congruent.
