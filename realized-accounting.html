14. ACCOUNTING: I HAVE THE ANSWER & SOLUTION, I JUST NEED SHORT EXPLANATION (1 PARAGRAPH) REGARDING THE SOLUTION. 38. The net realizable value of accounts receivable as of December 31, 2015 is a. P15,367 c. P143,700 b. P140,700 d. P114,568 Answer: b Solution: Accounts receivable, 12/31/2015 P150,000 Less: Allowance for doubtful accounts (9,000) Allowance for cash discounts (300) Net realizable value P140,700 Explanation: ?


Answer:

The net realizable value of accounts receivable is the estimated amount that a company will be able to collect from its customers after deducting any uncollectible accounts and cash discounts. In this problem, the net realizable value is computed by subtracting the allowance for doubtful accounts and the allowance for cash discounts from the accounts receivable balance as of December 31, 2015. The resulting amount of P140,700 represents the estimated net amount that the company is expected to collect from its customers as of the end of the accounting period. This figure is an important indicator of a company's financial health and liquidity, as it reflects the company's ability to collect its outstanding accounts receivable in a timely and efficient manner.


15. how much must be invested today in saving account to realize Php 9.000.00 in 4 years if money earns at the rate of 4% compound quarterly​


Answer:

Php7,675.39

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula to be used to find the pricipal amount or amount to be invested in compounding interest.

[tex]P = \frac{F}{(1 + \frac{r}{n} )^{nt} }[/tex]

where:

P is the principal amountF is the future or maturity valuer is the interest ratet is time in yearsn is number of times the interest would be compounded each year

Solution:

[tex]P = \frac{F}{(1 + \frac{r}{n} )^{nt} } \\ \\ P = \frac{9000}{(1 + \frac{0.04}{4} )^{(4)(4)} } \\ \\ P = \frac{9000}{(1 + 0.01 )^{(16)} } \\ \\ P = \frac{9000}{(1.01 )^{(16)} } \\ \\ P = \frac{9000}{1.1725786449} \\ \\ P =7,675.391360012 \: or \: 7,675.39[/tex]
