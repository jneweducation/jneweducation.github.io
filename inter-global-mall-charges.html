In mathematics, solving word problem required knowledge and skills in basic mathematics that will improve your analytical and critical thinking skills.

In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math specially in functions.

Given:30 pesos for the first hour or a fraction na hour for the parking fee15 pesos is charge for every additional hour7am to 12 midnight everyday

Solution:

In this case, the problem represent as function of charge fee of parking in terms of number of hours.

let x is the number of hours

F(x) = 30 + 15(x-1)

F(x) = 30 + 15x - 15

F(x) = 15x + 15

Find x

Number of hours between 7 am to 12 am.

12:00 am → 24:00

24 - 7 = 17 hours

Solve for Total Charge Everyday

F(x) = 15x + 15, x = 17

F(x) = 15(17) + 15

F(x) = 255 + 15

F(x) = 270

Therefore, in everyday, Inter Global Mall will charges 270 pesos.
