30. a ladder 8 meters long lean against the wall of a building. If the foot of the laader makes an angle og 65° with the ground, how far is the base of the ladder from the wall? ​


[tex]\bold{PROBLEM:}[/tex]

A ladder 8 meters long lean against the wall of a building. If the foot of the ladder makes an angle of 65° with the ground, how far is the base of the ladder from the wall?

[tex]\bold{GIVEN:}[/tex]

length of the ladder (hypotenuse) = 8 metersangle of elevation = 65°

[tex]\bold{UNKNOWN:}[/tex]

distance of the ladder from the base of the wall (adjacent)

[tex]\bold{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

Given the hypotenuse and the adjacent side as the unknown, we will use the ratio of cosine.

[tex]\qquad\qquad\tt cos\theta= \frac{adjacent}{hypotenuse} \\ \\ \qquad\qquad\tt cos \: 65= \frac{d}{8} \\ \\ \qquad\qquad\tt (8)(cos \: 65)=\bigg(\frac{d}{\cancel{8}}\bigg)(\cancel{8}) \\ \\ \qquad\qquad\tt d=(8)(cos \: 65) \\ \\ \qquad\qquad\red{\boxed{\tt d=3.38 \: m}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\small\therefore\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\textsf{The base of the ladder is 3.38 meters}\\\small\textsf{far from the wall.}\end{array}}[/tex]

[tex]\tiny\textsf{Note that the answer is rounded off to the nearest hundredths}[/tex]
