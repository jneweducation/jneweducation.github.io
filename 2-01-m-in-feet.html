24. a toad stretched its .01 kg tounge to catch its fly at rate of 4 m/s in 2 seconds. what was the force the toad exerted on its tounge to speedily catch its prey.​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The force acting on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F = ma}[/tex]

where:

F = force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the acceleration

[tex]a = \dfrac{v_{f} - v_{i}}{t}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{4 m/s} - 0}{\text{2 s}}[/tex]

a = 2 m/s²

Solving for the force

F = ma

F = (0.01 kg)(2 m/s²)

[tex]\boxed{F = \text{0.02 N}}[/tex]

Therefore, the force exerted is 0.02 N.
