26. WHAT IS THE LEAST COMMON MULTIPLE (LCM) OF THE FOLLOWING?? 72 472 960 220 828


Answer:

LCM(72, 472, 960, 220, 828) = 42989760

Step-by-step explanation:

72 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3

472 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 59

960 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 5

220 = 2 × 2 × 5 ×  11

828 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 23

LCM(72, 472, 960, 220, 828)

= 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 5 × 11 × 23 × 59

= 42989760

Note:

So basically, ang gawin mo lang is to do prime factorization (Find all possible factors of a number. Halimbawa:

6=2x3

Afterwards, isulat mo yung mga factors na yun. Kung may repeating na number, isulat mo lang yung factors na mas marami kaysa sa factors ng ibang number. Halimbawa:

472 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 59

960 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 5

Mas marami ang 2s sa 960 dahil may tatlong 2s ang 472 ngunit may anim na 2s ang 960 kaya ito ang gamitin mo sa final solution mo.

Pagkatapos, imultiply mo yung lahat na factor na nakuha mo at iyon na ang final solution mo.
