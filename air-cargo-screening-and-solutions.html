speed = distance / time

[tex]30 = \frac{500}{t} \\ \: 30t = 500 \\ \frac{30t}{30} = \frac{500}{30} \\ t = 16.67 \: hour[/tex]

Get now the speed of cargo plane.

s= 440/16.67 = 26.39 kph

29. Answer the following problem solving. Use GUFSA: Given, Unknown (missing), Formula, Solution and Answer to get the answers. 1. A jet plane fly horizontally 1500 m above the ground at 200 km/h drops a cargo at a target on the ground. Determine where the cargo will strike the ground. ​


Answer:

3500m

Explanation:

Given: dy = 1500, vx = 200

Unknown: t & dx
Formula: [tex]dx = vx t[/tex] & [tex]t = \sqrt{\frac{2dy}{g} }[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]t = \sqrt{\frac{2(1500m)}{9.8m/s^{2} } }[/tex]  [tex]= \sqrt{\frac{3000m}{9.8m/s^{2} } }[/tex]   [tex]= \sqrt{306.12}[/tex]  [tex]= 17.5s[/tex]

[tex]dx = (200m/s)(17.5s)[/tex]    [tex]= 3,500m[/tex]

Answer:  3,500m
