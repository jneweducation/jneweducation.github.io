truck time = 350/s

car time = 350/(s+20)

Truck time - car time = 2 hrs so,

[350(s+20) - 350s]/s(s+20) = 2

then simplify more to become quadratic equation

2s^2 + 40s - 7000 = 0

to make it simplest divide the equation by 2

s^2 + 20s - 3500 = 0

by factoring we can get

s= 50 s=-70 (impossible because of the sign)
