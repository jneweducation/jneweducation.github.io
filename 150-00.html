4. Luis earns a base salary of \$150.00$150.00dollar sign, 150, point, 00 every week with an additional 14\%14%14, percent commission on everything he sells. Luis sold \$6050.00$6050.00dollar sign, 6050, point, 00 worth of items last week.


Answer:

$997

Step-by-step explanation:

The total amount that Luis earned from last week's sales is equal to the sum of the base pay and the commission. This is expressed in the equation,

   T = B + C

where T is total pay, B is the base pay, and C is the commission. Commission is known to be 14% of A which is the amount sold.

   T = B + 0.14A

Substituting the known values,

T = ($150) + 0.14($6050)

T = $995

The answer to this item is $997.
