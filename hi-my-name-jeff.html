9. Jeff is 10 years old and his brother is 2 years old. In how many years will Jeff be twice as old as his brother?


Present ages:

Jeff: 10 years old

His brother: 2 years old

Let the number of years in the future be x.  In x years, Jeff will be twice as old as his brother.

10 + x = 2(2 + x)    ⇒  Equation

10 + x = 4 + 2x

2x - x = 10 - 4

x = 6

ANSWER: In 6 years, Jeff will be twice as old as brother.

Check:

Age in 6 years:

Jeff: 10 + 6 = 16 years old

Brother: 2 + 6 = 8 years old

16 = 2(8)

16 = 16  (True)

23. What is the theme of my floor is somebody ceiling by jeff moss


Answer:

The theme of "My Floor is Someone's Ceiling" by Jeff Moss is the interconnectedness of human experiences and the idea that everyone's perspective is unique and valid. Through the poem, the speaker acknowledges that their floor is someone else's ceiling, suggesting that every individual has a different vantage point and perception of the world. The poem encourages empathy and understanding of others' experiences, even if they differ from our own. It suggests that there is value in recognizing and appreciating different viewpoints, and that this can lead to a more compassionate and inclusive society. Overall, the theme of the poem emphasizes the importance of empathy, interconnectedness, and perspective-taking.
