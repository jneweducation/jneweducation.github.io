Answer:

Explanation:

The rhythm of the music composition entitled Ili-ili Tulog Anay is in slow tempo. The rhythm is slow first because the music is a lullaby song, typically sung slowly (or may be sung as well in moderate tempo), and second because each end of the line prolonged the syllable as shown below:

Ili-ili __________

Tulog anay __________

Wala diri __________

Imong nanay __________

Kadto tienda __________

Bakal papay __________

Ili-ili __________

Tulog anay __________

While, it is not considered as fast tempo because if you sung it in fast manner, the nature of being a lullaby or cradle song will be vanished, secondly, it is not appropriate to sung it in fast tempo.
