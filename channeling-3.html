22. Water is flowing in a trapezoidal channel at a rate of q = 20 m 3 /s. the critical depth y for such a channel must satisfy the equation given below:


Answer:

The critical depth of a channel refers to the depth at which the flow in the channel becomes supercritical, meaning that the flow velocity exceeds the speed of gravity waves. The critical depth is an important parameter in the design and analysis of open-channel flow systems.

To determine the critical depth of a channel, you can use the following equation:

y = (q^2 / (g * (b1 + b2)))^(1/3)

Where:

y is the critical depth in meters

q is the flow rate in cubic meters per second

g is the acceleration due to gravity in meters per second squared

b1 is the width of the channel at the bottom in meters

b2 is the width of the channel at the top in meters

This equation assumes that the channel has a trapezoidal shape with a horizontal bottom and vertical sides, and that the flow is uniform and steady.

To use this equation to determine the critical depth for your given flow rate and channel dimensions, you will need to know the values of q, g, b1, and b2. Once you have these values, you can plug them into the equation and solve for y.

30. A trainee statistician reported to his supervisor the following data:17 persons liked channel A20 persons liked channel B14 persons liked channel C8 persons liked channels A & B7 persons liked channels A & C6 persons liked channels B & C2 persons liked all 3 channels2 persons liked none of the 3 channelsWhen the trainee statistician reported to have interviewed 36 persons, he was relieved from his postExplain why and Put how you solved it​


Answer:

There were only 34 persons or respondents.

Step-by-step explanation:

Liked all channels: 2

Liked channels A & B: 8

Liked channels A & B only:  (8-2) = 6

Liked channels A & C: 7

Liked channels A & C only: (7-2)  = 5

Liked channels B & C: 6

Liked channels B & C only: (6-2) = 4

Liked channel A: 17

Liked channel A only: [17 - (2+6+5] = [17 - 13] = 4

Liked channel B: 20

Liked channel B only: [20 - (2+6+4)] = [20 - 12] = 8

Liked channel C: 14

Liked channel C only: [14 - (2+5+4)] = [14 - 11] = 3

Liked none of the channels: 2

TOTAL RESPONDENTS = (Liked all channels) + (A+B only) + (A+C only) + (B+C only) + (A+B+C only) + (Liked none of the channels)  

Total respondents = (2) + (6) + (5) + (4) + (4+8+3) + (2)

Total respondents = 34

The total respondents or persons the statistician have interviewed were 34, not 36.
