30. It is used excessively in the modern world, and it is widely used in cooking equipment and in food packaging industry.


Answer:

Aluminum

Explanation:

Aluminum has been extensively used as material for creating cooking equipments due to the fact that aluminum won't react to the food. Aluminum is a reactive element, but when used as a cooking utensil, it creates an Aluminum Oxide layer to prevent aluminum from leaching out into the food, making it safe to it. It also being used in packaging industry in the form of aluminum foil.
