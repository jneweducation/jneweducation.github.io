Dividing both sides by 15:

P = 15

Therefore, the equilibrium price is P = 15.

To check for QD and QS at this equilibrium price, we can substitute P = 15 into the original equations:

QD = 150 - 10P = 150 - 10(15) = 0

QS = -75 + 5P = -75 + 5(15) = 0

Demand at Supply

Upang maunawaan ang ekwilibriyong pamilihan, kailangan nating magsimula sa mga batas ng demand at supply. Alalahanin na ang batas ng demand ay nagsasabi na habang bumababa ang presyo, ang mga consumer ay humihiling ng mas mataas na dami. Katulad nito, sinasabi ng batas ng supply na kapag bumaba ang presyo, ang mga producer ay nagsusuplay ng mas mababang dami.

Equilibrium: Kung saan Magsalubong ang Supply at Demand

Kapag nagkrus ang dalawang linya sa isang diagram, karaniwang may ibig sabihin ang intersection na ito. Sa isang graph, ang punto kung saan nag-intersect ang supply curve (S) at ang demand curve (D) ay ang equilibrium. Ang presyong ekwilibriyo ay ang tanging presyo kung saan nagkakasundo ang mga kagustuhan ng mga mamimili at ang mga hangarin ng mga prodyuser—iyon ay, kung saan ang halaga ng produkto na gustong bilhin ng mga mamimili (quantity demanded) ay katumbas ng halagang gustong ibenta ng mga prodyuser (quantity supplied) . Ang halagang ito na magkaparehong ninanais ay tinatawag na equilibrium quantity.

What is quantity demanded?

The quantity desired can be thought of as the consumers' desire to purchase a particular quantity of goods and services. It also depends on the prices advertised for the goods and services in the market and is independent of the state of the market equilibrium.

The demand curve illustrates the relationship between the quantity desired and the price at which it is offered. Changes in the quantity levels in relation to the price are described by the elasticity of demand. The seller of the goods and services must make sure that he sets a competitive and profitable price in order to stimulate demand.

The seller should provide a variety of goods and services to pique the buyer's interest and encourage them to take action and make a purchase. The price of goods and services is inversely correlated with the amount desired. A decrease in price for goods and services corresponds to a rise in demand, and vice versa.
