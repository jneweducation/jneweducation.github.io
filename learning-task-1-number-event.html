The reproductive organs are divided into two structures, namely the external and internal structures. The external structures consist of the l4bia majora, l4bia minora, Bartholin's gl4nds, and cl1toris. The main function of the female reproductive organs is to produce eggs and fertilize. The ovaries (ovaries) produce eggs (oocytes).
