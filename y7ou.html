20. you are along 55 miles per hour when yiou are passed by a car doing 85 miles per hour. how long will it take for the car that passed you to be 1 mile ahead of you?


Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Time = Distance ÷ Speed

Time = 1 mile ÷ (85-55) mile/hr

Time = 1 mile /30 mile/hr    

Time = 1 hour/30 = 0.03333 hrs       ⇒  1hr = 60 minutes

Time = 60 minutes / 30

Time = 2 minutes    

It will take 2 minutes for the car that passed you to be 1 mile  ahead
