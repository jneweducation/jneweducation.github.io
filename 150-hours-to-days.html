2. 120 men working 9 hours a day can construct a road in 40 days.how long would it take to construct the same road if 150 men work 6 hours a day on it? ​


Answer:

40 days..

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]40x \frac{9}{6} x \frac{120}{150} [/tex]

[tex] = 40 \: days \: [/tex]

Answer:

This is a question from Ratio and Proportion based concept.

Given that,

120 men working for 9 hours a day, construct a road in 40 days. We are required to calculate the number of days to construct the road if 150 people work for 6 hours.

Let's assume the number of days taken for constructing is "x".

So normally we take 2 ratios and obtain the unknown value by cross multiplication.

Applying the same approach we get:

[tex]\implies \frac{ 120\:men}{150\:men}=\frac{9\:hours}{6\:hours}=\frac{40\:days}{x\:days}[/tex]

Since the first two fractions are inversely related, we multiply the numerators and denominators respectively.

Hence we get:

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\implies \dfrac{ 120\: men \times 9\: hours}{150\: men \times 6\: hours} = \dfrac{ 40\: days}{x\: days}\\\\\\\implies \dfrac{1080}{900} = \dfrac{40}{x}\\\\\\\sf{Cross~ multiplying ~these~ 2 ~fractions~ we~ get:}\\\\\\\implies 1080 \times x = 40 \times 900 \\\\\\\implies x = \dfrac{ 40 \times 900}{1080}\\\\\\\implies x = \dfrac{36000}{1080}\\\\\\\implies x = \boxed{ 33.33 \:\: days}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

Hence it takes 33.33 days for 150 men to construct the road working for 6 hours each day.
