3. The Library Publishing house want to know the ISBN check digit of Math Power book with the ISBN: 978-971-0499-31-?


ANSWER:

Check digit: 3

ISBN: 978-971-0499-3

Multiply each number in turn, from left to right by a number. The first digit is multiplied by 1, the second by 3, the third by 1 gain, the fourth by 3 again, and so on to the eleventh which is multiplied by 1 and the twelfth by 3.

1*9 = 9

3*7 = 21

1*8 = 8

3*9 = 27

1*7 = 7

3*1 = 3

1*0 = 0

3*4 = 12

1*9 = 9

3*9 = 27

1*9 = 9

3*3 =9

1*1 = 1

After that, add all of the results.

9+21+8+27+7+3+0+12+9+27+9+9+1

Total: 137

Now do a modulo 10 division from your result, if the remainder is 0, 0 is your check digit, if it's not, subtract your remainder from 10 and the answer would be youe check digit.

137 modulo 10 = 130 remainder 7

Check Digit

10-7 = 3

Your ISBN is:

978-971-0499-3
