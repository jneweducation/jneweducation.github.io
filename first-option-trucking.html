13. PROBLEM SOLVING1. Consider a 28,000 kg truck travelling at 8 m/s towards another stationary truck. After the two truck collide, they couple together and move along at 5 m/s. What is the mass of the second truck?2. A Blue Truck and Green Truck are identical truck that are on a flat, horizontal smooth surface. The blue truck is motionless, while the green truck is moving 3.5m/s to the left and collides to blue truck. the toy trucks have a mass of 25g. After the collision, the blue truck is moving at 3m/s to the left. What is the final velocity of the green truck?================================NONSENSE/INCOMPLETE = REPORTCORRECT ANSWER = BRAINLEIST================================​


1.) Given:

m1 = 28,000 kgs.

v1 = 8 m/s

v2 = 0 m/s (stationary)

Vt = 5 m/s

Solution:

[tex] (mv)_{1} + (mv)_{2} = (mv)_{t} [/tex]

[tex](28000 \times 8) + ( m_{2} \times 0) = (28000 + m_{2})(5) [/tex]

[tex]224000 + 0 = 140000 + 5 m_{2}[/tex]

[tex] m_{2} = \frac{224000 - 140000}{5} [/tex]

[tex] m_{2} = 16800 \: kgs.[/tex]

2.) Given:

m1 & m2 = 25 kgs.

v1 = 0 m/s (motionless)

v2 = 3.5 m/s (to the left)

vt1 = 3 m/s (to the left)

Solution:

[tex] (mv)_{1} + (mv)_{2} = (mv)_{t1} + (mv)_{t2}[/tex]

[tex](25 \times 0) + (25)( - 3.5) = (25)( - 3) + (25) (v)_{t2}[/tex]

[tex]0 - 87.5 = - 75 + 25 v_{t2}[/tex]

[tex] v_{t2} = \frac{ - 87.5 + 75}{25} [/tex]

[tex] v_{t2} = - 0.5 \: \frac{m}{s} \: [/tex]

or

[tex] v_{t2} = 0.5 \: \frac{m}{s} (to \: the \: left)[/tex]

19. a truck transports 800 kg fruits at the first time ,950kg at the second time and 1100 kg at the third time . assuming the sequence continues ,how many fruits does the truck transport at the sixth time ?​


Answer:

2000 fruits at the sixth time

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the common difference of the sequence subtract the given term

second term subtracted from the third term

1100-800= 300 (common difference)

Since the first term is not given, the common difference be subtracted from the second term:

1st term 800-300 = 500

To find the following term just add the common difference to previous term:

2nd term 500 + 300= 8003rd term 800+300= 11004th term 1100 + 300 = 14005th term 1400 + 300 = 17006th term 1700 + 300 = 2000

Therefore the 6th term is 2000
