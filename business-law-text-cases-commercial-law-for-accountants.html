EFFECT OF CONVERSION OF FOREST TO COMMERCIAL AND AGRICULTURAL BUSINESS

Cattle ranching, animal agriculture, and logging are the leading causes of deforestation in our forests. ... This constant destruction of our forests threatens biodiversity, decreases carbon absorption, magnifies natural disaster damage, and disrupts water cycles

11. The following are solid wastes covered by the Philippine law on solid wastes management except A.discarded chemical containers B.street sweepings C.discarded household commercial wastes D.agricultural wastes E.construction debris


Answer:

Letter D

Explanation:

Agricultural wastes are not covered by the Philippine law on solid waste management. The law covers discarded chemical containers, street sweepings, discarded household commercial wastes and construction debris as solid wastes. Agricultural wastes are considered as different type of waste and are typically managed through different regulations.
