28. Rover weighs less than Fido Rover weighs more than Boomer Of the three dogs Boomer weighs the least. If the first two statements are true the third statement is?


Answer:

True

Explanation:

Let Rover's weight be R

Let Fido's weight be F

Let Boomer's weight be B

Rover weighs less than Fido

Rover's weight < Fido's weight

R < F

Rover weighs more than Boomer

Rover's weight > Boomer's weight

R > B

Rover weighs more than Boomer but lesser than Fido

Boomer's weight < Rover's weight < Fido's weight

B < R < F

If (R < F) and (R > B) = True, Then, Fido weighs the most, Rover is in between, and Boomer weighs the least.

Therefore, the third statement is true.
