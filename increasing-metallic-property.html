Question:

which of the following statement about metallic property is true?

Choices:

a. the metallic property decrease from top to bottom of a group

b. metallic property increase across a period

c. metallic property increase decrease the metallic property decreases across a period

Answer:

c. metallic property increase decrease the metallic property decreases across a period

Explanation:

Metallic character decrease as you move across a period in the periodic table from left to right. This occurs as atoms more readily accept electrons to fill a valence shell than lose them to remove the unfilled shell. Metallic character increases as you move down an element group in the periodic table


2. increasing metallic properties​


Answer:

The metallic character increases as you go down a group. Since the ionization energy decreases going down a group (or increases going up a group), the increased ability for metals lower in a group to lose electrons makes them more reactive.

10. is metallic properties increased from left to right. explain?​


Answer:

left to right

Metallic character goes on decreasing while going from left to right in a period because the tendency of atoms of the elements to lose electrons (or gain electrons) changes in a period. As we move from left to right in a period, the nuclear charge increases due to a gradual increase in the number of protons.
