1. What is the difference between the endpoint and equivalence point in a titration?


Answer:

Equivalence point represents the stage of titration where the concentrations of titrate and titrant are chemically equivalent. While an endpoint represents the stage of titration that indicates the completion of the titration with the help of the change in colour or intensity of the solution.
