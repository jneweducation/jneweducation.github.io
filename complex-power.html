26. Can any of our "essential" human qualities be taken from us? For example, only human beings can communicate with complex language; are you human if you lose the power of speech?​


Answer:

No, human qualities cannot be taken from us.

Answer:

Yes, one is still human if they lose the power of speech. They are also still human if they lose the ability to read and write along with the loss of speech. They are also still human if they have a difficult time understanding speech when spoken to. This is a common condition called Aphasia. It happens when there is damage to the language area of the brain from a stroke, traumatic brain injury or brain tumor. People who are stricken with Aphasia retain their intellect. Anyone who knows someone with Aphasia of any severity can tell you they are human. They still have complex thoughts, emotions and consciousness. Life can be quite a struggle when you have a difficult time communicating. Aphasia is incredibly isolating. Friends and even many family members tend to just fade away, feeling awkward when they cannot have a regular conversation with the survivor any more. It so very worth it to hang in there and learn how to interact with the person with aphasia.
