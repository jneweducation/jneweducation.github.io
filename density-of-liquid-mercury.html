23. A 1-liter of Liquid X and 2 liters of Liquid Y are mixed together to produce a resulting mixture having a resulting density of 900 kg/m3. If liquid X has a density of 800 kg/m3, determine the density of liquid Y​


Answer:

ρ2 = 950 kg/m3

Explanation:

From the mixture formula:

ρ1V1 + ρ2V2 = ρV

ρ1 = 800 kg/m3

V1 = 1 L = 0.001 m3

ρ2 = ?

V2 = 2 L = 0.002 m3

ρ = 900 kg/m3

V = V1 + V2 = 3 L = 0.003 m3

substituting the given values:

(800)(0.001) + ρ2(0.002) = (900)(0.003)

ρ2 = 950 kg/m3
