Question

1. In proportion p/q = 7/4 , what are the extremes?

ANSWER:  

C. p and 4

SOLUTION:

p/q = 7/4 can be re-written as, p:q = 7:4

And in the proportion,

p : q  =  7 : 4

^--------------^

  Extremes

p and 4 are the outer terms and they are called the extremes

while

q and 7 are the inner terms and they are called the means.
