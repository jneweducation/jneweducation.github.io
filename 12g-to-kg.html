9. A. Subtract as indicated: 4. (15g3 - 6g + 8) - (7g² +12g-8) ​


To subtract the two polynomials, we need to perform the subtraction operation for each corresponding term:

15g³ - 6g + 8 - 7g² - 12g + 8

Simplifying by combining like terms:

15g³ - 7g² - 6g - 12g + 8 + 8

15g³ - 7g² - 18g + 16

Therefore, the simplified result of the subtraction 4. (15g3 - 6g + 8) - (7g² +12g-8) is 15g³ - 7g² - 18g + 16.

28. (+4g-16g+8g-12g+20g)divided by (4g)


Answer:

the answer here is g

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Add the positive numbers first  (4g,8g, and 20g) and keep their common variable

4g+8g+20g = 32g

2. Once you find the sum, add the negative integers (-16g,-12g) and keep their common variable

therefore, -16g + (-12g) is -28

3. Now add both of them

32g + (-28g) = 4g

4g / 4g = 1g or g
