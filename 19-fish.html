18. 2.) An adult sambagon tuna fish can weigh up to 19 ___.A.mgB.gC.kgD.t​


Answer:

kg (C)

Step-by-step explanation:

Observe that:

In option A, "mg" (milligrams) is used for measuring small doses of minerals and vitamins in medicine which is too small.

In option B, "g" (grams) is used for measuring the weight of food products like chips and snacks which is still quite small.

In option D, "T" (tonnes) is used for measuring load on heavy vehicles like trucks, vans, and cargo ships which is very big.

Thus, the most acceptable weight of an adult sambagon tuna fish is 19 kg which is option C.
