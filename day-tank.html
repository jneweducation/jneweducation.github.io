21. A tank contains a certain amount of water. At the end of each day, one-fourth of the water in the tank is removed and is not replaced. If 4608 liters of water remained at the end of the third day, how many liters of water did the tank contain at the end of the first day?


Answer:

There are 13824 liters of water left at the end of the first day.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we need to know the maximum amount of water of the tank.

We will use multiplication to solve the first problem.

4608 liters of water (There is only 1/4 of water in the tank left) * 4 (The amount needed to make the tank full) = 18432 liters of water

Finally, we can finally solve the given inquiry.

We will use subtraction to solve the last problem.

18432 liters of water (The maximum capacity of the tank) - 4608 liters of water (The 1/4 that is removed at the end of the day) = 13824 liters of water

Therefore, I can conclude that the amount of water left at the end of the first day is 13,824 liters.

30. One tank of goldfish is fed the normal amount of food once a day, a second tank is fed twice a day, and a third tank four times a day during a 8 weeks study. the fishes' body fat is recorded daily. what is the dependent variable in this situation


DEPENDENT VARIABLE

★ Problem :-

One tank of goldfish is fed the normal amount of food once a day, a second tank is fed twice a day, and a third tank four times a day during a 8 weeks study. the fishes' body fat is recorded daily. What is the dependent variable in this situation?

★ Asked :-

What is the dependent variable in the situation?

★ Given :-

foods,tank,fish,weeks and finally the fishe's body fat

★ To find :-

In order to find the dependent variable we should know what is the meaning of dependent variable. Dependent variable means the variable that is affected by the changes conducted by independent variable.

★ Final answer :-

The dependent variable in the situation is fishe's body fat or the fishes since they're the ones that is affected by changes brought by independent variable like the amount of food and the weeks.
