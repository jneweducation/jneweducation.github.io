3. During the year, the equity of Krystel Company increased by ₱30 000 while itsliabilities decreased by ₱10 000. How did Krystel Company’s asset change duringthe year?a. Increase of ₱40 000b. Increase of ₱20 000c. Decrease of ₱40 000d. Decrease of ₱20 000


Subject Economics

Answer:

The correct answer among the given listed choices is letter b. Understanding the accounting equation is important to easily demonstrate on how to arrive at the correct answer which is Increase of  ₱20,000.

Accounting Equation

The accounting equation is also called the balance sheet equation because it represents the elements in the balance sheet to be balanced. This is the most fundamental equation of double entry accounting system. Furthermore,it is the basic principle in accounting and it represents the relationship between the company's

assets, liabilities and owner's equity.

The accounting equation formula is:

Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

From this formula, the value of assets should be equal to the total value of liabilities and owners' equity.

Problem Solving Using the Accounting Equation

Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

? = ↓ ₱ 10,000 + ↑ ₱ 30,000

? = ( ₱-10,000 + ₱30,000)

Δ Asset = ↑ ₱20,000 ( increase)

Therefore, an Increase of ₱20,000 of assets is the effect of the  following accounting transactions:

the equity of Krystel Company increased by ₱30,000liabilities of Krystel Company decreased by ₱10,000

Accounting Equation

The accounting equation is also called the balance sheet equation because it represents the elements in the balance sheet to be balanced. This is the most fundamental equation of double entry accounting system. Furthermore,it is the basic principle in accounting and it represents the relationship between the company's

Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

↓ ₱ 10,000 = ? + Equity (Constant/ cannot be changed)

↓ ₱ 10,000 = ↓ ₱ 10,000 + Equity (Liability account should have the same value changes as the asset since Equity is constant)

Liability account should decrease also as the same value that the amount of  asset decreases. Therefore, Payment of bank loan worth ₱10 000 is the transaction that both decreases assets and liabilities while equity remained constant.
