26. ito ay bahagi ng dula na naglalaman ng tagpuan tauhan at sulyap sa kaaiglahan suliranin[tex]pasagot \: po \: orr \: sorry \: naistorb[/tex]​


Answer:

Dula

isang uri ng panitikang ang pinakalayunin ay itanghal sa tanghalan.

Simula

parte nang dula kung saan mamamalas dito ang tagpuan, tauhan, at sulyap sa suliranin.

Gitna

parte nang dula kung saan matatagpuan ang saglit na kasiglahan, ang tunggalian, at ang kasukdulan

Wakas

parte ng dula na kung saan nandito ang kakalasan at ang kalutasan.

tagpuan

panahon at pook kung saan naganap ang mga pangyayaring isinaad sa dula

tauhan

ito ang mga kumikilos at nagbibigay-buhay sa dula; dito umiikot ang mga pangyayari; sila ang bumibigkas ng dayalogo at nagpapadama sa dula

sulyap sa suliranin

bawat dula ay may suliranin, walang dulang walang suliranin; mawawalan ng saysay ang dula kung wala itong suliranin; maaaring mabatid ito sa simula o kalagitnaan ng dula na nagsasadya sa mga pangyayari; maaaring magkaroon ng higit na isang suliranin ang isang dula

saglit na kasiglahan

saglit na paglayo o pagtakas ng mga tauhan sa suliraning nararanasan

tunggalian

ito ay maaaring sa pagitan ng mga tauhan, tauhan laban sa kanyang paligid, at tauhan laban sa kanyang sarili; maaaring magkaroon ng higit sa isa o patung-patong na tunggalian ang isang dula

Komedya

kapag masaya ang tema, walang iyakan at magaan sa loob, at ang bida ay laging nagtatagumpay.

About Figure of Speech

Figure of speech is an art of literacy that can be applied in spoken or written form so that a sentence does not seem stiff.

The main function of figure of speech is to make an expression or sentence more colorful and aesthetic

This means that you can convey certain meanings to other people through figure of speech, without having to express the meaning literally. Figures of speech are also known as rhetorical figures. Because, figure of speech can give a rhetorical effect (emphasis) on a sentence.

Figure of speech is generally used in literary works such as poetry (poem) or rhyme. But, over time, now figure of speech has been widely applied to several other works, for example, such as English greeting cards, slogans for a brand, the headline of an article, films, and many more.

Characteristics of Figure of Speech

In general, the following are the characteristics of figure of speech:

1. Can give a more emotional and imaginative effect for the reader, listener, or for those who express it.

2. Figures are often in the form of phrases consisting of one word or phrase.

3. Has independent nature (can stand alone) to describe an object.

4. Able to make something that seems abstract to be more concrete.

5. Can evoke an aesthetic feeling or situation, happy, or sad.
