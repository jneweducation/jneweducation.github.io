23. edge of table centimeter or meter​


Answer:

 A table's LENGTH are the longest sides. The rectangular table pictured is a 6 foot banquet table. So, the length of the table is 72 inches.

-A table's WIDTH are the shorter sides. The rectangle table shown has a width of 30 inches. Some tables have different widths, though many standard banquet tables are 30 inches wide. It always helps to measure.

(Note: for square tables, the length and width are the same dimension. A 4 foot square table, for example, will measure 48"L x 48"W.)

28. an orange rolls across a table with a speed 5 m/s and falls of the edge. If it lands 3 m long from the edge of the table, from what height did it fall?


Answer:

We can use the formula for the distance traveled by a falling object:

d = 1/2 * g * t^2

where d is the distance traveled, g is the acceleration due to gravity (approximately 9.81 m/s^2), and t is the time of fall.

We know that the horizontal distance the orange traveled is 3 m, and we can find the time of fall using the vertical distance the orange traveled:

d = 1/2 * g * t^2

3 = 1/2 * 9.81 * t^2

t^2 = 6.122

t = 2.47 seconds

Now we can use the time of fall to find the height the orange fell from:

h = 1/2 * g * t^2

h = 1/2 * 9.81 * (2.47)^2

h = 30.1 meters

Therefore, the orange fell from a height of approximately 30 meters.
