The theme and the message of Jose Garcia Villa's poem written in 1942.

A poem surely has its own theme and message, and this is an unwritten rule for all poets. We can identify the theme by looking at the poem overall, and trying to decide the theme in each stanza. I am sure that even one line gives a hint or clue in deciding the theme, and this holds true for the poem's message as well.

Jose Garcia Villa's poem that was written during the Japanese occupation era talks about fighting against a certain power. I believe that the poet here refers to the colonizers, but the poet skillfully uses God in each of the line.
