What would be its population in 2029? Find the increase of each month.​


Answer:

We already have the formula A = Pe^rt.

Since you already have the given that P = 680,000, r =12% per year, or 0.12 in decimal form, and t = 8  years, the unknowns left are A and e. But e is a constant, e = 2.71828..., then the unknown is A.

Now, solving for A, all we need to do is substitute these in the equation and type them in a calculator:

A = 680,000 x e^((0.12)(8)) = 680,000 x e^0.96 = 1,775,953.60
