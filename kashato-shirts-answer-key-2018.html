9. The number of shirts sold by the shopkeeper is given by the expression 3x-5.The price per shirt is given by the expression 2x+1.Find the total amount of revenue earned by the shopkeeper by selling the shirts Please need answer


Answer:

The total amount of revenue earned by the shopkeeper by selling shirts is represented by this expression:

[tex]6 {x}^{2} - 7x - 5[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

the total amount of revenue earned = the number of shirts sold × the price per shirt

We can also simplify this by substituting our given values.

The total amount of revenue earned =

[tex](3x - 5)(2x + 1)[/tex]

If we multiply these two given quantities or expression, we can get an answer of

[tex]6 {x}^{2} - 7x - 5[/tex]
