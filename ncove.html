7. introduction of ncov


The Introduction of Novel Corona Virus (NCoV)

The Novel Corona virus is the newest strain of Corona virus which is unlike anything else, hence the name. There are several Corona virus strains which originated from animals and have mutated, gaining the ability of cross-species transmission. This is why from animal carriers, Corona virus was introduced to humans.

This new and threatening virus does not simply cause a common cold. It has started an epidemic called the Novel Corona Virus Acute Respiratory Disease (NCoV ARD) which killed 1,114 people in China and 2 others in Asia. This disease of the respiratory system has currently infected more than 40,000 people in China and more than 500 people in other countries. There is no vaccine developed yet and the death toll and infected may still continue to rise.

The virus developed from two other corona virus strains, one from affecting bats and the other unknown. However, the Novel Corona virus; genetic design strongly suggests that it was spread among snakes and was cross-transmitted to humans late in December of 2019.

14. What is NcoV virus? ​


What is NCoV?

Answer: Novel Corona Virus

Novel Corona virus is a newly discovered strain of the Corona virus family that caused a new and threatening disease called the Novel Corona virus Acute Respiratory Disease.

The Novel Corona virus mutated from a combination of two existing corona viruses that has also developed the ability to cross-contaminate a different organism. Corona viruses originate from animals but mutations within certain strains allowed them to be transmitted in humans and survive long enough to cause illnesses.

Previous cases or corona viruses originated from animals such as birds, bats, cats, pigs, animals, and camels. This recent strain is strongly suspected to have originated from snakes. However, this strain could have mutated from a strain that affected bats and another with unknown origins. The first cases of  Novel Corona virus Acute Respiratory Disease was reported in Wuhan, China where the initial patients were workers at the Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market where there is an abundance of snake meat.
