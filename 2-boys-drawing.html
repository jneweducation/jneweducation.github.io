Answer: There are 30 girls in bus.Answer: 2 : 3

—————————————————

Solution:

• To find the number of girls in the bus, we will subtract the number of boys to the total number of pupils in the bus.

• Let N will be the representer of the unknown value.

N = Total number of pupils – Number of boys

N = 50 pupils – 20 boys

N = 30 girls

Final answer:

• If there are 50 pupils in the bus, and the number of boys is 20, then there are 30 girls in the bus.
