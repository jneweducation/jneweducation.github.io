13. what is the meaning of this saying " a man who has committed a mistake and does not correct it is committing another mistake


Answer:

The quote is a paradox.

Explanation:

It means that if one ignores a mistake that he has committed, for example, breaking a promise, then it will lead to regret for committing the said mistake. One thing leads to another. If that mistake is not corrected, then it will create another mistake, then another, and so on. So if one does not want to be troubled with such problems, then one must do everything in his\her power to correct any mistakes
