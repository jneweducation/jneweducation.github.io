30. the water is flowing at the rate of 2.5 cubic meter per second. compute the rate of flow im ft^3/hr​


Answer:

Given:

Q = 2.5 m³/s

we will just convert it to ft³/hr.

(Note: 1 meter = 3.28 feet)

(Note: 1 hr = 3600 secs)

Solution:

[tex](2.5\frac{m^{3} }{sec} )(\frac{3.28ft}{1m} )^{3}(\frac{3600sec}{1hr} )[/tex]

Q = 317,587.97 ft³/hr
