20. 5. The density of ethylene glycol (antifreeze, HOCH2CH2OH) is 1.09 g/mL. What is the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume if you have 30.7 grams of ethylene glycol mixed with 375 mL of water?​


✏️CONCENTRATION ETHYLENE GLYCOL

[tex]\red{•••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

ANSWER

We can use the density of ethylene glycol to convert its mass to volume.

Density of ethylene glycol = 1.09 g/mL

Volume of ethylene glycol=mass/density[tex]\\=\frac{ \rm30.7 \: g}{ \rm1.09 \: g/ ml } = \rm28.18ml[/tex]

Total volume of solution = volume of water + volume of ethylene glycol= 375 mL + 28.18 mL= 403.18 mL

[tex]\red{•••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

Now we can calculate the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume:

Concentration of ethylene glycol = (volume of ethylene glycol / total volume of solution) x 100%[tex]\\ =\frac{ \rm \: 28.18ml}{ \rm403.18 \: ml} \times 100[/tex]

[tex] 6.99%[/tex]

Therefore, the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume is 6.99%.

23. Calculate the molarity of 10.8 g ethylene glycol (C2H6O2) in 314 g water. The density of ethylene glycol is 1.11 g/mL.


The molarity of 10.8 g ethylene glycol = 0.534 M

Further explanation  

The concentration of a substance can be expressed in several quantities such as moles, percent (%) weight/volume,), molarity, molality, parts per million (ppm) or mole fraction. The concentration shows the amount of solute in a unit of the amount of solvent.  

Molarity shows the number of moles of solute in every 1 liter of solution or mmol in each ml of solution  

M = n : V  

Where  

M = Molarity  

n = Number of moles of solute  

V = Volume of solution  

volume of ethylene glycol :

= mass x density

= 10.8 g x 1.11 /ml

= 11.988 ml

volume of water :

= 314 g x 1 g/ml

= 314 ml

Volume of solution :

= 11.988 + 314

= 325.988 ml

= 0.326 L

Mol of ethylene glycol :

= mass : MW

= 10.8 g : 62,07 g/mol

= 0.174

Molarity :

= 0.174 : 0.326

= 0.534 M
