1. Give the connection between conclusion and data analysis results


Answer:

In doing a research, we have to follow some steps. These were divided into five parts.

These five parts include:

1. The Problem and its Background

Consists what is the research all about, the origin and everything surrounding the problem.

2. Review of Related Literature

These are researches, documents and any texts from books related to the topic of the research.

3. Method and Procedures

This part answers how the experiment was done, gathering of data and processes that were followed to obtain results.

4. Presentation, Analysis and Interpretation of Data

This part consist the end-product of the research. It contains all the results of the experiment and any researches done.

5. Summary, Conclusions, Recommendations

This summarizes all the efforts done from the beginning until the end. It is compose of the problem, process done and the answer that was found including suggestions to those interested to continue further on the research.

The data analysis results is included in number 4 which must be done before proceeding to part 5. This will answer your objectives. Upon answering the objectives this will give you something that you're going to put into conclusion. In other words, data analysis results is needed in order to complete the conclusion part of the research.
