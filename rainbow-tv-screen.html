15. the size of a tv screen is s given by the length of its diagonal.if the dimension of a tv screen is 16 inches by 14 inches,what is the size of the tv? with solution plss​


Given: TV Screen

Dimension: 16 in. x 14 in.

Find: Determine TV Size(Diagonal)

Plan: Use the Pythagorean Theorem

Diagonal (D)^2 = 16^2 + 14^2

D^2 = 256 + 196 = 452 => D = √(452) ≈ 21.26 in. ✅

Double Check: Reasonable/Recalculated ✅ ✅

Answer: TV Screen Size ≈ 21.26 ins.
