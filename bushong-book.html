29. In how many ways can you arrange 5 mathematics book,4 science bokks,and 3 english books on a shelf such that book of the same subject are kept togehther?


Answer:

103,680 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

The number of arrangements per book is determined as,

Math = 5!

Science = 4!

English = 3!

Number of ways that 3 different books of the same subject be grouped together = 3!

Using Fundamental Counting Principle, we have

5!4!3!3!=(120)(24)(6)(6)=103,680
