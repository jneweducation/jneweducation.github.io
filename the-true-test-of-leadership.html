21. True or False: A good leader is one who leads others on to leadership.Ans: true#OED


Who is a good leader? A good leader is someone who leads others on to leadership. Among the qualities of a good leader is not stopping at helping his followers to reach a goal together but to hone his followers to eventually become leaders themselves.  

As Kris McCrea Scrutchfield of McCrea Coaching managers could tend to micromanage while leaders wil opt to inspire. Managers tell others what to do and how to do it but leaders allow people to be creative and figure out ways to do what must be done making sure the tap into the best in people.  

This idea of Scrutchfield is supported by Susan Taylor of Generon International. She claims that managers tend to build competencies and skills specific to people’s line of work while leaders help their people develop personally and professionally. Their aim is for long-term growth of the people they lead so they can advance in their career paths.

Finally, Frances McIntosh of Intentional Coaching LLC notes that leaders are grower of people. They go beyond their job description. They look into the strengths and weaknesses of their people, the challenges their people face, their wants and needs, and they find ways to somehow help their people work through all these troubles.  

The 5 characteristics of a good leader according to Peter Economy  of The Leadership Guy would be as follows:  

1. Clarity – they are clear about their goals that is why people who are lost are often drawn to them. They have a vision and they know how to accomplish this vision.  

2. Decisiveness – they make up their minds and they commit to what they decide. Seldom do they change their minds unless necessary. Such commitment makes their followers confident that they are not in a shaky boat; that they will not be lost along the way.  

3. Courage – leaders never fear to take the path that others dare not to take. They are fearless and bold and such qualities extends to their followers, who in turn also become fearless.  

4. Passion – a leader must care about what he or she does, and this is utterly important. Someone with passion for something never stops finding ways to reach a goal. They remain energetic and they are never embarrassed to express such passion. Such strong passion is imbibed by followers eventually.  

5. Humility – of course, a leader must be confident, but a successful leader is someone to whom followers can relate. People love to be able to relate and interact with their leaders; it makes them feel welcomed. It also helps for a leader to admit a wrongdoing and to accept criticism. This makes them look human to their followers.  

With all these five qualities being manifested by leaders, followers learn how to be leaders themselves
