15. Please answer with explanation! Mang Ben and Aling Julie have 3 children who are all studying abroad. One of their children returns home after two years, the second every three years and the last one, every five years. Last December 1992 there was a big family reunion, the children left to return to their studies abroad. The old couple is waiting again for another reunion. For how long will they have to wait to be with their children again? What year will that be?


To answer this problem, we need to find the least common multiple (LCM) of 2, 3, and 5, which represents the number of years that will pass before all three children return at the same time.

The prime factors of 2, 3, and 5 are:

2: 2
