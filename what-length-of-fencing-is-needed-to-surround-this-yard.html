4. marku needs to put a fence,around a square flower garden with a side lenght of 12yards.how many yards does he need to fence the garden?


Answer:

Since the flower garden is a square with a side length of 12 yards, each side of the garden is 12 yards long. To find the total length of fencing needed to go around the garden, we need to add up the lengths of all four sides.

Therefore, the total length of fencing needed is:

$$\text{Total length of fencing} = 4\times\text{length of one side} = 4\times12\text{ yards} = 48\text{ yards}$$

So Marku needs 48 yards of fencing to surround the flower garden.

24. 1. Jeffrey fences a rectangular lot for his dog to play in the backyard. The lot measures 38 yards by 12 yards. What is the total length of fence that Jeffrey uses?


Step-by-step explanation:

The perimeter of a rectangular lot can be calculated by adding the lengths of all four sides. In this case, the length is 38 yards and the width is 12 yards. Therefore, the perimeter can be calculated as:

Perimeter = 2 x (length + width)

Perimeter = 2 x (38 yards + 12 yards)

Perimeter = 2 x 50 yards

Perimeter = 100 yards

So, Jeffrey uses a total length of 100 yards of fence to enclose the rectangular lot.
