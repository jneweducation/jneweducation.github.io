24. Jen buys 44 tires for \$272$272. What is the cost of 11 tire?


Answer:

$68

Step-by-step explanation:

There are actually a number of ways that we could find the answer to this question.

We know that it cost Jen a total of $272 to buy 44 tires.

We can take the total price of the tires and divide it by 4 to find how much 11 tires would cost.

272 / 4 = 68

Another way is to find the value of a single tire by dividing the total price by 44.

272 / 44 = 6.1818

Now that we know that each tire costs $6.1818, we can then multiply the number by 11.

6.1818 x 11 = 68.
