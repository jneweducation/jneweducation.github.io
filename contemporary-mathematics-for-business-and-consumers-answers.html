10. 1 As the CEO of a business firm, how will you strengthen your business operations when you are faced with the previously identified contemporary economic issues?​


1.

Making Customers a Priority

Customer service is about providing customers with what they want, when they want it. If your business provides quality customer service, you are likely to retain your existing customers. This also means you have a greater chance of keeping and increasing your client base. This may mean pivoting your business towards new markets or products and services.

2.

Marketing Strategies

Marketing Strategies can help you come up with new ideas to increase sales and find better ways of using your marketing dollars.

3.

Managing Staff

Make sure you have an up-to-date human resources (HR) plan. Use your plan to detail your staffing costs, which in turn will allow you to accurately cost your products or services.

4.

Networking

Networking during an economic downturn can be useful to understand how other businesses are coping. You may also discover new opportunities, customers, staff, suppliers and business partners with minimal cost to your business.

5.

Developing Innovative Practices

Developing Innovative Practices may help you adapt to changing market conditions and stay ahead of your competitors. As part of this process you should review if using technology will increase efficiency, reduce costs and make your business more competitive, for example, installing a customer management system, or doing business online.

6.

Seeking Assistance

Speak to a financial adviser or other types of business advisers, such as accountants and consultants, to help you survive an economic downturn.

15. (150-Word Essay) As a CEO of a business firm, how will you strengthen your business operations when you are faced with the previously identified contemporary economic issues?​


As a CEO, I recognize that contemporary economic issues such as inflation, global supply chain disruptions, and labor shortages can pose significant challenges to business operations. To strengthen my company's operations in the face of these issues, I would implement several strategies.

First, I would diversify our supply chain and source materials from multiple suppliers to mitigate the impact of disruptions in any one region. I would also invest in technology that provides real-time visibility into our supply chain to identify potential issues and allow for quick adjustments.

Second, I would focus on retaining and developing our existing employees, including offering competitive wages and benefits packages, providing training and development opportunities, and fostering a positive company culture.

Third, I would explore alternative revenue streams and business models that are less vulnerable to economic volatility. For example, I might consider offering subscription-based services or expanding into new markets or product lines.

Finally, I would stay up-to-date on economic trends and work with industry experts to anticipate and proactively address potential challenges. By taking these steps, I believe we can strengthen our business operations and navigate the contemporary economic landscape with greater resilience and agility.
