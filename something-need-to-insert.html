Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula: an=a1+(n-1)d

-30,_,_,_,-10 = 5th term

> -10=-30+(5-1)d

> -10=-30+4d

> 30-10=4d

> 20=4d

> 20/4=4d/4

> 5=d

Evaluate

> -30+5=-25

> -25+5=-20

> -15+5=-10
