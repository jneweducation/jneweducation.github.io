14. example of conclusion in research​


A conclusion in a research paper is a summary of the main findings and results of the study. It is a crucial part of the research paper that ties together all the different elements and presents a final interpretation of the data. An example of a conclusion in a research paper on the topic of the effects of social media on mental health might look like this:

In conclusion, this study provides evidence that social media use is associated with negative mental health outcomes, particularly among younger users. The findings suggest that excessive social media use can lead to increased feelings of anxiety and depression, as well as decreased self-esteem and social support. However, the study also highlights the need for further research to explore the complex relationship between social media use and mental health, as well as the potential benefits of social media in promoting positive mental health outcomes. Overall, the results of this study underscore the importance of monitoring social media use and promoting healthy online behaviors to protect mental health and well-being.

17. what is the difference between the conclusion in essay and the conclusion in research?​


Answer:

The conclusion in an essay is a summary of the main points discussed in the essay. It is usually a paragraph or two long and is meant to provide closure to the reader. The conclusion in an essay is often used to restate the thesis statement and to provide a final thought or opinion on the topic.

On the other hand, the conclusion in research is the final section of a research paper. It is a summary of the research findings and the implications of those findings. The conclusion in research is meant to provide closure to the research project and to offer suggestions for future research. It is usually longer than the conclusion in an essay and may include recommendations for further study or practical applications of the research findings.
