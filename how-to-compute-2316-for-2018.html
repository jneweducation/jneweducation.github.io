4. 1. 12/01/2018, "B company" bought office equipment P50,000. It was estimated to have a useful life of 10 years. After 10 years, it has a scrap value of P2,000. Compute Annual depreciation on 12/31/2018 and prepare adjusting journal entry.​


Answer:

To compute the annual depreciation for the office equipment, we can use the straight-line depreciation method:

Annual Depreciation = (Cost - Scrap Value) / Useful Life

Annual Depreciation = (50,000 - 2,000) / 10 = 4,800

Therefore, the annual depreciation for the office equipment is P4,800.

To prepare the adjusting journal entry on 12/31/2018, we need to recognize the depreciation expense for the period:

Depreciation Expense 4,800

Accumulated Depreciation 4,800

The adjusting journal entry debits Depreciation Expense for P4,800 and credits Accumulated Depreciation for the same amount. This entry reduces the book value of the office equipment and recognizes the depreciation expense for the period.
