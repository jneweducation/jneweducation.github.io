Answers with Step-by-step explanation:

250 students in a batch62 are members of Sports Club170 are members of Drama Club40 are members of both clubs

To obtain the number of students who are members of Sports Club only, simply subtract 40 from 62, and we will get 22.

To obtain the number of students who are members of Drama Club only, simply subtract 40 from 170, and we will get 130.

Then we can obtain the number of students who are not members of the Sports and Drama Club by this expression:

250 - (22+130+40)250 - 192 = 58 students

Therefore, there are 58 students who are not members of any of the two clubs.

6. of the 50 members of mathematics club 30% are the are also member of science club how many members of the club are also members of science club​


Answer:

15 members

Step-by-step explanation:

When we are using percentages, what we are really saying is that the percentage is a fraction of 100. "Percent" means per hundred, and so 50% is the same as saying 50/100 or 5/10 in fraction form.

So, since our denominator in 30/50 is 50, we could adjust the fraction to make the denominator 100. To do that, we divide 100 by the denominator:

100 ÷ 50 = 2

Once we have that, we can multiple both the numerator and denominator by this multiple:

30 x 2=60

50 x 2=100

Now we can see that our fraction is 60/100, which means that 30/50 as a percentage is 60%.

We can also work this out in a simpler way by first converting the fraction 30/50 to a decimal. To do that, we simply divide the numerator by the denominator:

30/50 = 0.6

Once we have the answer to that division, we can multiply the answer by 100 to make it a percentage:

0.6 x 100 = 60%
