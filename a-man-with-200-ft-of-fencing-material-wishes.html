3. A man with 200 ft. of fencing material wishes to fence off an area in the shapeof a rectangle. What should be the dimensions of the area if the enclosed space is tobe as large as possible? What is the largest area?Hint: A = 1w, P = 21 + 2w​


Answer:

200 ft. is the perimeter, since that's how much fencing there is. 2 lengths and 2 widths of the field = 200: 2L+2w=200.

We also know the area of a rectangle is length times width, and we want to maximize that. A=L(w).

We can't solve a single equation with two variables, so we can eliminate either one in the first equation. For no particular reason, let's solve for L:

2L+2w=200. Subtract 2w from both sides: 2L=200-2w.

Divide through by 2 to get L:

L=100-w.

Substitute (100-w) for L in the area equation: A=(100-w)w.

Distribute the w, rearrange the terms, and A= -w2 + 100w

Graph this to find your vertex. (You can also use -b/2a=x to find the x coordinate of your vertex then plug it in to get the y.)

The maximum point is at (50, 2500), which is (w, A) in variables. So the width at maximum area 2500 ft2 is 50 ft.

Squares always maximize area! The field should be 50 ft x50 ft, and the area is 2500 ft2.

Answer:

The maximum area is 2 500 sq. feet.

Step-by-step explanation:

There are many ways finding the maximum area using knowledge and principles of algebra and complex problem are solve using derivatives (calculus).

In this case, let's solve this in easiest way.

Take note! A rectangle will have a maximum possible area when all sides are the same length if perimeter was already given.

Since the perimeter was 200 feet, and rectangle has 4 sides. Then 200 divided by 4, the possible length of rectangle that we can have is 50 feet.

The formula for area is the product of length and width.

A= LW, since the length is 50, and we can have the maximum area if all sides of rectangle are the same length.

A= LW

A= (50)(50)

A= 2 500 sq. ft.

Therefore, the maximum area for playground is 2,500 sq. feet.
