2. What is the volume and the surface area of a cubic solid whose side measures 15 cm?


Answer:

Volume of cube = 3,375 cm³Surface area of cube = 90 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

A cubic solid is a three-dimensional figure whose 12 edges or sides are equal in measure. The six surface areas or faces are also equal.

Volume of cube = (side)³

Volume of cube = (15 cm)³

Volume of cube = 3,375 cm³

Surface area of cube = 6 (side)

Surface area of cube = 6 (15 cm)

Surface area of cube = 90 cm.

26. C. Convert Liter to cubic centimeter. Liter Example: 1.8L 2.39 L 45 L Cubic Centimeter 45,000cm³​


Answer:

To convert liters (L) to cubic centimeters (cm³), you need to multiply the number of liters by 1000 (since there are 1000 cubic centimeters in one liter).

So, for the given examples:

- 1.8 L = 1.8 x 1000 = 1800 cm³

- 2.39 L = 2.39 x 1000 = 2390 cm³

- 45 L = 45 x 1000 = 45000 cm³
