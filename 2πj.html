Problem:

2. A heat engine is given 120J120J by heat and releases 20J 20J by heat to the  environment. What is the amount of work done by the system?​

Give Data:

Q ( Heat transferred ) = 20 J

ΔU ( change in internal heat ) =  120 J

W ( Work done ) = ? unknown

Solution:

W = ΔU - Q

    = 120 J - 20 J

    = 100 J

Answer:

The work done of the system is equal to 100 J
