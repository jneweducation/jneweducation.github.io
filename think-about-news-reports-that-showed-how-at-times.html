Nomination Strategy
When someone is presenting a particular topic, or saying what he thinks is relevant, he is using the nomination strategy.
Restriction

Turn-taking

Topic shifting

Repair
When the communication is not in good shape, a repair strategy is being done to overcome communication breakdown.
Termination
It uses verbal or nonverbal signals to end communication or interaction.
