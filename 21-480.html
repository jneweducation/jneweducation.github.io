Explanation:

To find the total area to be painted, we need to determine the area of all six sides of the rectangular box.

The area of one side (one of the faces) is simply the length multiplied by the width. So the area of one face is:

36 cm x 48 cm = 1,728 cm^2

Since there are six sides to the box, the total area to be painted is:

6 x 1,728 cm^2 = 10,368 cm^2

Therefore, the answer is not one of the options given. The correct answer is 10,368 cm^2.
