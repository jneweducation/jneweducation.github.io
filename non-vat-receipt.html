14. 3. What is the VAT on an item that cost 100AED if VAT is calculated at 15%? A. 155AED B. 5AED C. 15AED


To calculate the VAT on an item that costs 100AED with a VAT rate of 15%, you can use the following formula:

VAT amount = (VAT rate/100) x price of the item

Substituting the values given in the problem, we get:

VAT amount = (15/100) x 100AED = 15AED

Therefore, the VAT on the item is 15AED (option C).
