9. why u can't see the particles of instant coffee in hot water


Answer:

To take the most simple view:

1) Ground coffee has two parts: the part that dissolves in hot water and makes “coffee”, (the soluble coffee oils and flavors from the coffee bean), and the part that does not dissolve in hot water, (the woody part of the coffee bean).

2) Instant coffee only has the part that dissolves in hot water. The part that does not dissolve in hot water has been discarded prior to packaging.

13. 1.what is the solution of water and salt?2.what is the solution of water and sugar?3.what is the solution of water and instant cofee?4.what is the solution of water and food coloring?what is the solution of water and powdered milk​


Answer:

1.In a NaCl solution (salt-water), the solvent is water. A solute is the component in a solution in the lesser amount. In a NaCl solution, the salt is the solute. A solution may contain more than one solute.

2.When you stir a spoonful of sugar into a glass of water, you are forming a solution. This type of liquid solution is composed of a solid solute, which is the sugar, and a liquid solvent, which is the water. As the sugar molecules spread evenly throughout the water, the sugar dissolves.

3. A solution is a mixture containing one substance that dissolves (solute), and another substance that does the dissolving (solvent). An example of a solution is salty water.

4.In the case of food coloring in water, the water is the solvent while the food coloring is the solute. Once they've mixed, they make a solution.
