3. The circumference of a circle varies directly as its diameter.If the circumference of a circle having a diameter of 8 cm is 8 (pi)cm,what is the circumference of a circle having a diameter of 12 cm?​


Answer:

12π cm

Explanation:

Given that : Circumference is equal to 8π cm when the diameter is 8 cm,

Let : C = circumference

d= diameter

k = constant of variation

The equation of variation is:

C= kd

We will use this to find the value of k (constant of variation)

C=kd8π = (k)(8)8π=8kπ=kk=π

The constant of variation is equal to π (pi).

Hence, we will have the equation C=πd, a formula for finding the circumference of a circle.

We will now use this to solve the problem:

C=πd

C=(π)(12)

C=12π

We, now have the answer:

12πd cm.
