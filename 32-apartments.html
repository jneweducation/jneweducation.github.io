29. In an apartment complex, there are 5 windows in each apartment, and 9 apartments in each floor. How many windows are there on 8 floors?


Answer:

Algebraic Equations

In an apartment complex with 5 windows in each apartment and 9 apartments in each floor, there should be 360 windows in 8 floors.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this equation, we need to list down the items given, and what we are looking for. This way, we can solve the problem easily and in a systematic manner.

Given:

5 windows in each apartment 9 apartments in each floor

Find:

Number of windows on 8 floors

Solution:

Total number of windows = 5 X 9 X 8

Total number of windows = 360 windows

Therefore, on the first 8 floors of the apartment complex, there should be a total of 360 windows.

30. favorite and dislike scene in five feet apart?plot: ( five feet apart)​


Answer:

Favorite Scene:

The favorite scene in Five Feet Apart is when Stella and Will share their first kiss. Will has been struggling with his illness and Stella has been trying to stay away from him to protect her own health, but when they finally come together, it's a powerful moment of connection and hope. The moment is even more special because of the challenges they have faced to get to that moment.

Dislike Scene:

The dislike scene in Five Feet Apart is when Stella's best friend Poe collapses and is rushed to the hospital. The scene is difficult to watch as Poe has been a source of strength and joy for Stella throughout the film. Seeing her friend in such a vulnerable state brings up a lot of fear and sadness for Stella, and it's a difficult moment to watch.
