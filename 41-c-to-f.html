To convert temperatures in degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 and multiply by .5556 (or 5/9).

1. 41 °F to °C

Formula : (41°F − 32) × 5/9

Solution :

[°C] = ([°F] − 32) × 5⁄9

Thus, subtract 32 from the value '41' in Fahrenheit multiply the result by 5, then divide the result by 9. Thus,

41 Fahrenheit = (41 - 32) × 5⁄9 = (9) × 5⁄9 = 5 Celsius.

Final Answer : (41°F − 32) × 5/9 = 5°C
