23. Container A contains 20 blue marbles and 15 red marbles with a total mass of 340 g. Container B contains 10 blue marbles and 6 red marbles with a total mass of 152 g. Find the mass of a blue marble and a red marble. love makale = 89​


Answer:

Blue marbles = 12g each

Red marbles = 8 grams each

Step-by-step explanation:

Blue marble = x

Red marble = y

eq1. 20x + 15y = 340

eq2. 10x + 6y = 152

Step 1:

Isolate "X"

eq2. 10x = 152 - 6y

x = (152 - 6y) / 10

Step 2:

Substitute "x" to eq1.

eq1.

20x + 15y = 340

[20 (152 - 6y) / 10] + 15y = 340

find the value of y

***y = 12***

Step 3:

Substitute to eq 1.

20x + 15(12) = 340

x = [340 - (15 * 12)] / 20

x = (340 - 180) / 20

x = 160 ÷ 20

***x = 8***
