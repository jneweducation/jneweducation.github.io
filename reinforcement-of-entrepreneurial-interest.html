1. Reinforcement of Entrepreneurial interest


The Reinforcement of the Entrepreneurial interest is a very important goal. Many Filipinos have lost that spirit after the the colonizers have established themselves in our country. People were resigned to being second class citizens to the Spanish occupiers and in the process, destroying the merchant culture that was shaped through years of trading with the Chinese, Arabs and other Asian neighbors. This became the norm after the Americans came, they promoted a system of education wherein a graduate is expected to work for a company and live the American Dream or something similar to that.

It is in our interest to promote entrepreneurship, not only for the nation’s pride but to also create jobs for many of us. Those who succeed in the business word would eventually need to hire people in order to grow, and in the process giving livelihood to the everyone around them. If enough of these local Industrialist do well, then it will be a benefit to the whole country. And that is a good enough reason to push for the reinforcement of the entrepreneurial interest in our country.
