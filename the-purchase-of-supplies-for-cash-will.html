13. If the person pays part of the cash price at the time of purchase.


If the person pays part of the cash price at the time of purchase, the transaction is classified as a credit sales.

Explanation :

In conducting sales transactions, activities generally occur where consumers or customers provide cash to service/goods providers and vice versa, producers or providers provide goods or services needed by consumers. Delivery of cash directly and intact is part of trading or selling activities. However, there is a condition where when a sales activity occurs, the buyer only gives part of the money to buy an item or service, this is called a credit sale.

In cash sales, the buyer directly pays for the product sold by the seller at the agreed price. Credit sales are sales that are not paid in cash. The buyer will pay by installments or credit according to the agreement with the seller.

Credit or installments are more attractive to some buyers because they solve cash flow problems because the perceived price of the merchandise appears lower than if (the price) were charged all at once.
