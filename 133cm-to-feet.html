Answer:

48 ft²

Step-by-step explanation:

First:

Find the area of the whole rectangle.

Area= l x w

= 10x6

Area= 60ft²

Second: Find the area of the small rectangle.

Area= l x w

= 3x4

Area= 12ft²

Last: Subtract the area of the whole rectangle and small rectangle.

Area= Are of the whole rectangle minus the small rectangle

=60-12

Area=48ft²
