2. Noli Me Tangere Kabanata 33 questions with answers?​


Noli Me Tangere

Kabanata 33: Malayang Kaisipan

Tanong at Sagot:

1. Sino si Elias?

Sagot:

Si Elias ay ang binatang naligtas ni Ibarra mula sa buwaya ng sila ay mag piknik dahil dito nagkaroon siya ng utang na loob kay Ibarra.

2. Ayon sa binasa, bakit nagkaroon ng kaaway si Ibarra?

Sagot:

Nagkaroon ng kaaway si Ibarra dahil sa kanyang mga ninuno at ama na nagkaroon ng kagalit at bunga na rin ng pagnanais niya na maipatayo ang akademya ng wikang kastila.

3. Paano natuklasan ni Elias ang tangkang pagpatay kay Ibarra?

Sagot:

Natuklasan ni Elias ang tangkang pagpatay kay Ibarra ng marinig niya ang taong madilaw na may kausap at sinabing hindi kakainin ng isda si Ibarra.

4. Bakit tinanggap ni Nor Juan ang taong madilaw sa kabila ng kakulangan nito ng kasanayan sa trabaho?

Sagot:

Sapagkat bukod sa makakatipid siya ng bayad dito ay batid din ni Nor Juan ang tangkang pagpatay kay Ibarra.

5. Bakit malayang kaisipan ang pamagat ng kabanatang ito?

Sagot:

Ang kabanatang ito ay pinamagatang malayang kaisipan sapagkat ito ang nais ni Elias para kay Ibarra. Nais niya na magkaroon si Ibarra ng malayang kaisipan. Malaya sa anumang isipin tulad ng panganib at kapahamakan na dulot ng kanyang mga kaaway.

When Was it written ?What else was happening at the time of its writing ? How did it come to be in the form we have it today? What did it mean to the people who first read or heard it?​


Answer:

The novel Noli Me Tangere was written by Dr. Jose Rizal.

It was written in 1887-1889.

During the time of its writing, the Philippines was under the colonial rule of Spain, and the people were suffering from oppression, poverty, and injustice. The country was also experiencing a period of enlightenment and nationalism, with the emergence of the ilustrados or the educated class who sought reforms and changes in the social, political, and economic aspects of the society.

Noli Me Tangere was initially written in Spanish, but was later translated into different languages, including Tagalog and English. It was published in Berlin, Germany in 1887, and was smuggled and distributed in the Philippines, where it had a profound impact on the people, particularly the ilustrados and the masses.The novel was a wake-up call to the Filipinos, exposing the abuses and corruption of the Spanish colonizers and the friars, and advocating for social justice, equality, and nationalism. The novel was banned by the Spanish authorities, but it sparked a revolution that led to the Philippine independence in 1898.

To the people who first read or heard it, Noli Me Tangere was a powerful and revolutionary work that exposed the truth about the colonial oppression and exploitation in the Philippines. It inspired the Filipinos to rise up and fight for their rights and freedom, and it gave them a sense of national identity and pride. The novel became a symbol of resistance and a source of inspiration for the Philippine revolution and the struggle for independence.
