1. Let: A1 = area of the smaller square

A2 = area of the semi-circle

A3 = area of the composite figure

Solution:

• A1 = s²

= 2²

A1 = 4 in²

• A2 = (1/2)(πD²/4)

= 1/2 x [(π)(2)²/4)]

= 1/2 x π

A2 = 1.57 in²

• A3 = A1 + 2(A2)

= 4 + [2 x 1.57]

A3 = 7.14 in²

The area of the heart is 7.14 in²

2. Let: A1 = area of the rectangle

A2 = area of the semi-circle

A3 = area of the triangle

A4 = area of the shaded region

Solution:

• A1 = L x W

= (150 + 30 + 120) x (120 + 130)

= 300 x 250

A1 = 75,000 mm²

• A2 = 1/2 x πr²

= 1/2 x (π)(100)²

= 1/2 x 10,000π

A2 = 15,707.96 mm²

• A3 = 1/2 bh

= 1/2 x 120 x 120

A3 = 7,200 mm²

• A4 = A1 - A2 - A3

= 75,000 - 15,707.96 - 7,200

A4 = 52,092.04 mm²

The area of the shaded region is 52,092.04 mm²
