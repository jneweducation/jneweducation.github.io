pa follow

a. 5,600,000 mm

To convert kilometers to millimeters, multiply by 1,000,000. Therefore, 0.0056 km = 0.0056 x 1,000,000 mm = 5,600,000 mm.

b. 0.00000758 m³

To convert cubic centimeters to cubic meters, divide by 1,000,000. Therefore, 7.58 cm³ = 7.58 ÷ 1,000,000 m³ = 0.00000758 m³.

c. 16,500 mL

To convert liters to milliliters, multiply by 1,000. Therefore, 16.5 L = 16.5 x 1,000 mL = 16,500 mL.

d. 16.3 kg/L

To convert grams per milliliter (g/mL) to kilograms per liter (kg/L), divide by 1000. Therefore, 16.3 g/mL = 16.3 ÷ 1000 kg/L = 0.0163 kg/L.

e. 0.0025 m³

To convert liters to cubic meters, divide by 1000. Therefore, 2.5 L = 2.5 ÷ 1000 m³ = 0.0025 m³.
