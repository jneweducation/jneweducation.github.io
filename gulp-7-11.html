18. what is more polite sip or gulp?​


Answer:

The word "sip" is more polite because taking a sip means "taking a small or very small drink," and taking a gulp means "taking a big drink in one swallow" and drinking some more has no indication as to how much. You can also be a bit more direct, e.g., sipping her tea, gulping her tea or drinking her tea.
