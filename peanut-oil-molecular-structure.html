28. Describe the molecular structure of water.​


Answer:

A water molecule is made up of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom. A single oxygen atom contains six electrons in its outer shell, which can hold a total of eight electrons. When two hydrogen atoms are bound to an oxygen atom, the outer electron shell of oxygen is filled.

Answer:

Peanut butter is made by grinding roasted peanuts. During grinding, pressure dispersed the oil in the peanut cells in small droplets to remain stabilized in the peanut butter body. When it is not done properly, the oil droplets become unstable, coalesce with the nearby drops and form large mass. In time, as oil is lighter than the peanut butter, they slowly rise upwards and eventually form the layer. It happens more in higher temperature environment.
