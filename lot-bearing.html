1. 1. Why is it important for bears to eat a lot during autumn?​


During the fall months, bears eat and drink nearly nonstop. They need to put on weight to prepare for winter and hibernation. This process is called hyperphagia. During hyperphagia, bears are very active and many visitors have a chance to see them in action.

Answer:

Cone-bearing plants have two types of cones: female cones and male cones. Reproduction begins when one of the pollen grains from inside a pollen sac on the male cone floats through the air to the scale of a female cone. A pollen tube then grows from the pollen grain toward the female ovule.
