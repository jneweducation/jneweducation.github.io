14. Miley buys a triangular pennant for souvenir.The base of the pennant is 35 cm shorter than the two other sides, which are equal.if the perimeter of the pennant is 145 cm, find its dimensions​


Answer:

Let x be the length of each of the two equal sides of the triangular pennant.

Then, the length of the base is x - 35.

The perimeter of the pennant is the sum of the lengths of its sides: x + x + (x - 35) = 145.

Simplifying the equation, we get:

3x - 35 = 145

Adding 35 to both sides, we get:

3x = 180

Dividing both sides by 3, we get:

x = 60

Therefore, the length of each of the two equal sides of the triangular pennant is 60 cm, and the length of the base is 25 cm (60 - 35).

So, the dimensions of the triangular pennant are: 60 cm, 60 cm, and 25 cm.
