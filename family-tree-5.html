7. Write three to five sentences about the benefits for planting trees and fruit bearing trees to the family. (5 points) Parent's Signature​


In Botany, a tree is a plant characterized by an extended stem, woody trunk, and leaves. There are two groups of trees: deciduous and coniferous. Deciduous trees are composed of broad leaves that change color during fall and spread their seeds using flowers. Coniferous trees have needle-like leaves, color does not change, and they use cones to spread their seeds. Fruit-bearing trees produce fruit when their ripened ovaries in flowers contain one or more seeds.

Benefits derived from planting trees and fruit-bearing trees-

Planting trees and fruit-bearing trees give the following benefits to families and communities:

>Provide food and shelter.
