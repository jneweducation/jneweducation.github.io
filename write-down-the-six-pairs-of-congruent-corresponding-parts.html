EXPLANATION:

Given,

It is given that ∆ABD≅ ∆CBD.

To find,

We have to find the six pairs of congruent corresponding parts.

Solution,

The six pairs of congruent corresponding parts are AB = CB, BD = BD, AD = CD, ∠A = ∠C, ∠ABD = ∠CBD, ∠ADB = ∠CDB.

The six pairs of congruent corresponding parts are three sides and three angles of two traingle ∆ABD and ∆CBD

It is given that ∆ABD≅ ∆CBD, then

AB = CB

BD = BD

AD = CD

∠A = ∠C

∠ABD = ∠CBD

∠ADB = ∠CDB

Hence, the six pairs of congruent corresponding parts are AB = CB, BD = BD, AD = CD, ∠A = ∠C, ∠ABD = ∠CBD, ∠ADB = ∠CDB.

5. LEARNING ACTIVITY 1 B C AABC = A EDC. Write down the six pairs of corresponding congruent parts. ​


Answer:

∠ADB≅∠CDB

∠DAB ≅∠DCB

∠ABD≅∠CBD

AD≅CD

AB≅CB

BD≅BD

Step-by-step explanation:

A triangle has three sides and angles.

To get the corresponding parts, we list the letter (denoting the points) on what order they appear.

For example, on ΔABD, A and B are the first and second letters. They should correspond to the first and second letters of the other triangle, ΔCBD. The first and second letters on the other triangle are C and B; therefore, AB corresponds to CB.

AB≅CB

For the other segments, we get the other ordered points. For AD (1st and 3rd), its corresponding segment is CD. AD≅CD. For BD (2nd and 3rd), its corresponding is BD. BD≅BD.

For the angles, we do the same, but do with 3 letters. For example, ∠ABD corresponds to ∠CBD since the order of the letters is 1st, 2nd, 3rd. ∠ABD≅∠CBD.

The other corresponding angles are

∠ADB≅∠CDB and

∠DAB ≅∠DCB
