1. State the qualities of qualitative and quantitative research Qualitative Quantitative ​


Answer:

Quantitative research deals with numbers and statistics, while qualitative research deals with words and meanings. Quantitative methods allow you to systematically measure variables and test hypotheses. Qualitative methods allow you to explore concepts and experiences in more detail.

28. what is qualitative research and its qualities write down 5 phrases​


Answer:

Qualitative research involves collecting and analyzing non-numerical data, such as text, audio or video. Qualitative research is the opposite of quantitative research, which involves collecting and analyzing numerical data for statistical analysis.

Qualities:

1. Natural environment (natural setting).

2. Researcher as a key instrument (researcher as key instrument).

3. Multiple sources of data.

4. Inductive data analysis.

5. The meaning of the participants (participant’s meaning).
