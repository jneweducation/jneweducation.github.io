22. the maglalatik or magbabao is classified as coconut shells because it deepest site mock battle between the_________ ​


Answer:

The Maglalatik or Magbabao is classified as War Dance because it despicts mock battle between the Moros and Christian over "Latik" (residue left after the coconut milk has been boiled) Moros were successful in getting the latik. The dance became more popular during the Spanish Era in the barrios of Loma and Zapote of Binian, Laguna.

The dance became part of the celebrations during fiestas and religious gathering. Maglalatik dancers go from house to house performing for money or gifts. Maglalatik dance was performed as an offering to patron Saint of the farmers, San Isidro De Labrador.

The dance is performed by males who use 12 pieces of Coconut shells attached to the different parts of the body. Maglalatik is an energetic dance and considered as a skill dance that develops proper coordination of the body.
