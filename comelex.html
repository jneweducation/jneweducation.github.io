4. Simple or complex or compound or compound complex


A simple sentence is a sentence that consists of just one independent clause. A complex sentence is a sentence that contains an independent clause and one or more dependent clauses. A compound-complex sentence combines the two.

11. How can complex and compound-complex sentences connect multiple ideas?


Answer:

Conjunctions. Conjunctions are those little connecting words that can put different phrases and clauses together in to larger more complex sentences. In compound-complex sentences, we use conjunctions to string together most of our independent and dependent clauses. The most common conjunctions are and, or, and but.

14. english compound complex sentence any tip to create a compound complex sentence?


Example: Begin with two independent clauses: The team captain jumped for joy. The fans cheered. Then combine the independent clauses to form a compound sentence: The team captain jumped for joy, and the fans cheered. Now, add a dependent clause to your compound sentence to create a compound-complex sentence.


15. What are the two complex solutions or complex zeros of a polynomial function?


Answer:

Complex number are ones that include the square root of a negative number, they are usually represented with the italic letter i or j. The concept comes from the Euler equations, and is used in advanced algebra and calculus. It is also part of mathematics that includes the LaPlace transforms. Simply put to answer your question complex roots and zeros are the solutions to sets of polynomial equations that include the square root of a negative number. Solutions to such equations work the same way as any other polynomial solution.

The roots and zeros are the solutions to a ratio of polynomials, ie polynomials in the numerator and the denominator. Zeros are the solution to the numerator and roots are the solutions to the denominator. These are often used in controls modeling.
