(5 points) Feb 5: PHP 48.085= 1 USD


Answer:

To determine what Melissa would gain or lose with her decision, we need to compare the exchange rates on the days she received the money (in January 2021) with the exchange rate on February 5, 2021.

Since the exchange rates for January 2021 are not provided, we will assume that Melissa received the money on January 31, 2021. In this case, we will compare the exchange rate on February 5, 2021, with the exchange rate on January 31, 2021.

Assuming the exchange rate on January 31, 2021, was PHP 48.034 = 1 USD (the same rate as February 1, 2021), and the exchange rate on February 5, 2021, was PHP 48.085 = 1 USD, we can calculate the difference in the exchange rates.

On January 31, Melissa received the money at the rate of PHP 48.034 = 1 USD. If she decided to exchange the money on February 5, she would receive PHP 48.085 for 1 USD.

To calculate the gain or loss, we can subtract the initial exchange rate from the final exchange rate:

Final Exchange Rate - Initial Exchange Rate = PHP 48.085 - PHP 48.034 = PHP 0.051

Therefore, Melissa would gain PHP 0.051 for every 1 USD if she exchanged the money on February 5, 2021, compared to the exchange rate on January 31, 2021.
