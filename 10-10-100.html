Answer:

a. 10,-10

Step-by-step explanation:

Simplifying

2x^2 = 200

Solving

2x^2 = 200

Solving for variable 'x'.

Move all terms containing x to the left, all other terms to the right.

Divide each side by '2'.

x^2 = 100

Simplifying

x^2 = 100

Take the square root of each side:

x = {-10, 10}
