16. A rafter is any parallel beams that support a roof. if the rafter is 4 meters long and makes an angle of 35° with horizontal, what is the height of the rafter's peak? ​


Problem:

A rafter is any parallel beams that support a roof. if the rafter is 4 meters long and makes an angle of 35° with horizontal, what is the height of the rafter's peak?

Formula:

Use the formula of trigonometric ratios to find their the height of the problem.

sin θ = opposite side/hypotenuse

Solution:

One trigonometric ratios form the SOH may be used in order to come up with height of the rafter's peak. Let x be the height of the rafter's peak.

Solve the mathematical equation.

sin θ = opposite side/hypotenusesin 35° = x/4 mx = (sin 35°)(4 m) x = (0.573576)(4 m) x = 2.29431 mx = 2.3 m

Answer:

∴ The height of the rafter's peak is 2.3 meters.
