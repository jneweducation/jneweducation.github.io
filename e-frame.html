29. 1. Liza used a circular frame for her project in Art. If the diameter of the frame is 34centimeters, what is the area of the circular frame?A. What is asked?_________________________________________________B. What are the given facts?_________________________________________C. What formula will be used?_____________________________________D. What is the mathematical sentence?________________________________E. What is the correct answer?_______________________________________​


A. What is asked?The area of the circular frame.

B. What are the given facts?The diameter of the frame is 34 centimeters.

C. What formula will be used?The formula to calculate the area of a circle is A = πr², where A represents the area and r represents the radius of the circle.

D. What is the mathematical sentence?The mathematical sentence is A = πr², where r = 1/2 of the diameter (r = 34/2 = 17).

E. What is the correct answer?Using the formula A = πr², we can substitute the value of r and calculate the area:

To find the area, we calculate:Area = π * (17 cm)^2≈ 3.14 * (17 cm)^2≈ 3.14 * 289 cm²≈ 907.46 cm²Therefore,  the correct answer is approximately 907.46 square centimeters.
