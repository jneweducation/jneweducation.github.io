2. Answer the question with three to five sentences.What do you mean by end service procedure?​


The end service procedure refers to the steps taken to terminate or conclude a service agreement or contract between a provider and a client.

The process may involve a range of activities such as communication, documentation, and administrative tasks that ensure a smooth and satisfactory termination of the service relationship.

Depending on the nature of the service, the end service procedure may include transferring knowledge or information to the client, returning assets or equipment, or settling financial obligations.

In some cases, the end service procedure may be initiated by either the provider or the client, and it may be subject to specific terms and conditions outlined in the service agreement or contract.

Overall, the end service procedure is an important aspect of service management that helps to establish a positive relationship between the provider and the client even after the service relationship has ended.
