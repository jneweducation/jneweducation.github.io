What does ADSL mean on a modem?

Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line

Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line (ADSL) is a technology that provides high transmission speeds for video and voice to homes over an ordinary copper telephone wire.

Which is the standard modulation used by DSL modems?

Discrete Multitone Modulation

Data transmission

With ADSL, the modem and the DSLAM communicate by a protocol called discrete multitone modulation (DMT), which is a form of frequency division multiplexing. The modem only uses frequencies above 8 kHz, to avoid interfering with normal phone service.

30. One of the components of photocells and space vehicles is rubidium. How many moles are there in 9.50 g of Rb? The molar mass of Rb is 85.47 g/mol.​


[tex]\large\underline{\rm{PROBLEM}:}[/tex]

One of the components of photocells and space vehicles is rubidium. How many moles are there in 9.50 g of Rb? The molar mass of Rb is 85.47 g/mol.

[tex]\tt\color{black}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{ANSWER}:}[/tex]

[tex]\: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \:[/tex] ↳ [tex]\large{{\tt\color{skyblue}{0.111 \: moles \: of \: Rb}}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{black}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{SOLUTION}:}[/tex]

⇛To find the number of moles in 9.50 g of Rb, we can use the formula: [tex]\small{{\tt{moles(n) \: = \: \frac{mass(m)}{molar \: mass(m)} \\ }}}[/tex]

[tex]\small{{\tt{moles(n) \: = \: \frac{mass(m)}{molar \: mass(m)} \\ }}}[/tex]

[tex]\small{{\tt{moles(n) \: = \: \frac{9.50 \: g}{85.47 \: g/mol} \\ }}}[/tex]

[tex]\small{{\tt\color{skyblue}{moles(n) \: = \: 0.111}}}[/tex]

∵　Therefore, there are 0.111 moles of rubidium in 9.50 g of Rb.
