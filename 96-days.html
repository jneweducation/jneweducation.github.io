1. 3 days to 96 hours Anong anwer​


Answer:

Yes, 3 days is equivalent to 96 hours. The reason for this is that there are 24 hours in a day. Therefore, if you want to convert the number of days to its equivalent in hours, you simply need to multiply the number of days by 24. Hence, 3 days x 24 hours per day = 72 hours. However, since you want to know the total number of hours in 3 days, you must add another 24 hours (1 day) to the result. Therefore, 72 hours + 24 hours = 96 hours, which is the total number of hours in 3 days.

5. Calculate the interest and amount on a loan of $140.00 at 5% for 96 days.​


Answer:

To calculate the interest and amount on a loan of $140.00 at 5% for 96 days, we can use the formula I = P × R × T, where I is the interest, P is the principal loan amount ($140.00), R is the rate (5%), and T is the time (96 days). This gives us an interest of $28.00. The total amount payable is the principal loan amount plus the interest which is $168.00.

10. Curium (Z=96) was formed by reacting Pu-239 with alpha particles 42He. it has a half-life of 162 days

There are two types of nuclear reactions

Fission reaction is the splitting of an atomic nucleus by firing a particle

Example:

²³⁵₉₂U + ¹₀n -> ⁸⁹₃₆Kr + ¹⁴⁴₅₆Ba + 3¹₀n

Fusion reaction is a combination of two atomic nuclei

Example:

³₁Ti + ²₁D -> ⁴₂He + ¹₀n

There are several particles involved in atomic nuclei reactions:

alpha α particles ₂He⁴

beta β ₋₁e⁰ particles

gamma particles γ

positron particles ₁e⁰

neutron particles ₀n¹

In a nuclear reaction, it is always found that the atomic number (Z) and mass number (A) are the same either before or after the reaction

In the fusion reaction of Plutonium (Pu) and Helium (He), it can also be said to be a synthesis reaction due to the combination of two elements.

Reaction :

number of atomic numbers (Z) =  94+2=96+0

number of mass numbers (A) = 239+4= 242+1
