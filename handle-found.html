The cutting blade is a lever, the handles are wedges, and the part that turns the can is a gear. D. The cutting blade is a wedge, the handles are levers, and the part that turns the can is a wheel and axle.


Answer:

D.

Explanation:

The above can opener contains four simple machines: a screw, a lever, a wedge, and a wheel and axle. The wedge is found on the cutter, the lever is the handle, and the screw is the device that holds the can opener together. The wheel and axle attaches the cutter to the opener.
