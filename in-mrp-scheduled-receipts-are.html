1. What is the inventory position if the on-hand quantity is 462 units, scheduled receipts are 200 units, and 130 units are on backorder?


INVENTORY POSITIONING

If the on-hand quantity is 462 units, the scheduled receipts are 200 units, and the other 130 units are on backorder, the inventory position is  532 units

How to solve it?

First, we have to identify the values of the following parameters: on-order inventory, inventory level, and backorder.Then, we have to know the formula for each of them.Inventory Level= on-hand inventory - backorderInventory Position = on-order inventory + inventory level

Solving:

Inventory Level = 462 - 130 = 332 units

Inventory Position = 200 + 332 = 532 units
