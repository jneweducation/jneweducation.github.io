6. An amount of P15,000.00 is invested at a simple interest of 6% per annum in 6 years. Find the interest and accumulated amount in six years.​


INTEREST || ACCUMULATED AMOUNT

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Problem:} [/tex]

An amount of P15,000.00 is invested at a simple interest of 6% per annum in 6 years. Find the interest and accumulated amount in six years.

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Answer:}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad \huge \rm I = 21,277.78[/tex]

[tex] \qquad \huge \rm A = 36,277.78 [/tex]

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Solution:}[/tex]

Determine the amounts of interest and it's maturity value using the give formula where p is the principal, r is the rate of interest, t is the time and m stands for maturity value itself

[tex]\bold{Formula\:||\:M_{Value} = P + P(1+r) ^{t}}[/tex]

[tex]\rm M_{Value} = 15,000 + 15,000 (1 + \frac{6\%}{100}) ^{6}[/tex]

»To find how much is the accumulated value, plug the values to the given formula,

[tex]\rm M_{Value} = 15,000 + 15,000 ( 1 + 0.06) ^{6}[/tex]

[tex]\rm M_{Value} = 15,000 + 15,000 (1.06) ^{6}[/tex]

[tex]\rm M_{Value} = 15,000 + 21,277.78[/tex]

[tex]\rm M_{Value} = 36,277.78[/tex]

Therefore,

Interest = 21,277.78Maturity Value = 36,277.78

==================================
