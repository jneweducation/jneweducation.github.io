9. A lighthouse is 3,500 km north-east of a port. A ship leaves the port at noon, and sails east at a speed of 500 km per hour. At what time will it be 3,000 km from the lighthouse?


Solution:

Let (x) be the hour

[tex] \frac{500}{1} = \frac{3000}{x} [/tex]
[tex]500x=(3000)(1)[/tex]
[tex]500x=3000[/tex]
[tex] \frac{500x}{500} = \frac{3000}{500} [/tex]
[tex]x=6[/tex]

Answer:

6 hours500 km = 1 hr

3, 000 km = __ hrs

SOLUTION:

= (3, 000 ÷ 500) x 1

= 6 x 1

= 6

ANSWER = 6 Hours

Angle of elevation=20°Perpendicular=P=12ftBase=B=?

We know

[tex]\boxed{\sf tan20=\dfrac{P}{B}}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto 0.9=\dfrac{12}{B}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto B=\dfrac{12}{0.9}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto B=13.3ft[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto B=13ft(Approx)[/tex]

Hence the man is 13ft far from the house
