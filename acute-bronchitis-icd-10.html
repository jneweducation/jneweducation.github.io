23. Give the definition of the following: 1. Acute Respiratory Infections 2. Pneumonia 3. Bronchitis 4. Tuberculosis 5. Influenza 6. Dengue 7. Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) 8. HIV and AIDS​


Answer:

1. Acute Respiratory Infections: A type of respiratory infection that affects the upper or lower respiratory tract, such as the common cold, influenza, or bronchitis.

2. Pneumonia: An inflammatory condition of the lung affecting primarily the tiny air sacs known as alveoli, causing them to fill with fluid or pus, making it difficult to breathe.

3. Bronchitis: An inflammation of the bronchial tubes, which carry air to and from the lungs, causing coughing, difficulty breathing, chest discomfort, and other flu-like symptoms.

4. Tuberculosis: A potentially serious bacterial infection that mainly affects the lungs, but can also affect other parts of the body, causing cough, fever, night sweats, and weight loss.

5. Influenza: A highly contagious viral infection that affects the respiratory system, causing high fever, body aches, sore throat, cough, and other symptoms.

6. Dengue: A viral infection spread by mosquitoes that can cause high fever, severe joint and muscle pain, headache, and in severe cases, bleeding and shock.

7. Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs): Infections caused by bacteria, viruses, or parasites that are spread through sexual contact. Examples include gonorrhea, chlamydia, genital herpes, and human papillomavirus (HPV).

8. HIV and AIDS: HIV (human immunodeficiency virus) is a virus that attacks the immune system, leaving the body vulnerable to infections and other diseases. AIDS (acquired immunodeficiency syndrome) is the most severe stage of HIV infection, marked by the occurrence of life-threatening opportunistic infections and cancers.

29. A strong link between poor sanitation and acute respiratory infections such as pneumonia and bronchitis.A.)Respiratory DiseasesB.)Skin DiseasesC.)Gastrointestinal DiseasesD.)Neurological Impairment​


Answer : A. Respiratory Diseases

Explanation:

Pneumonia and Bronchitis are both diseases affecting the respiratory system. Pneumonia is a complication due to a bacterial or viral infection (specifically caused by the streptococcus pneumoniae) that affects the lungs and other respiratory organ. Bronchitis on the other hand, is a complication when there's an inflammation on the bronchial lining.

Poor environmental sanitation is the common risk factor of the respiratory diseases.
