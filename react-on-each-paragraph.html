13. React and write a two-paragraph journal regarding the question:"Why do some people fail to fulfill their dreams and aspirations?"​


There are various reasons why some people fail to fulfill their dreams and aspirations. One of the most common reasons is the lack of motivation or commitment to achieving their goals. Without a strong sense of determination, people may give up on their dreams and aspirations when faced with challenges or setbacks. Another factor is fear, which can prevent individuals from pursuing their dreams. Fear of failure, fear of success, and fear of the unknown are some of the common fears that hinder people from taking action towards their goals.

Moreover, some people fail to fulfill their dreams and aspirations because they lack the necessary resources or support. Financial limitations, lack of access to education, and lack of opportunities are some of the barriers that prevent individuals from achieving their dreams. Additionally, social pressure and expectations from family or society can also play a role. Some people may give up their dreams to meet the expectations of their parents, friends, or society, even if it goes against their own desires and passions. Ultimately, fulfilling one's dreams and aspirations requires a combination of determination, support, and resources, as well as the ability to overcome obstacles and challenges along the way.
