Step-by-step explanation:

Overall attack rate is the total number of new cases divided by the total population.

To calculate the rate you are comparing one data point (A) to another data point (B), your formula would be A/B. This means you are dividing information A by information B. For example, if A is five and B is 10, your ratio will be 5/10.
