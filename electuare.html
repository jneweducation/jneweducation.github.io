27. 1. It is the type of electricity that flows or in motion. A current electricity B. static electricity C. solar electricity D. wind electricity


Answer:

A. Current Electricity

Explanation:

Electric current is the flow of electric charge in a circuit. It is the rate at which electric charge flows through a conductor. The unit of electric current is the ampere (A), which is defined as the flow of one coulomb of electric charge per second.
