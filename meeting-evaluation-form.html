Intrapersonal Communication: This communication occurs when we speak to ourselves through our thoughts. (Examples: talking over the phone, shouting with your friend on the other side of the fence)Public Communication: This communication occurs during a speech or a talk for a group of people. (Examples: graduation speech, spoken poetry)Mass Communication: This communication occurs when we use mass media to communicate with a very large group of people. (Examples: radio, television)
