24. Cory writes the polynomial x7 + 3x5 + 3x + 1. Melissa writes the polynomial x7 + 5x + 10. Is there a difference between the degree of the sum and the degree of the difference of the polynomials?


Answer:

There is a difference in the degree when adding or subtracting the two polynomials.

Further Explanation

There are rules that need to be followed when adding or subtracting two different polynomials. There are terms that would be retained or remain the same. There are terms that can be combined and there are terms that don't need to be combined.

Characteristics of a Polynomial

1. It has a leading term.

2. It has a degree (the highest exponent).

3. It has a constant term.

4. It contains a variable and a numerical coefficient.

Cory's Polynomial:         [tex]x^7+3x^5+3x+1[/tex]

Melissa's Polynomial:    [tex]x^7+5x+10[/tex]

Cory's polynomial has a degree of 7. This is the same as Melissa's polynomials.

Let's add and subtract the two polynomials and see the results. Remember, that we can't combine terms with different exponents, we can only just copy them to be included in the results. We can combine the terms with the same exponent by adding or subtracting the numerical coefficients.

Solution:

Adding the two polynomials.

                           [tex]\begin{aligned}\array{cccccccc}&x^7&+&3x^5&+&3x&+&1\\+&x^7&&&+&5x&+&10\:\\\\\text{Answer}&2x^7&&&+&8x&+&11\end{aligned}[/tex]

                                               or

                                           [tex]\bold{2x^7+8x+11}[/tex]

Subtracting the two polynomials.

                                [tex]\begin{aligned}\array{cccccccc}&x^7&+&3x^5&+&3x&+&1\\-&(x^7&&&+&5x&+&10)\:\\\\\text{Answer}&0&+&3x^5&-&2x&-&9\end{aligned}[/tex]

                                                or

                                          [tex]\bold{3x^5-2x-9}[/tex]

As we have explained above that the degree of a polynomial is the largest exponent. When we add the two polynomials, we get a degree of 7. When we subtract the two polynomials, we get a degree of 5. Thus, the degree of the results is not the same.
