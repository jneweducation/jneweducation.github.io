18. 9. How many committees can be formed by choosing 4 men from an organization of a membership of15 men?​


Answer

There are 15 ways to choose the first member, 14 ways to choose the second member, 13 for the third member, and 12 for the fourth member.

However, choosing A, then B, then C, then D is the same as choosing D, C, A, B, which is the same as choosing B, C, A, D, etc.

Therefore, we have counted each possible committee 24 times, since there are 4! = 24 ways of rearranging any four committee members.

To account for this, we will divide by 24 so we only count each possible committee once.

15⋅14⋅13⋅1224  

=1365  possible committees

22. lea3 What is the importance of private security in organizations?


Private security is important in an organization because it helps to protect the assets and people of the organization. This includes both physical assets like buildings and equipment, as well as intellectual property and sensitive data.

Private security can also help to deter and respond to criminal activity, as well as provide employees and customers with a sense of safety and security. Furthermore, private security can assist in ensuring compliance with laws, regulations, and industry standards.

Overall, private security is an important part of risk management for businesses of all sizes and types.

Answer:

Private security is important in organizations because it helps to protect the organization, its assets, and its employees from potential threats such as theft, vandalism, and violence. Private security personnel can also help to monitor and control access to the organization's facilities, ensure compliance with safety and security policies, and provide a rapid response to emergencies. Additionally, the presence of private security can help to deter criminal activity and improve the overall sense of safety and security within the organization.
