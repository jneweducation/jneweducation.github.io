Answer:

A

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

c=0.1

d=0.2

First, substitute it to the given problem. Then, get the answer.

121c^2-64d^2

121(0.1)^2 - 64(0.2)^2

=-1.35

Second, try substitute the assumed value of c and d on the given choices. If the calculated answer is the same with the given, then it's the answer.

So in this case, i'll be substituting the assumed values of c and d on choice letter A.

[11c-8d][11c+8d]

[11(0.1)-8(0.2)][11(0.1)+8(0.2)]

=-1.35

Therefore, A is the answer.
