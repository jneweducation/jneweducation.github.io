ACCESSIBILITY AND EFFECTIVENESS ARE IMPORTANT IN UNDERSTANDING THE CONTENT OF MATERIALS.

Because, it will be in vain if students do not have access to the media that carries the teaching materials.

Likewise with the effectiveness of learning. Because it involves five indicators of effective learning, namely:

Preparing Lesson PlanAbility in TeachingStudent AbilityStudent Behavior ChangeThere is Teaching Progress

EXPLANATION:

1. Accessibility on particular materials

No matter how important the teaching materials to be delivered and no matter how good the delivery techniques are, it will be in vain if students do not have access to the media that carries the teaching materials. This picture shows that the access or availability and ease of using learning media for students greatly affects the learning process. In connection with this, every educational institution needs information about the accessibility of students to learning media that are in the student's environment.

Education providers in the Philippines in general have difficulty knowing which learning media can be used. This is due to the uneven social, economic and infrastructure conditions in the country. This condition hinders education providers to optimize academic services, especially in the learning process. In distance education, it is necessary to pay attention to several factors, including access to media. The definition of access to media here is the availability and ease of obtaining or using media. Access to this media must be seen from two sides, both from the side of the education provider institution and from the side of the students/prospective students of the institution.

2. Effectiveness on particular materials

Prepare Lesson Plan

In order for learning to run smoothly and effectively, of course there must be a mature plan. In this plan the teacher determines the duration of the learning time, the material to be delivered, the learning method, the learning objectives, and the way of assessment or assessment.

Ability in Teaching

Indicators of teacher effectiveness in teaching can also be seen during the learning process. There are several things that need to be considered, such as how to present subject matter to students. If students easily accept and understand the subject matter explained by the teacher, it can be said that the teacher is successful in teaching.

Student Ability

The success of students is also the success of the teacher, because students are guided and given knowledge by the teacher. Students who succeed in achieving well-defined learning indicators, meaning that they understand  subject matter that has been given by the teacher. The way the teacher delivers the material, the learning media, and the teaching methods used affect students' understanding.

Changes in Student Behavior

The success of the teacher is not only seen from the ability or knowledge of students, but their behavior. Good student behavior and character shows that the teacher has succeeded in instilling good values ​​in students. Students who are initially naughty become more disciplined, responsible, respecting their parents, and others. Changes in student behavior are certainly influenced by how the teacher teaches it.

There is Teaching Progress

Teachers who want to develop better will certainly evaluate the results of their work on a regular basis. This evaluation is not an assessment of student learning outcomes or student work after the exam, but an evaluation of the extent to which learning activities are effective.
