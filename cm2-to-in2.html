QUESTION

The diameter of a circle is 15 inches. What is the area of the circle?

CHOICES

A. 94.2 in2

B. 176.625 in2

C. 47.1 in2

D. 706.5 in2

Note: Since, the diameter of the circle is given to get its radius, just divide the diameter by 2. So, 15 divided by 2 would be 7 . 5. Therefore r = 7. 5 inches.

SOLUTION

[tex]A = \pi {r}^{2} \\ A = (3.14) {(7.5 \: in)}^{2} \\ A = (3.14)(56.25 \: {in}^{2} ) \\ A = 176.625 \: {in}^{2} [/tex]

FINAL ANSWER

Area of Circle is given by the formula:

[tex]\large{A = \pi r^2}[/tex]

where

A = Area

π = 3.14

r = radius*
