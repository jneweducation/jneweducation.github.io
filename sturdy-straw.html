29. there are x pink straws 10 yellow straws and x + 2 green straws in a bag. a straw is selected at random and the probability of selecting a green straw is 2/5 find the value of x


Answer:

x= 14

Solution:

[tex]total = x + 10 + x + 2[/tex]

[tex] = 2x + 12[/tex]

[tex]p(green) = \frac{x + 2}{2x + 12} = \frac{2}{5} [/tex]

[tex]5(x + 2) = 2(2x + 12)[/tex]

[tex]5x + 10 = 4x + 24[/tex]

[tex]x = 24 - 10[/tex]

[tex]x = 14[/tex]
