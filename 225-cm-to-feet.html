13. If the lateral area of a right circular cylinder is 90 cm^2 and the volume is 225 cm cube, find its radius


Answer:

The measurement of the radius is 5 centimeters.

Further Explanation

The volume of the cylinder is defined by the equation, [tex]V=\pi{r^2}h[/tex]. The lateral area of the cylinder is derived from the area of the rectangle which is [tex]A= l\times{h}[/tex].

The rectangular flat surface, when it's formed into a cylinder the height stays the same however the length becomes the circumference of the bases of the cylinder, which is equal to [tex]2\pi{r}[/tex]. So the area of the lateral surface of the [tex]A=2\pi{r}h[/tex].

Models That Can Be Used In The Problem

1. Area of the lateral surface of the cylinder

2. Volume of the cylinder

To understand the explanation above, refer to the illustration below.

Substitute the value of [tex]V=225[/tex] into the volume formula to form equation 1.

                                        [tex]225=\pi{r^2}h\rightarrow{eq1}[/tex]

Substitute the value of [tex]A= 90[/tex] into the lateral surface area to form equation 2.

                                        [tex]90=2\pi{r}h\rightarrow{eq2}[/tex]

Methods in Solving Systems of Equation

1. Substitution Method

2. Elimination Method

3. Graphical Method

Let's solve the situation following the substitution method.

Steps in Doing Substitution

1. Rewrite equation 2 as a function of [tex]h[/tex] with respect to [tex]r.[/tex]

2. Substitute the value of [tex]h[/tex] into equation 1.

3. Solve for [tex]r[/tex] applying the appropriate mathematical laws

                                               [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{90}{2\pi{r}}&=\frac{2\pi{r}h}{2\pi{r}}\\\frac{90}{2\pi{r}}&=h\\\frac{45}{\pi{r}}&=h\end{aligned}[/tex]

                                              [tex]\begin{aligned}225&=\pi{r^2}\left(\frac{45}{\pi{r}}\right)\\225&=45r\\\frac{225}{45}&=\frac{45r}{45}\\5&=r\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that according to the solution process above, the length of the radius is 5 centimeters.

30. Find the dimension of the square painting, if it is surrounded by 1.5 cm frame. The total area of the painting plus frame is 225 sq cm.​


Answer:

The side of the square painting is 47 cm.

The dimensions of the square painting is 47 cm × 47 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Length of a side of a painting: xWidth of frame around the painting: 1.5 cm.Length of the side of the painting including frame: x + 2(1.5) or x+3Area of painting including the frame: 2,500 cm²

Area of square formula:

Area of square = (side)²

Application:

Area of painting with frame = [x + 2(1.5)]²

2,500 = (x + 3)²

Find x, the length of a side of the painting (inner square on the illustration):

(x + 3)² = 2,500

√(x+3)² = √2,500

x + 3 = 50

x = 50 -3

x = 47

The side of the square painting is 47 cm.

Distance is absolute value. So we choose only the positive value of the root of √(2,500).

The dimensions of the square

Check:

Area of painting = (side)²

Area of painting = (47 cm)²

Area of painting = 2,209 cm²

Area of painting with frame = (side + width of frame surrounding the painting)²

2,500 cm² = (47 cm + 1.5 cm + 1.5 cm)²

2,500 cm² = (47 cm + 3 cm)²

2,500 cm² = (50 cm)²

2,500 cm² = 1,500 cm² true.
