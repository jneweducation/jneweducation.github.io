17. . A __________ ion or __________ . a neutral nonpolar substance. This results to a distortion of the substance and leads to the development of positive and negative poles. Kind of attraction: _______________​


IONS

Nonpolar refers to a substance with molecules that do not have opposite negative and positive poles. 

What are ions? 

- When an atom gains an electron from other atoms or loses an electron, they will become ions. A negatively charged ion (acquired electrons) is called an anion, and a positively charged ion (stripped-off electrons) is called a cation. 

The kind of attraction created when two opposite charges interact, like an anion (negative) and a cation (positive), is called an ionic bond or ionic bonding. Positive attracts negative, and both positive and negative pairs repel. This creates an ionic bond.

What is an atom?

The atom is the tiniest particle of a particular element that is able to exist on its own or in combination with other elements.

Parts of an atom

1. Neutron - The uncharged or neutral elementary particle found inside the nucleus of an atom.

2. Electron - Negatively-charged particle that moves and vibrates around the nucleus of an atom.

3. Proton - The positively-charged particle found inside the nucleus along with the neutron.

27. __________ is the ability of a Solid material to return to its original shape after being distorted.


Answer:

Elasticity, ability of a deformed material body to return to its original shape and size when the forces causing the deformation are removed. A body with this ability is said to behave (or respond) elastically.

Answer:

Explanation:

Solid objects will deform when adequate loads are applied to them; if the material is elastic, the object will return to its initial shape and size after removal. ... This is known as perfect elasticity, in which a given object will return to its original shape no matter how strongly it is deformed.
