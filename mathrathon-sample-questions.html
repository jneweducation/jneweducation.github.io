19. sample questions for harmonic means​


Answer:

Example: What is the harmonic mean of 1, 2 and 4?

Step-by-step explanation:

The reciprocals of 1, 2 and 4 are:

11 = 1, 12 = 0.5, 14 = 0.25

Now add them up:

1 + 0.5 + 0.25 = 1.75

Divide by how many:

Average = 1.753

The reciprocal of that average is our answer:

Harmonic Mean = 31.75 = 1.714 (to 3 places)

22. Question: How will you describe a sample? Population?TERMSample:population:​


Answer:

A population is the entire group that you want to draw conclusions about. A sample is the specific group that you will collect data from. The size of the sample is always less than the total size of the population. In research, a population doesn't always refer to people.
