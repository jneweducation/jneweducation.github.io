23. how does culture affect people's relationship?​

Culture can have a large effect on the way people interact and build relationships with one another. Culture influences the way people communicate, how they show respect and affection, and how they resolve conflict. It also influences the way people view family, gender roles, and expectations for behavior. All of these factors can significantly impact relationships. For example, people from cultures that emphasize collectivist values may be more likely to prioritize group needs over individual needs, while those from more individualistic cultures may be more likely to prioritize their own needs.
