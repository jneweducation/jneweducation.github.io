13. A motorcycletravels 80 meters in 10 seconds. How fast does a motorcycle move?


QUESTION

A motorcycle travels 80 meters in 10 seconds. How fast does a motorcycle move?

ANSWER

36000 Km/h or 36000 kilometers per hour.

EXPLANATION

What is the speed of a car that travels 80 meters in 8 seconds?

Speed- Average distance traveled per unit time

Speed = distance/time

Speed of car = 80/8 = 10m s^-1

Since you know that the car is traveling 80 km in 8 seconds, you can divide the two numbers be 8 to get the distance the car would travel in 1 second

80/8=10 (Distance)

8/8=1 (Time in seconds)

Next you would multiply the number by 60 to get how far in a minute can the car travel.

10*60=600 (Distance)

1*60=60 (Time in seconds)

Lastly you multiply the numbers again by 60 to get how far in a hour it would travel.

600*60=36000 (distance)

60*60=3600 (Time in seconds)

In conclusion, the car is traveling 36000 Km/h
