Answer:

h = 0.6366 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

the volume of a right circular cone is 200 .its radius is 10 cm.what is the height of the right circular cylinder having the same volume and base as the cone

Given:

Volume cone (V1) = 200 cm³

radius (r)= 10 cm

Volume cylinder (V2)

Required: height of cylinder

V2 = V1

V2 = π × r² × h

200 cm³ = π × (10 cm)² × h

h = 0.6366 cm
