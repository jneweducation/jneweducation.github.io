10. tell something about metamorphism​


Answer:

Metamorphism is the change of minerals or geologic texture in pre-existing rocks, without the protolith melting into liquid magma. The change occurs primarily due to heat, pressure, and the introduction of chemically active fluids.

15. Which is NOT true about metamorphism​


Pressure is the main factor of contact metamorphism.

Pressure is not the primary variable of contact metamorphism; it is heat. However, pressure and temperature are variables for the change in rock depth. On the other hand, pressure and heat are variable to cause the change of grain size. They have a positive correlation with each other, having one increase, increases the other.

27. explain the concept of metamorphic grading in the transformation of racks​


Answer:

Metamorphic grade refers to the general temperature and pressure conditions that prevailed during metamorphism. As the pressure and temperature increase, rocks undergo metamorphism at higher metamorphic grade.
