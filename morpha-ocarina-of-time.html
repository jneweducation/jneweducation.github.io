Musical instruments from Latin America are mostly concentrated on wind and percussion. Ancient Latin America is where the Aztec, Mayan, Incan, and Andean civilizations lived and thrived. Musical instruments from Latin America were mainly for religious purposes only having trained players play them in reverence and respect.  

Here are some musical instruments from Latin America.
