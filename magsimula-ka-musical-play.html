1. Florante at Laura - I know that this is a famous Filipino literary classic written by Francisco Balagtas. The musical adaptation of the story has been staged numerous times and features popular songs such as "Handog" and "Awit ni Florante."

4. El Filibusterismo - I am aware that this is a sequel to another famous Filipino literary classic, Noli Me Tangere. It was also written by Jose Rizal and tells the story of a young man named Simoun, who seeks revenge against the Spanish authorities in the Philippines. The musical adaptation of the story features songs such as "Kundiman ng Bayan Ko" and "Pasyon ng Bayan."

The show features some of his famous works such as "Forever" and "Half an Hour in a Convent," as well as original music and choreography. It aims to inspire and educate audiences about the rich cultural heritage of the Philippines.
