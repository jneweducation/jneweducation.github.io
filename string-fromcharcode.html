1 point

A. 3 strings

[tex]1 \frac{4}{5} \div \frac{3}{10} = n \\ \\ 5 \times 1 + 4 = 9 \\ \frac{9}{5} \div \frac{3}{10} \\ \\ 5 \times 13 = 15 \\ 10 \times 9 = 90 \\ \\ \frac{90}{15} = 6 \\ \\ \\ = 6[/tex]
