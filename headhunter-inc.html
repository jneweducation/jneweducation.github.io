The Bendayan, also known as Bendian, is a dance that was adopted from the Benguet Mountain Province custom that honors hunters. Despite being a variation or version of the original, it is nevertheless a part of every celebration in Benguet and retains its importance. In addition, the circles provide a clear message.

There are several reasons to execut3 a bedian. Some of these include celebrating a rich harvest, curing a protracted disease, and alleviating natural disasters like starvation and drought. The l4rgest Bendian is used to commemorate both a military triumph and a fruitful headhunt. The entire town celebrates the arrival of successful head takers, or "ul;ul," with tremendous revelry.

The Benguet, Ibaloy, and Kankanay people celebrate the Bendian celebration, which is always significant and remarkable. The ul;ul leader is circled and danced around by the people while they do various arm motions. The loudest oway or battle scream signals the end of the bedian rite, which l4sts into the early hours of the morning. Bendayan is a circle dance, popularly called Bendian, of Ibaloi.

About dance art

Dance is a rhythmic movement, performed in a certain place and time to express a feeling and convey a message from a person or group. Art is a form of human self-expression, which is often used as a means of entertainment and performance.

In general, dance is a branch of art that expresses beauty, expression, to a certain meaning through the medium of body movements that are arranged and demonstrated in such a way as to provide a pleasant appearance and experience or grow a new horizon for the audience. Dance can be performed singly, in pairs, in groups or colossal.

The function of dance art

The art of dance which is known by many people has several functions, including:

1. Performing Arts

The art of dance is very functioning as a show of art performances, especially regional arts. With dance performances, people know the beauty of every dance movement. What's more, well-conceived dance moves will increase the appeal of many people, so that the audience will be touched when they see it. This dance performance can increase tourism in the area.

2. Traditional Ceremonial Facilities

The function of the next dance art is a means of traditional ceremonies. In Indonesia, many dances have been performed during traditional ceremonies. Not only that, dance is sometimes performed in certain religious rituals. The art of dance which is performed during traditional ceremonies or religious rituals usually aims to beg for harvests to be smooth, beg for rain, and so on.

3. Entertainment

The audience of a dance performance certainly wants to get the meaning of the dance while at the same time making themselves entertained. Therefore, the art of dance functions as a means of entertainment, whether by dance lovers or the general public. The more interesting a dance performance is, the more entertained the audience will be.

4. Association

The final function of dance is as an association between individuals with one another. In other words, the art of dance can improve social relations, be it with fellow dancers or people who help success in a dance performance.
