Sets

Collections of well defined and distinct objects. It is considered as an object in its own right. One of the most fundamental concepts in Mathematics. Gathering together into a whole of definite, distinct objects of our perception. Sets can be anything: numbers, people, letters of the alphabet, other sets, and so on.
