Answer:

Hold the microscope with one hand around the arm of the device, and the other hand under the base. This is the most secure way to hold and walk with the microscope. Avoid touching the lenses of the microscope. The oil and dirt on your fingers can scratch the glass.

Explanation:

The proper way to carry a microscope is to grasp the arm of the microscope with your dominant hand, lift the microscope up slowly, and use your other hand to firmly hold the base to stabilize the microscope as you transport it from one place to another.

The three basic, structural components of a compound microscope are the head, base and arm. Arm connects to the base and supports the microscope head. It is also used to carry the microscope.

12. how many different ways can be six grab with motorcycles be parked if there are 8 available parking space​


Answer:

20,160 ways.

Step-by-step explanation:

No. of slots = 8

No. of cars = 6

6 cars in 8 slots can be arranged in 8C6 ways.

8C6 = 28.

Now, these 6 cars can be arranged in 6! ways.

Therefore the answer you're looking for is

28 x 6! = 20,160 ways.
