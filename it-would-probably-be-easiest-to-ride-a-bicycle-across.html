5. Which surface would ve easiest for a bicycle to move?.


Answer:

In orderfor a bicycle to move easily or smoothly it should be in a flat surface

Answer:

Concrete road or flat surface road(smooth road)​

Explanation:

The reason why it is easier to ride a bike on a concrete road is because of friction. Friction is the term used to refer to the force that resists the relative motion of solid surfaces, fluid layers, and other material elements that are sliding against each other.

In the case of a concrete road and the rubber tire of a bike, it produces more friction, making it better for biking. On the other hand, riding a bike on a sandy surface, a grassy surface, or a muddy road will make it more difficult because it has less friction.
