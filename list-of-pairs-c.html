Answer:

To find the GCF of a set of numbers, list all the factors of each number. The greatest factor appearing on every list is the GCF. For example, to find the GCF of 6 and 15, first list all the factors of each number. Because 3 is the greatest factor that appears on both lists, 3 is the GCF of 6 and 1
