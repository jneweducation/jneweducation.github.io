17. What appropriate scale chart from paper chart folio, Electronic Chart System (ECS) or Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS)​


The most appropriate scale chart for a paper chart folio, Electronic Chart System (ECS), or Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS) would depend on the specific application and needs of the vessel. Generally, a chart scale of 1:50,000 to 1:150,000 is recommended for coastal navigation, while a chart scale of 1:500,000 to 1:3,000,000 is suitable for offshore navigation. Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS) are capable of displaying charts of almost any scale.

Answer:

5pm

Step-by-step explanation:

Time when Two alarm ring simultaneously = 4 AM

First alarm rings every 15 minutes.

Second alarm rings every 20 minutes.

============================

Find LCM of the 15 and 20

15 = 3 x 5

20 = 2 x 2 x 5

LCM = 2 x 2 x 3 x 5 = 60

The two alarms will ring simultaneously after every 60 minutes or 1 hour.

4am + 1hour = 5am

30. Fire Science: A fire department received 24 false alarms out of a total of 200 alarms received. What percent of the alarms received were false alarms?


Answer:

To calculate the percentage of false alarms received, we need to divide the number of false alarms by the total number of alarms and multiply by 100 to get the percentage.

percentage of false alarms = (number of false alarms / total number of alarms) * 100%

In this case, the number of false alarms is 24, and the total number of alarms received is 200. Therefore, the percentage of false alarms is:

percentage of false alarms = (24 / 200) * 100% = 12%

So, 12% of the alarms received were false alarms.
