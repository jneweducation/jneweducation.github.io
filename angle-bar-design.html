are versatile tools that serve to temporarily hold work securely in place. They are used for many applications including carpentry, woodworking, furniture making, welding, construction and metal working.

7. B. Calipers

They are usually of two types- inside and outside calliper. They are used to measure internal and external size (e.g. diameter) of an object.

8. ?9. A. Dovetail saw

a small backsaw used to cut dovetails. These saws will usually have a higher number of teeth per inch (around 15 - 20 T.P.I.) with teeth sharpened in a rip tooth pattern and minimal set to leave a narrow kerf. This fine rip tooth pattern also works well in cross cutting operations.

10. B. Pull Push Rule

This familiar device is spring-loaded so that the tape retracts into its metal or plastic case when not in use. It is also known as a flexible rule.
