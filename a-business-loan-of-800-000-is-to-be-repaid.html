30. A business loan of Php 800,000.00 is to be repaid in full after 2 years. What is the amount to be paid if the effective interest rate is 8% compounded annually?


Problem Solving:

Asked: The amount to be paid in full after 2 years.

Given: ₱800, 000. 00 - amount loaned

           2 years - duration of loan

           8 % - interest rate

Operation/s: multiplication, addition

Number Sentence: F = P(1 + i)ⁿ

Answer: ₱933, 120. 00

Solutions:

F = P(1 + i)ⁿ

F = ₱800, 000. 00 (1 + 8%)²

F = ₱800, 000. 00 (1 + .08)²

F = ₱800, 000. 00 (1.08)²

F = ₱800, 000. 00 (1.1664)

F = ₱933, 120. 00

Therefore, the amount to be paid in full after 2 years is ₱933, 120. 00
