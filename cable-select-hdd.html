4. give the advantage and disadvantage of FLASH MEMORY, HDD​


Answer:

Advantages of Flash Memory

1. Speed

Flash memory posses high transferring speeds. Compared to a traditional hard disk drive, flash memory does read/write function at a fast rate. However the factors latency and IOPs are considered, Solid State Drives still tops in terms of performance.

2. Durability

Flash memory does not contain any moving parts. It solely depends on electrochemical processes. Having no any moving parts means that it is more durable as well.

3. Form Factor

Most of the flash memories comes in different forms. More or same capacity can be packed in smaller units, making it require less space to accommodate data storage.

Disadvantages of Flash Memory

1. Cost

Based on the cost per gigabyte basis, flash memories are always expensive than traditional hard disk drives drives. This is because hard disk drives are less expensive to manufacture and has been for very long time. Therefore, usually business of small budgets prefer HDDs over other forms of flash memories.

2. Effectiveness

Most NAND flash drives use a process called Program/Erase to storing data. Unless the number of new writes are reduced, this process eventually wears and tears down the flash drives. As a result flash memories does not have the capability to hold up heavy write loads.

3. Lifetime

Al though flash memories are more durable than a traditional hard disk drive, it does not posses infinite lifetime. It eventually degrades upon applying high voltages across transistors. However most flash memories won't wear down until completing at least 10000 rewrites.
