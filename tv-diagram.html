9. B. Sketch the diagram showing TV intersecting PQ at point W,and prove that TW = WV.​


Answer:

See the attached diagram.

Step-by-step explanation:

Proof:

W is the midpoint of TV.

PQ is a bisector of TV at midpoint W.

PQ divides TV at W into equal segments TW and WV.

Therefore:

TW = WV

(The single red line each on TW and TV is a marker that  TW and WV are congruent.)
