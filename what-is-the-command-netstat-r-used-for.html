1. What command will you use if you want to change the font face of the text in your document from Calibri to Arial?

- CTRL + Shift + F

2. I want to highlight a certain word using a line just below the word in my document, what command will I use?

- If you want to highlight one word at a time, press Ctrl while holding down Shift , and then press the Left arrow or Right arrow . If you want to highlight a whole line of text, move your cursor to the start of the line, hold the Shift key, and then press the Down arrow.

3. Josh wants to draw a line through the middle of the selected text in his document, what command will he use?

- Josh will use the StrikeThrough command.

4. What command will Maria use to increase the size of the selected text?

- Grow Font

5. What command will Mark use to put his copied contents to the clipboard?

- If you're not already there, click Home, then click the launcher in the lower-right corner of the Clipboard group. Select the text or graphics you want to copy, and press Ctrl+C. Each selection appears in the Clipboard, with the latest at the top.

6. I want to align text to both the right and left margins of my document, what command will I use?

- Select the text that you want to align and On the Home tab, in the Paragraph group, click Align Left or Align Right .

7. I want to customize the borders of the selected text in my document, what command will I use?

- Select the paragraph text you want to format. Click the Home tab. Click the Borders and Shading button arrow, and then click to select the border commands to add or remove a border.

8. How many groups are there in Home tab?

- Home tab has five groups: Clipboard, Font, Paragraph (circled below), Styles, and Editing. A dialog box is opened when you click on the arrow in the bottom corner of a group. The dialog box gives more options and commands to format documents in one given area.

9. The format painter command belongs to what group?

- Home tab under clipboard group.

10. The text effects command belongs to what group?​

- The Text Effects button is in the Font group of the ribbon.
