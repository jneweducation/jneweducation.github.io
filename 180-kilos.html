[tex] \color{green}\rm \:5.25 \times 25[/tex]

[tex] \color{green} \rm \: = \: 131.25 \: pesos[/tex]

Multiply the cost of grapes (180) and the kilo-gram bought by the costumer (3.75).

[tex] \color{green} \rm \: 180 \times 3.75[/tex]

[tex] \color{green} \rm \: = \: 675 \: pesos[/tex]

Then, add all the cost of the fruits that costumer buy.

[tex] \color{green} \rm \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 131.25 \\ \color{green} \rm \: + \: 675[/tex]

[tex] \color{green} \rm \: = \: 810.25 \: pesos[/tex]

Answer :

The costumer will pay 810.25 pesos in all.
