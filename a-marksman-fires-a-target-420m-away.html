5. what is the perimeter of a rectangle L=420m W=-110m with solution


The formula in finding the formula of a rectangle is 2(length) + 2(width).

P = 2l + 2w
   = 2(420 m) + 2(-110)
   = 840 m - 220 m
   = 620 m

The perimeter of the rectangle is 620 m.

19. 3.) the length of a regular rectangular basketball court is 13 m longer than its width and its area is 420m².​


Answer:

15m, 28m

Step-by-step explanation:

l x w = A

x = w

x  + 13 = l

(x + 13)x = 420

x^2 + 13x = 420

x^2 + 13x - 420 = 0

(x + 28) ( x - 15 ) = 0

x = 15m = width

length = x + 13 = 28m
