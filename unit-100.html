5. Write the unit rate for 100 games in 5 years.


The unit rate for 100 games in 5 years is 20 games per year. To find the unit rate, we need to divide the total number of games by the number of years. In this case, the total number of games is 100 and the number of years is 5, so the unit rate is 100 / 5 = 20 games per year.
